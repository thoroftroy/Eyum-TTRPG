# Newton — Generic/Force

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You may swear Newton’s First Law for 1 day; you gain **Resistance to Force**, and once per turn when you deal Force damage you may push or pull the target 5 ft. Outside combat, once per day you may “steady” an object: stop a falling item, halt a swinging trap, or lock a door in place for 1 minute (GM bounds). In return, you must carry a “mass token” (a heavy iron weight) on your person all day and you cannot drop it voluntarily. If you fail, you are forcibly pushed 10 ft whenever you take damage for 12 days and you cannot reduce that forced movement.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** For 3 days, once per combat you may declare **Inertia** for 3 turns: you are immune to being pushed/pulled/knocked prone unless you choose, and the first time each turn you hit with Force you also deal **1d10 Force to HP** (extra instance). Outside combat, once per day you may open or seal a mundane door by force-locking its frame for 10 minutes. In return, each dawn you take **2d10 Force to HP** (cannot be reduced) and you must never take cover from ranged attacks (you may still move, but can’t claim cover). If you fail, you permanently lose **-1 DEX**.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** For 7 days, once per turn your Force damage ignores Resistance, and once per combat you may “vector rewrite” one creature you can see: after it moves, you may rotate that movement direction 90 degrees (same distance) once that turn (no save; GM adjudicates collisions). Outside combat, once per day you may create a stable floating platform (5-ft square) for 10 minutes. In return, you must end three combats without moving more than 10 ft total per combat (measured roughly) and you must break one promise you made for comfort. If you fail, you begin every combat **Slowed** for 30 days (Remove Curse only).

**Tier 4 (12 days):** For 12 days, once per combat you may create a 15-ft “gravity well” for 3 turns: enemies that start their turn inside take **2d10 Force to HP** and must pass **DC 18 STR** or be pulled 10 ft toward the center; ranged attacks from inside have disadvantage. Outside combat, once per day you may pin a large object in place (boulder, gate, statue arm) for 1 hour if you can touch it. In return, you must not jump, climb, or fly during the pact (no vertical advantage) and you must accept one hit per combat without using reactions to mitigate it. If you fail, you permanently lose **-10 ft movement**.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** For 30 days, once per combat you may declare **Principia** on a Force hit: it is **tripled**, and for the next 3 turns you may redirect one instance of non-True damage from an ally within 30 ft onto yourself (Reaction, once per turn). Outside combat, once per week you may create a temporary “zero-g corridor” (30 ft long, 10 minutes) allowing controlled floating traversal and bypassing pits/ruins. In return, you must permanently sacrifice 1 stat point (you choose) and complete a task that advances knowledge (recover a text, map a ruin, deliver a proof). If you fail, you are crushed by unseen weight: you die instantly.
