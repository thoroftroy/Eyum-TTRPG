Some affinities cannot be increased until reaching high enough affinity with other affinities. 

| Affinity Type | Notes                                                                                   |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| Air           | None                                                                                    |

| Cursed        | Must have at least 10 points in Radiant + Necrotic                                      |
