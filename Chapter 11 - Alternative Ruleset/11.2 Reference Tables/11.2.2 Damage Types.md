Damage types fall into groupings like magical or physical and so on, different creatures may have resistances or weaknesses to specific damage types or groupings. 
- Immunity: Take no damage from source
- Greater Resistance: Take 1/3 damage from source
- Resistance: Take half (1/2) damage from source
- Weakness: Take double (2x) damage from source
- Greater Weakness: Take 3x damage from source

Groupings
- Physical: From non-magical sources
- Magical: From magical sources
- Elemental: From elemental sources
- Mixed: Physical damage enhanced with magic
- Special: From unusual sources

So for example, if a creature has greater resistance to Elemental damage then when they take damage from fire or water they will only take 1/3 of the damage it normally would have dealt to them. But if a creature has greater weakness to slashing damage then a sword will deal 3x the damage to them.

## Combination Damage Types
Combination Damage Types are advanced damage categories formed by merging two or more elemental or magical sources into a single hybrid effect. In order to be resistant or weak to a combination damage type you must either be weak or resistant to all components or the combination type specifically. 

**Note:** If a condition causes a damage type which causes a condition or vice versa it is not recursive.
