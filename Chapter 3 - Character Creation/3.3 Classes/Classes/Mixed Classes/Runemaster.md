The Runemaster is a specialized spellcaster who uses **runes** rather than traditional spellcasting. All Runemaster abilities function through the **Xeran**, **Serenite**, and **Vindroth** rune systems. For more information please see [[6.4.1 Runes]]

This class is advanced and not recommended for beginners.
## Class Features
**Hit Dice:** 1d4  
**Vitality Dice:** 1d6  
**Mana Dice:** 1d4

**Armor Proficiency:** Light armor, Medium armor  
**Weapon Proficiency:** Simple weapons  
**Saving Throws:** Intelligence, Wisdom  
**Spellcasting Modifier:** Affinity

**Skills:** Choose two  
Arcana, History, Investigation, Insight, Sleight of Hand
## Rune Casting Rules (Class Integration)
- The Runemaster is considered the **caster** for all rune activations they inscribe.
- Rune effects use the rules defined in:
    - [[6.4.2 Xeran Runes]]
    - [[6.4.3 Serenite Runes]]
    - [[6.4.4 Vindroth Runes]]
- Any rune effect that requires an attack roll uses:  
    **1d20 + Affinity + Material Modifier**
- Any rune effect that requires a saving throw uses:  
    **DC = 10 + Affinity + relevant School Level**  
## Runic Schooling Progression
At **Runemaster level 1**, choose **one Runic Schooling**:
- Xeran
- Serenite
- Vindroth
Rules:
- You may not use runes from an unlearned schooling.
- You may not learn a new schooling until you have taken **6 levels in each schooling you already know**.
- Each schooling tracks its own **School Level**.
- After completing **all three schoolings to level 6**, features that reference “School Level” may instead reference **total Runemaster level** where explicitly allowed.
## Rune Knowledge
- You know **1 rune per Runemaster level**.
- Carbon, If and Than do not count toward this limit.
- You may reselect known runes every **2 Runemaster levels**.
## Rune Inscription
Runemasters follow all rules in **Crafting Runes**:
- Inscription Kit required
- Durability, Modifier, and Time rules apply
- Interrupted inscriptions fail
- Inscription during combat requires **one full action**
- For more information please see [[6.4.1 Runes]]
## Subclasses
_(Chosen at Runemaster level 3)_
## Augmenter
Augmenters inscribe runes onto their own bodies, using **Serenite rune mechanics**. You may only have 1 Serenite Triangle inscribed at one time.
#### Level 3 - Body Inscription
- You may inscribe **Serenite runes** onto **Skin**.
- These inscriptions follow Skin material rules.
- Only **Serenite** runes may be inscribed this way.
#### Level 6 - Physical Reinforcement
While you have at least one active Serenite rune on your body:
- Reduce incoming damage by **1d4** once per turn.
#### Level 10 - Reactive Activation
Once per round when you take damage:
- You may activate a **single Serenite rune circle** on yourself as a reaction.
#### Level 14 - Sustained Augmentation
If you maintain a full **Serenite triangle** on your body:
- Gain resistance to non-magical damage.
#### Level 18 - Dual Triangles
You may maintain **two Serenite triangles** on your body simultaneously.
## Enchanter
Enchanters focus on long-term rune inscriptions on items. Reminder, only one circle can be inscribed onto an item at a time. 
#### Level 3 - Stable Engraving
When inscribing runes onto weapons or armor:
- Increase durability by **+1**
#### Level 6 - Mixed Inscription
You may inscribe:
- One **Xeran circle** and one **Serenite triangle** onto the same item (normal limits apply).
#### Level 10 - Reinforced Channel
At the cost of a bonus action Ignore durability loss from a rune activation.
#### Level 14 - Controlled Overchannel
You may exceed a rune limit:
- +1 Xeran non-carbon rune **or** +1 Vindroth effect clause  
- However, each activation, the item loses **2 durability** instead of 1. 
#### Level 18 - Permanent Enchantment
Choose one inscribed item:
- Its runes no longer lose durability.
- The item cannot be re-inscribed.
## Trapper
Trappers specialize in **Vindroth logic** and delayed rune activation.
#### Level 3 - Rapid Paragraphs
You may inscribe Vindroth paragraphs with **1 If/Than block** as a **bonus action**.
#### Level 6 - Embedded Payload
Vindroth effects may directly call **Xeran payloads** without requiring a separate circle.
#### Level 10 - Layered Logic
Your Vindroth inscriptions may:
- Trigger **twice** before using durability.
#### Level 14 - Remote Awareness
You are aware when any of your rune inscriptions activate, regardless of distance as well as which one is activated. If a mark is placed on an object you can see which mark is placed and how many were placed. 
#### Level 18 - Networked Traps
When an object has a Mark from one of your traps you can see it and tell which one it is. You always know the location of the Marked object and what is around it in a 5 ft sphere. 
## Rune Slinger
Rune Slingers emphasize fast, active rune use. This allows you to use Xeran Runes without them being in a circle, not all runes will have effects this way. 
#### Level 3 - Runic Projectile
You may throw an inscribed rune or rune circle:
- Range: 30 ft
- Activates on impact
#### Level 6 - Mana Amplification
You may spend **10 mana** to add **+1 damage die** to a rune effect.
#### Level 10 - Chain Activation
If a rune destroys itself on activation:
- You may activate another rune as a bonus action.
#### Level 14 - Ranged Circles
You can activate any runic circles/triangles/paragraphs that you own from any distance.
### Level 18 – Mana Reclamation
Once per turn:
- Refund the mana cost of one rune activation.
