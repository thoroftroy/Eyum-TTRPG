### Spending FDL
- Each time you gain **+1 FDL**, you assign it to **one Discipline**.
- A Discipline has **Ranks 1–5**. Investing FDL increases that Discipline’s Rank.
- **At each Rank**, you choose **one Feature** from that Rank’s list.
- If you invest in a Discipline already at Rank 5, you gain a **Mastery Pick** instead: choose **one** Feature you don't know from any rank in that Discipline and learn it. 
### Feature Types
- **Passive:** always on.
- **Technique (Ap / BAp / Rp):** something you actively do. If a Technique calls for a roll, it uses the normal roll for that action (weapon attack roll, Athletics contest, etc.).
- Unless a Feature says otherwise, it can only be used **once per round**.
# Discipline: Guardian
### Rank 1 (pick 1)
- **Brace (Rp):** When you take damage, reduce it by **your Proficiency Bonus** (minimum of 0).
- **Bodyguard (Rp):** If an ally within 5 ft is hit, you may spend **1 Rp** to become the target instead.
- **Shielded Footing (Passive):** You have advantage on checks to resist being shoved or grappled.
### Rank 2 (pick 1)
- **Intercept (Rp):** When an enemy makes a melee attack against an ally within 5 ft, impose **-2** on that attack roll.
- **Hold the Line (Passive):** Opportunity Attacks you make gain **+1 Melee Accuracy**.
- **Stand Fast (Passive):** When you would be knocked prone, you may instead remain standing (once per round).
### Rank 3 (pick 1)
- **Taunt (Ap):** Choose a creature within 30 ft that can see/hear you. Until your next turn, it takes **-2 Accuracy** on attacks that do not include you as a target.
- **Fortify (BAp):** Gain damage reduction **= Proficiency Bonus + 5** (minimum of 1 damage must still be taken) until the start of your next turn.
- **Anchor (Passive):** If you do not move during your turn, you gain **+1 Accuracy** on Opportunity Attacks until your next turn.
### Rank 4 (pick 1)
- **Guard Breaker Defense (Passive):** When you Brace, also gain **+1 Accuracy** on your next weapon attack.
- **Wall (Ap):** Until your next turn, enemies moving through your melee range treat it as difficult terrain (movement costs double while inside your melee range).
- **Iron Nerves (Passive):** You cannot be forced to move by a shove effect unless you choose to.
### Rank 5 (pick 1)
- **Last Stand (Passive):** Once per short rest, when you would drop to 0 HP, instead drop to **1 HP**.
- **Punish Opening (Rp):** When a creature misses you with a melee attack, make an Opportunity Attack against it (even if it didn’t move).
- **Bulwark (Passive):** Your damage reduction effects from Guardian increase by **+2**.
# Discipline: Weapon Mastery
### Rank 1 (pick 1)
- **Weapon Focus (Passive):** Choose a weapon you’re proficient with. Gain **+1 Accuracy** with it.
- **Clean Strike (Passive):** Once per turn gain +3 damage on one attack with a weapon you are proficient in. 
- **Ready Hands (BAp):** Equip/swapping held weapons costs **0 BAp** once per round.
### Rank 2 (pick 1)
- **Follow-Through (BAp):** After you hit with a weapon attack with a weapon you are proficient in, make a second weapon attack at the cost of a BAp. 
- **Punishing Miss (Passive):** Your first weapon attack with a weapon you are proficient with each turn gains **+10 Accuracy**.
- **Control Grip (Passive):** You gain **+2** on contested Athletics checks when grappling while holding a weapon you are proficient with.
### Rank 3 (pick 1)
- **Precision Cut (Ap):** Make a weapon attack with advantage. On hit, reduce the target’s Accuracy by **-5** until its next turn.
- **Weapon Parry (Rp):** When hit by an attack from a weapon you are proficient with, reduce damage by **5 + Proficiency Bonus** (minimum of 0 damage).
- **Weapon Tempo (Passive):** When you spend 2 BAp → 1 Ap, you also gain **+3 Accuracy** on the next attack you make this turn.
### Rank 4 (pick 1)
- **Exploit Weakness (Passive):** Once per round, when you hit a creature you’ve already hit this combat, deal double damage.
- **Disarm (Ap):** Make a weapon attack. On hit, target must win a contested Athletics check vs your Athletics or drop one held item.
- **Dash and Slice (BAp):** If you move 10 ft or more you may make a weapon attack as a BAp instead of an Ap.
### Rank 5 (pick 1)
- **Masterstroke (Passive):** You gain +5 damage with weapons you have expertise in.
- **Weapon Expert (Passive):** Your Weapon Focus bonus increases to **+2 Accuracy**.
- **Relentless (Passive):** When you crit, gain **+1 BAp**.

- **Clincher (Passive):** You gain **+10** on contested Athletics checks for Grapple and resisting Grapple.
