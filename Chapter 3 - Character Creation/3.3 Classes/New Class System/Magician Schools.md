## Spending MSL
- Each time you gain **+1 MSL**, you assign it to **one School**.
- A School has **Ranks 1–5**. Investing MSL increases that Schools’s Rank.
- **At each Rank**, you choose **one Feature** from that Rank’s list.
- If you invest in a School already at Rank 5, you gain a **Mastery Pick** instead: choose **one** Feature you don't know from any rank in that School and learn it. 
# School: Elemental
### Rank 1 (pick 1)
- **Elemental Edge (Passive):** Your spells deal **+5 Damage** if they have a Fire, Earth, Water, or Air affinity.
- **Elemental Accuracy (Passive):** Your elemental spells gain **+5 Magic Accuracy**.
- **Attuned Casting (Passive):** When you cast an elemental spell, refund **5% of its Mana** (minimum of 1).
### Rank 2 (pick 1)
- **Elemental Surge (BAp):** When you cast an elemental spell, you may spend **+10 Mana** to add **+10 Damage**.
- **Elemental Pressure (Passive):** Targets hit by your elemental spells take **-5 Accuracy** until their next turn.
- **Dual Element (Passive):** When you deal damage with an Elemental spell, half of its damage is converted into the opposite element (Fire -> Water | Earth -> Fire)
### Rank 3 (pick 1)
- **Overchannel (Ap):** Cast a spell with **advantage** on the spell attack roll; costs **+15 Mana**.
- **Lingering Element (Passive):** If you cast a spell with a lingering damaging effect or condition the length of the effect is doubled.
- **Elemental Guard (Rp):** When hit, reduce damage by **10 + Relevant Affinity** if the damage type matches an element you have >3 Affinity in.
### Rank 4 (pick 1)
- **Cataclysmic Cast (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, an elemental spell gains **+20 Damage**.
- **Elemental Control (Passive):** Enemies damaged by your elemental spells have their movement reduced by **50%** until next turn.
- **Affinity Feedback (Passive):** When you cast an elemental spell, gain **+5 Magic Accuracy** on all spells next turn.
### Rank 5 (pick 1)
- **Elemental Mastery (Passive):** Elemental spells gain **+10 Damage** and **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
- **Perfect Overchannel (Passive):** Your Overchannel no longer costs extra Mana (Must have taken Overchannel).
- **Elemental Collapse (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, double the damage of an elemental spell.
# School: Vital
### Rank 1 (pick 1)
- **Vital Thread (Passive):** Healing spells restore **+10 HP/Vitality**.
- **Withering Touch (Passive):** Damage spells deal **+5 Damage** to Vitality.
- **Life Sense (Passive):** Gain **+5 Magic Accuracy** against wounded targets.
### Rank 2 (pick 1)
- **Drain (Ap):** When a spell deals damage, heal  **Vitality**.
- **Rot (Passive):** Targets hit by your spells take **-5 Damage** on their next attack.
- **Sustain (Passive):** Once per round, regain **5 Mana** when you heal a creature.
### Rank 3 (pick 1)
- **Vital Lock (Ap):** On hit, target cannot regain HP/Vitality until your next turn.
- **Rapid Mend (BAp):** Cast a healing spell as a BAp.
- **Deathward (Rp):** **Once per short rest**, prevent a creature within 30 ft from dropping below 1 HP.
### Rank 4 (pick 1)
- **Corrupt Flow (Passive):** Damage you deal prevents up to **10 + Prof Bonus points of healing** applied afterward.
- **Regen Loop (Passive):** Healing spells refund **10 Mana**.
- **Vital Shock (Ap):** On hit, deal **+20 Damage** if target is below half HP.
### Rank 5 (pick 1)
- **Vital Mastery (Passive):** Healing and damage spells gain **+20 magnitude**.
- **Perfect Drain (Passive):** Drain heals **20** instead of 10 (must have taken drain).
- **Life Sever (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, double Vitality damage dealt by a spell.

# School: Energy (Energymancy / “Energyweaver-style”)

Energy is about **direct resource manipulation** (Mana/Vitality/HP), conversion, and burst pacing. It does not replace spells—you still cast spells, but your spellcasting can **route through pools** and **steal/convert** energy.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Flow Control (Passive):** Once per round when you cast a spell, refund **10 Mana**.
    
- **Energy Edge (Passive):** Your spells gain **+5 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **Conversion (BAp):** Convert **10 HP → 15 Mana** OR **15 Mana → 10 HP** (self only).
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Siphon (Ap):** On a spell hit, restore **10 HP or 10 Vitality** (choose) to yourself.
    
- **Overcharge (BAp):** Spend **+15 Mana** to add **+20 Magic Damage** to the next spell you cast this turn.
    
- **Stability (Passive):** When you would lose Mana from an external effect, reduce that loss by **10 + Prof Bonus** (min 0).
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Invert (Ap):** **Once per round**, when you would take damage, you may pay **Mana equal to the damage** to reduce that damage by the same amount (you still take at least 1 damage).
    
- **Drain Pulse (Ap):** On a spell hit, target loses **20 Mana** (or if it has no Mana system: it instead takes **+20 Damage**).
    
- **Battery (Passive):** Your maximum Mana increases by **+50**.
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Energy Theft (Passive):** Once per round when you hit with a spell, the target takes **-10 Magic Accuracy** until its next turn and you gain **+10 Magic Accuracy** until your next turn.
    
- **Lifeburn Casting (Passive):** If you don’t have enough Mana for a spell, you may pay the remainder in **HP at 1:1** (cannot reduce yourself below 1 HP this way).
    
- **Surge Loop (Rp):** When you cast a spell, you may immediately gain **+1 BAp** (once per round).
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Energy Mastery (Passive):** Once per round, **double** the Mana refund/restore from one Energy School feature you used that round.
    
- **Perfect Conversion (Passive):** Conversion becomes **10 HP → 25 Mana** OR **25 Mana → 10 HP**.
    
- **Overdrive (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, gain **+2 Ap** this turn (cannot reduce below 1 Ap afterward; you just lose the bonus when the turn ends).

# School: Illusion (Deception / false perception)

Illusion is about **forcing misses, breaking target selection, and stealth-like combat effects** without requiring Hide to function.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Blur (Rp):** When targeted by an attack, impose **-10 Accuracy** on that attack.
    
- **Illusion Accuracy (Passive):** Illusion-tagged spells gain **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **False Opening (Passive):** Once per round when your illusion spell hits, the target takes **-5 Accuracy** until next turn.
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Decoy (BAp):** **Once per short rest**, create a decoy until your next turn; the first attack that would hit you instead misses.
    
- **Misdirect (Rp):** When you are hit, force the attacker to reroll the attack roll and take the worse result.
    
- **Veil Step (BAp):** Move **15 ft** without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Disbelief Punish (Passive):** Once per round when an enemy fails to hit you (miss), your next spell against it gains **+10 Magic Accuracy** and **+10 Damage**.
    
- **Mirror Guard (Rp):** Reduce damage from a hit by **20** (min 0).
    
- **Phantom Target (Ap):** On a hit with an illusion spell, the target cannot make Opportunity Attacks until next turn.
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Total Misdirection (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, force a creature you can see to take **-20 Accuracy** on all attacks until its next turn.
    
- **Vanishing Cast (BAp):** After you cast a spell, you may immediately attempt **Hide** (still needs cover, but you roll with advantage).
    
- **Illusory Lock (Passive):** Targets hit by your illusion spells suffer **-10 Accuracy** on their next attack.
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Illusion Mastery (Passive):** Your Blur / Misdirect / Mirror Guard can each be used **one additional time per round** (still costs the action cost each time).
    
- **Perfect Decoy (Passive):** Decoy becomes **once per round** (still only blocks one hit per use).
    
- **Erase Presence (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, until your next turn you cannot be targeted by attacks unless you deal damage (AOE can still hit you if you’re in it).

# School: Binding (Summons / contracts / containment)

Binding is **summon scaling**, **locking targets**, and **tradeoffs**.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Bound Focus (Passive):** Binding-tagged spells gain **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **Leash (Ap):** On a spell hit, reduce target movement by **50%** until next turn.
    
- **Contracted Recovery (Passive):** When you cast a binding spell, refund **10 Mana** (once per round).
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Seal (Ap):** On hit, target cannot take **Reactions** until next turn.
    
- **Tether (Ap):** On hit, target cannot move more than **10 ft** away from its current position until next turn.
    
- **Summoner’s Edge (Passive):** Your summons (if any) gain **+10 Accuracy**.
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Punish Escape (Passive):** If a target successfully escapes your effect (breaks tether/seal/etc.), it takes **+30 Damage** (once per round).
    
- **Reinforced Binding (Passive):** Your binding penalties improve by **+5** (Accuracy debuffs, movement clamps, etc.).
    
- **Shared Action (BAp):** If you control a summon, it may take one simple action as a **BAp** (attack/move/guard; GM adjudicates “simple”).
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Containment Field (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, choose a point within 60 ft; enemies in a 10 ft area have their movement reduced by **50%** until next turn.
    
- **Forced Target (Ap):** On hit, target suffers **-20 Accuracy** if it attacks anyone other than you until next turn.
    
- **Summon Battery (Passive):** If you have a summon active, you regenerate **+20 Mana** at the start of your turn.
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Binding Mastery (Passive):** Your Binding school effects last **+1 round** longer (if they are duration-based; otherwise they gain +10 penalty magnitude).
    
- **Perfect Seal (Passive):** Seal also removes **1 BAp** next turn (minimum 1).
    
- **Absolute Tether (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, a Tethered target cannot move at all until next turn.

# School: Runic (Inscribed / persistent / delayed casting)

Runic is **setup**, **stored casting**, and **battlefield preparation**.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Quick Inscription (BAp):** Place a rune (or “prepared spell mark”) as a BAp (GM defines what surfaces qualify).
    
- **Stored Power (Passive):** Once per round, when you cast a runic spell, refund **10 Mana**.
    
- **Runic Accuracy (Passive):** Runic spells gain **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Delayed Trigger (Passive):** When you cast a runic spell, you may delay its trigger until a condition occurs (GM-approved simple condition).
    
- **Rune Anchor (Ap):** Create a marked space; the first enemy entering it takes **+30 Damage** (requires your next spell to be “stored” into it).
    
- **Efficient Storage (Passive):** Stored spells cost **15 less Mana**.
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Remote Trigger (BAp):** Trigger one stored rune/spell within 60 ft.
    
- **Runic Shield (Rp):** When you take damage, reduce it by **20** (min 0) if you are within 10 ft of one of your runes.
    
- **Recast Pattern (Passive):** Once per round, if a stored spell triggers, you gain **+10 Magic Accuracy** on your next spell.
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Rune Network (Passive):** You may have **two** stored runes active at once.
    
- **Amplified Glyph (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, a stored spell triggers with **double damage**.
    
- **Locking Script (Ap):** On a runic hit, target takes **-20 Accuracy** until next turn.
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Runic Mastery (Passive):** Stored spells gain **+20 Damage** and **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **Perfect Network (Passive):** Rune Network becomes **three** stored runes.
    
- **Instant Inscription (Passive):** Quick Inscription costs **0 BAp** once per round.

# School: Ritual (Slow / prepared / scaling)

Ritual is **big swings** with time, safety, and resource efficiency.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Ritual Efficiency (Passive):** Ritual spells cost **20% less Mana** (min 1 less).
    
- **Prepared Casting (Passive):** If you did not move this turn, your next spell gains **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **Ritual Stability (Passive):** Once per round, ignore disadvantage on a spell cast.
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Slow Power (Ap):** If you spend your entire turn casting only one spell (no other Ap used), that spell gains **+20 Damage**.
    
- **Ward Circle (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, reduce damage taken by you and allies within 10 ft by **10** until next turn.
    
- **Mana Reserve (Passive):** Gain **+75 Mana**.
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Perfect Focus (Passive):** If you take the Dodge action, your next spell gains **advantage**.
    
- **Ritual Echo (Passive):** Once per round, after you cast a spell, refund **20 Mana**.
    
- **Extended Duration (Passive):** If a ritual spell has a duration, it is **doubled**.
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Major Working (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, a spell gains **double damage** OR **double non-damage magnitude** (choose on cast).
    
- **Sanctum Step (BAp):** If you didn’t move this turn, you may teleport **15 ft** as a BAp.
    
- **Ritual Lock (Rp):** When hit, reduce damage by **30** (min 0).
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Ritual Mastery (Passive):** Your first spell each combat costs **0 Mana**.
    
- **Perfect Major Working (Passive):** Major Working can be used **twice per short rest**.
    
- **Absolute Efficiency (Passive):** Ritual Efficiency becomes **40% less Mana**.

# School: Nature (Plants / beasts / terrain / weather)

Nature is **battlefield control**, **sustain**, and **anti-mobility**.

### Rank 1 (pick 1)

- **Nature Accuracy (Passive):** Nature spells gain **+10 Magic Accuracy**.
    
- **Rooting Hit (Passive):** Once per round on a spell hit, reduce target movement by **20 ft** until next turn.
    
- **Natural Recovery (Passive):** At start of your turn, regain **10 Mana**.
    

### Rank 2 (pick 1)

- **Entangle (Ap):** On hit, target takes **-10 Accuracy** on Opportunity Attacks and its movement is halved until next turn.
    
- **Barkskin Reflex (Rp):** Reduce damage by **20** (min 0).
    
- **Terrain Favor (Passive):** You ignore difficult terrain (GM-defined) and gain **+10 Accuracy** against creatures standing in it.
    

### Rank 3 (pick 1)

- **Predator’s Mark (BAp):** Mark a target; your next spell hit vs it deals **+30 Damage** (once per round).
    
- **Thorn Backlash (Rp):** When a creature hits you in melee, it takes **20 Damage**.
    
- **Wild Growth (Passive):** Your control effects (roots/slows) last **+1 round**.
    

### Rank 4 (pick 1)

- **Storm Pressure (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, enemies in 15 ft take **-20 Accuracy** until next turn.
    
- **Beast Ally (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, summon a simple ally (GM-defined statline) for 1 minute; it acts after you.
    
- **Life in Motion (Passive):** After you cast a spell, gain **+15 ft movement** until end of turn.
    

### Rank 5 (pick 1)

- **Nature Mastery (Passive):** Your Nature spells gain **+20 Damage** and your slows become **-30 ft** (or 75% reduction if halving).
    
- **Perfect Predator (Passive):** Predator’s Mark becomes **+50 Damage**.
    
- **Overgrowth (Ap):** **Once per short rest**, all enemies within 30 ft have movement reduced to **0** until next turn.
