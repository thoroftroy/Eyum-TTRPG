In Eyum, there are no classes. Your character is defined solely by where they source their power.

1. The Martial Path (Innate Strength) This is the path of consistency. A sword does not run out of mana. A fist does not fizzle because a god is angry. Martial fighters are limited by biology and physics, you will never conjure a storm or teleport across a continent—but you are reliable. Your damage is constant, your defense is static, and you do not rely on external resources.
2. The Way of the Arcane (World Magic) This is the path of peaks and valleys. Magic allows you to break the rules of physics, controlling energy, rewriting nature, or borrowing power from Deities. The ceiling for magic is infinitely higher than martial prowess, but it is volatile. It requires resources (Mana, Runes, Favor) and often carries the risk of failure or backlash.

**Progression: The Tree System**
"Level" is just a measure of total points earned. Your actual identity is defined by your **Branches** and **Tiers**. When you create a character or level up, you gain **Skill Points**. You invest these into trees following three rules:
- **The Root:** To start a new discipline (e.g., _Fighter_ or _Arcane_), you must buy the **Root Node**. This unlocks the tree.
- **The Branch:** You follow specific paths (Branches) downwards. You cannot buy a skill unless you own the skill directly above it (the Prerequisite).
- **The Tier:** Depth represents power.
    - **Tier 1:** Basic competencies.
    - **Tier 2:** Expert abilities.
    - **Tier 3+:** Masteries.

**How to Level Up**
When you gain a point, you make a tactical choice:
1. **Go Deep (Vertical):** Buy the next Tier in your current branch.
    - _Result:_ Your specific specialty gets significantly stronger.
2. **Go Wide (Horizontal):** Buy a skill in a parallel branch or a new Root.
    - _Result:_ You gain versatility and new options, but your raw power output stays the same.

**Designating "Level"**
Players do not say "I am Level 5." You introduce your character by their highest achievement in their primary branches.

> **Examples:**
> - _"I am a Tier 3 Sniper."_ 
> - _"I am a Tier 2 Pugilist, Tier 1 Nature Mage."_ 
> - _"I am a Tier 4 Warlord."_ 

### **The Martial Path (Way of Steel)**
_The path of consistency. Steel does not run out of mana. Muscle does not fade in an antimagic field. This path relies on **Action Points (AP)** and **Reaction Points (RP)** to overwhelm foes._

**Root Skill: Basic Combat Training**
- **Effect:** You gain Proficiency with Simple Weapons and Light Armor. You gain **+5 Max Vitality** and **+1 to Hit** on all weapon attacks.
- **Growth:** Each time you invest a point in this tree (any branch), gain **+2 Max Vitality**.
#### **Branch A: The Body**
- **Tier 1: Physical Conditioning** (+1 Constitution per point invested, max 5).
    - **Path A: The Mountain**
        - **Iron Skin:** While not wearing Heavy Armor, you may add your CON Modifier to your AC instead of DEX.
        - **Pain Tolerance:** Reduce all non-magical Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage taken by **Proficiency Bonus**.
        - **Living Fortress:** You provide **Three-Quarters Cover** to allies standing directly behind you. You cannot be moved against your will by creatures smaller than Huge.
            - **Mastery (Immovable Object):** +5 Max HP per point invested.
    - **Path B: The Pugilist**
        - **Martial Arts:** Your Unarmed Strike deals **1d6 + STR/DEX** damage. You may use DEX for unarmed attacks.
        - **Flurry of Blows:** When you take the Attack action, you may spend **1 BAP** to make two additional unarmed strikes.
        - **Ki Flow:** Your unarmed attacks count as Magical for overcoming resistances. On a Critical Hit, the target must pass a **CON Save (DC 8 + Prof + STR/DEX)** or be **Stunned** until the end of your next turn.
            - **Mastery (Grandmaster):** +2 Unarmed Damage per point invested.
    - **Path C: The Wrestler**
        - **Technician:** You gain **Advantage** on Athletics checks to Grapple or Shove.
        - **Ground & Pound:** You have **Advantage** on attack rolls against creatures you are Grappling.
        - **Meat Shield:** While Grappling a creature of your size or smaller, you gain **+2 AC**. If an attack misses you, you may use **1 RP** to force the attack to hit the grappled creature instead.
            - **Mastery (Python Grip):** +1 to Grapple Checks per point invested.
    - **Path D: The Berserker**
        - **Adrenaline Rush:** You may attack Recklessly. You gain **Advantage** on melee attacks this turn, but attack rolls against you have Advantage until your next turn.
        - **Undying Rage:** If you drop to 0 HP but not killed outright, make a **DC 10 CON Save**. On success, drop to 1 HP instead. DC increases by 5 for each subsequent success per Long Rest.
        - **Bloodthirst:** When you kill an enemy, you regain **Vitality** equal to your **Constitution Modifier + Proficiency**.
            - **Mastery (Fury):** +2 Melee Damage when below 50% HP per point invested.
#### **Branch B: The Blade**
- **Tier 1: Arms Training** (Gain Proficiency in Martial Weapons, Medium Armor, and Shields).
    - **Path A: The Juggernaut (Heavy Weapons)**
        - **Heavy Stance:** You can wield **Two-Handed** weapons without disadvantage. Reroll 1s on damage dice for Heavy weapons.
        - **Cleave:** When you reduce a creature to 0 HP with a melee attack, you may immediately make another melee attack against a different creature within reach (Free Action).
        - **Sunder Armor:** On a Critical Hit, you permanently reduce the target's AC by **1** (Max -5 per target).
            - **Mastery (Titan Grip):** +2 Damage with Two-Handed Weapons per point invested.
    - **Path B: The Duelist**
        - **Ambidextrous:** You may add your Ability Modifier to the damage of off-hand attacks.
        - **Parry & Riposte:** When a creature misses you with a melee attack, you may spend **1 RP** to make a single melee weapon attack against them immediately.
        - **Blade Dancer:** You can move through enemy spaces without penalty. You gain **+1 AC** while wielding a melee weapon in each hand.
            - **Mastery (Thousand Cuts):** +1 To Hit with One-Handed Weapons per point invested.
    - **Path C: The Marksman (Ranged)**
        - **Eagle Eye:** Attacking at **Long Range** does not impose Disadvantage. Ignore Half-Cover.
        - **Volley:** You may spend **2 AP** to fire an arrow at every enemy within a 10ft radius area (within weapon range). Make separate attack rolls for each.
        - **Kill Shot:** If you attack a target that has not yet taken a turn in combat, the hit is an automatic **Critical Hit**.            
            - **Mastery (Sniper):** +5ft Range to Ranged Weapons per point invested.
    - **Path D: The Tactician (Support)**
        - **Assessment:** As a **Bonus Action**, learn the current HP, AC, and Vulnerabilities of one target you can see.
        - **Commander's Strike:** You may forego one of your attacks to allow an ally who can see/hear you to make an attack immediately (using their Reaction).
        - **Disarming Strike:** When you hit a creature, you may spend **1 BAP** to force a **STR Save**. On fail, they drop one item they are holding.
            - **Mastery (Battle Master):** +1 Initiative per point invested.
#### **Branch C: The Shadow**
- **Tier 1: Light Footed** (Gain Proficiency in Stealth, Acrobatics, and Thieves' Tools. +5ft Movement Speed).
    - **Path A: The Assassin**
        - **Dirty Fighting:** Deal an extra **1d6 Damage** to any target that is Prone, Restrained, or engaged with an ally.
        - **Vanish:** You may take the **Hide** action as a **Bonus Action**.
        - **Throat Slit:** Attacks against **Surprised** creatures deal double damage dice.
            - **Mastery (Lethality):** +1 Sneak Attack Damage (Static) per point invested.
    - **Path B: The Ranger**
        - **Hunter's Mark:** As a **Bonus Action**, mark a target. You deal **+1d4 damage** to them, and you have Advantage on checks to find them.
        - **Predator's Sight:** Gain **Darkvision (60ft)**. If you already have it, gain **+30ft**.
        - **Trap Master:** You can craft snares and alarms during a Short Rest. Gain Proficiency with the **Traps** skill.
            - **Mastery (Favored Enemy):** +2 Damage vs Beasts/Monstrosities per point invested.
    - **Path C: The Acrobat**
        - **Parkour:** Climbing and Swimming cost no extra movement. You take half damage from falling.
        - **Evasion:** When you succeed on a DEX Save to take half damage, you instead take **no damage**.
        - **Uncanny Dodge:** When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use **1 RP** to halve the attack's damage.
            - **Mastery (Fleet Foot):** +5ft Movement Speed per point invested.
### **The Way of the Arcane**
_The path of peaks and valleys. Magic allows you to break physics, but it costs **Mana** and requires **Affinity**. Spell DCs are **8 + Proficiency + Casting Stat Modifier**._

**Root Skill: Arcane Conduit**
- **Effect:** Unlock a **Mana Pool**. Your Max Mana is equal to **Intelligence Modifier × 5**. You regain all Mana on a Long Rest and half of your mana on a short rest.
- **Growth:** Each time you invest a point in this tree, gain **+1d8 + Intelligence Modifier** mana.
### **Branch A: Elemental Mastery**
_The manipulation of matter and energy. When you enter this branch, you do not pick a "class"; you pick a discipline of physics to violate._

**Tier 1: Matter State Control** (Passive. Pick one affinity: Fire, Water, Air, or Earth. You gain Resistance to damage of that type).
#### **Thermodynamics**
_Affinity: Fire. Stat: Intelligence._
- **Path A: The Pyromancer**
    - **Combustion:** (Action, **5 Mana**) Range 60ft. Ranged Spell Attack. Deal **1d10 + Fire Affinity** Fire damage. **Ignite:** On a critical hit, the target burns for **1d4** damage at the start of their turns until they use an Action to douse the flames.
        - **Cauterize:** (Action, **10 Mana**) Touch a willing creature. They take **1d4 Fire** damage (cannot be resisted) but immediately stop all Bleeding effects and regain **2d8 + INT** Health (HP).
        - **Fireball:** (Action, **20 Mana**) Range 150ft. A bright streak flashes from your finger to a point you choose, blossoming into a 20ft radius explosion. Deal **8d6 + Fire Affinity** Fire damage. DEX Save for half. Flammable objects ignite.
        - **Inferno:** (Reaction, **15 Mana**) When you are hit by a melee attack, you detonate the air around you. All creatures within 5ft take **2d10 Fire** damage and are pushed 10ft back.
            - **Supernova:** (Action, **50 Mana**) 60ft Radius centered on you. All creatures (except you) take **10d10 Fire** damage. The ground turns to glass/magma (Difficult Terrain, **2d6 Fire** damage per turn).
                - **Mastery (Eternal Flame):** +1 Fire Base Damage per point invested.
- **Path B: The Ionizer**
    - **Static Shock:** (Reaction, **2 Mana**) When a creature touches you or hits you with a metal weapon, they take **1d8 Lightning** damage and cannot take Reactions until their next turn.
        - **Chain Lightning:** (Action, **25 Mana**) Range 60ft. An arc of lightning strikes a target for **4d8 Lightning**, then jumps to up to 3 other targets within 30ft. Each jump deals equal damage. DEX Save for half.
        - **Plasma Beam:** (Action, **40 Mana**) Range 100ft Line (5ft wide). Deal **8d8 + Fire Affinity** Radiant/Lightning damage. This spell **ignores Armor Rating (AC)**; targets must make a DEX Save or take full damage.
            - **Mastery (Overload):** +5 Max Mana per point invested.
#### **Fluid Dynamics**
_Affinity: Water. Stat: Wisdom._
- **Path A: The Tidecaller (Hydrokinesis)**
    - **Ice Shard:** (Action, **5 Mana**) Range 90ft. Ranged Spell Attack. Deal **1d10 + Water Affinity** Piercing/Cold damage. **Chill:** Target's movement speed is reduced by 10ft.
        - **Water Whip:** (Bonus Action, **10 Mana**) Range 30ft. Melee Spell Attack. Deal **2d6 Cold** damage and pull the target **15ft** directly towards you.
        - **Glacial Prison:** (Action, **30 Mana**) Range 60ft. Target must pass a CON Save or be **Petrified (Frozen)**. They are encased in ice with **50 HP**. While frozen, they are immune to all damage except Bludgeoning and Fire (which breaks the ice).
            - **Tsunami:** (Action, **60 Mana**) Conjure a wall of water 20ft high and 50ft wide. It crashes forward 50ft. Creatures inside take **6d10 Bludgeoning** and are swept to the end of the wave.
                - **Mastery (Deep Freeze):** +1 AC (Ice Armor) per point invested.
- **Path B: The Bio-Mancer**
    - **Mend Flesh:** (Action, **10 Mana**) Touch range. Restore **1d8 + WIS** Health (HP). _Note: This heals actual injury, bypassing Vitality._
        - **Desiccate:** (Action, **30 Mana**) Range 30ft. Rip the water from a target's cells. Deal **6d8 Necrotic** damage. You regain HP equal to half the damage dealt. Plants take double damage.
        - **Blood Bend:** (Action, **40 Mana**) Range 60ft. Target must make a CON Save. On a fail, you control their physical motor functions for their next turn. You can make them Move and Attack, but cannot force them to cast spells or speak.
            - **Mastery (Vitality Siphon):** Healing spells restore an additional +2 HP per point invested.
#### **Aerodynamics**
_Affinity: Air. Stat: Dexterity or Intelligence._
- **Path A: The Gale**
    - **Air Slash:** (Action, **5 Mana**) Melee Spell Attack with 15ft reach. Deal **1d8 + Air Affinity** Force damage. This attack is silent.
        - **Tailwind:** (Passive) Gain **+10ft Movement Speed**. You ignore Difficult Terrain composed of gas, rubble, or wind.
        - **Flight:** (Action, **20 Mana**) Gain a Fly Speed of **60ft** for 10 minutes. Requires Concentration.
            - **Mastery (Mach 1):** +5ft Fly Speed per point invested.
- **Path B: The Void**
    - **Vacuum Bubble:** (Action, **15 Mana**) Create a 15ft radius sphere of absolute silence at a point within 60ft. No sound passes through. Thunder damage is negated inside. Creatures inside cannot cast verbal spells.
        - **Suffocate:** (Action, **30 Mana**) Concentration. Range 60ft. Target must make a CON Save each turn. On fail, they take **2d6 True Damage** (HP) and are Silenced.
        - **Implosion:** (Action, **50 Mana**) Create a singularity. All creatures within 20ft are pulled to the center. They take **8d6 Force** damage as they collide.
            - **Mastery (Nullify):** +1 to Spell Save DC per 2 points invested.
#### **Discipline 4: Tectonics**
_Affinity: Earth. Stat: Strength or Constitution._
- **Path A: The Architect**
    - **Hurl Stone:** (Action, **5 Mana**) Ranged Spell Attack. Deal **3d8 + Earth Affinity** Bludgeoning damage. Target must succeed on a STR Save or be knocked **Prone**.
        - **Stone Skin:** (Reaction, **15 Mana**) Gain **Resistance** to non-magical Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage until the start of your next turn.
        - **Wall of Stone:** (Action, **30 Mana**) Create a permanent wall (AC 15, 30 HP per 10ft section). Max length 60ft.
            - **Mastery (Granite Soul):** +1 Physical Resistance (Damage Reduction) per point invested.
- **Path B: The Graviturgist**
    - **Heavy Step:** (Action, **10 Mana**) Target within 30ft has their gravity increased. Speed halved, cannot Jump or Fly.
        - **Quake:** (Action, **35 Mana**) 40ft radius. Ground buckles. Creatures take **4d10 Bludgeoning** and fall Prone. Area becomes Difficult Terrain.
        - **Reverse Gravity:** (Action, **40 Mana**) 20ft radius cylinder. Gravity reverses. Creatures fall upward 100ft.
            - **Mastery (Impact):** +2 Earth/Force Damage per point invested.
