There are only two classes, Fighter and Magician, these represent how strong you are with physical or magical aspects. As you level up you will unlock skills and subclass features which will allow you to specialize into more specific fighting styles. 
#### Definitions
**Hit Dice:** Role this dice and add to your hit points total
**Vitality Dice:** Role this dice and add to your vitality points total and add your constitution modifier
**Mana Dice:** Role this dice and add to your mana points total and add your wisdom modifier
**Stat Point (Stp):** You can add these points to your Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom, Intelligence, or Charisma scores. Normal costs apply for stats over 20
**Skill Point (Skp):** You can add these points to your skills such as athletics and slight of hand and so on
**Affinity Point (Affp):** You may add these to your Affinity scores (besides generic) such as Fire, Earth, Water, Air, Craft, Creation, Utility, and Physical 
**Action Point (Ap):** The number of actions you may take per turn, cannot go lower than 1
**Bonus Action Point (BAp):** The number of bonus actions you can take per turn, cannot go lower than 1. You may consume 2 BAp to gain 1 Ap during your turn. 
**Reaction Point (Rp):** The amount of reactions you can take in the round until your next turn, cannot go lower than 1
