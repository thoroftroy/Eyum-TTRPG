Saramama, the wild mother of all creatures, grants magic that strengthens bonds with animals, calls forth mighty beasts, and awakens primal instincts.

- Domain: Animals, Beasts, Kinship
    
- Alignment: Chaotic Good
    
- Symbol: A wolf paw inside a sunburst
    
- Behavior: Followers of Saramama cherish the gifts of the land and the cycles that sustain life. They are nurturing, patient, and devoted to community, seeing the act of giving as sacred. To serve Saramama is to sow generously, tend faithfully, and celebrate the fruits of labor, not only in fields, but in relationships and healing. Her faithful are farmers, midwives, herbalists, and caretakers, often humble in station but rich in kindness. They work quietly yet steadfastly to feed the hungry, heal the sick, and foster growth wherever they walk. Saramama blesses those who live in harmony with the land, who give more than they take, and who help others flourish.
    

Level 5 Spell: Pack Leader’s Call

- Mana Cost: 20
    
- Affinity: Animal, Enhancement
    
- Range: 60ft (30ft radius)
    
- Concentration: Yes (5 turns)
    
- Effect:
    

- You emit a powerful call that empowers all beasts and animal-like allies in the area.
    
- All friendly creatures gain +2 to attack rolls and AC, and their movement speed increases by 10ft.
    
- Any animal or beast in the area instantly becomes friendly to you and will obey simple commands.
    
- If no beasts are present, a spectral wolf spirit manifests, fighting alongside you with HP equal to your level × 3, (Damage = 2d8 as a melee attack).
    

- Upcast: Increase bonus stats (+2) or summon additional wolf spirits (+1) per x2 Mana.
    

Level 10 Spell: Primal Awakening

- Mana Cost: 50
    
- Affinity: Transformation, Animal, Strength
    
- Range: Self (30ft Aura)
    
- Concentration: Yes (5 turns)
    
- Effect:
    

- You and all allies take on primal animalistic traits, gaining:
    

- +12 Strength and Dexterity
    
- Darkvision (120ft)
    
- Climbing and swimming speed equal to walking speed
    
- Unarmed attacks deal 2d10 Slashing damage (claws/fangs).
    

- You may choose one ally to undergo a complete bestial transformation, turning them into a Dire Beast (GM’s discretion) for the duration.
    

- Upcast: Increase stats (+6) per x2 Mana.
    

Level 20 Spell: Saramama’s Wild Hunt

- Mana Cost: 100
    
- Affinity: Nature, Beast, Summoning
    
- Range: 120ft (60ft radius)
    
- Concentration: Yes (5 turns)
    
- Effect:
    

- You summon a celestial stampede of primal beasts that fight for you.
    
- The Wild Hunt consists of:
    

- Two Ancient Dire Wolves (HP = Your Total Level × 10, 4d12 Bite Damage)
    
- A Gargantuan Sky Serpent (HP = Your Total Level × 5, Flying, 80ft speed, 8d10 Lightning Breath)
    
- A Titan Bear (HP = Your Total Level × 20, Massive, 5d12 Claw Attacks)
    

- Enemies in the area must make a Wisdom Save (DC 20) or be paralyzed in fear for 3 turns.
    
- Any allied beast or animal in the area gains double damage and regeneration (20 HP per turn) for the duration.
    
- After the spell ends, the battlefield remains scarred with nature’s fury, causing wildlife to flourish or reclaim the land.
    

- Upcast: Summon additional beasts (+1 of each) per x2 Mana.
