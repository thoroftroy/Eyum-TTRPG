- Mana Cost: 8
    
- Affinity: Radiant
    
- Range: 15ft radius (Centered on self)
    
- Concentration: Yes (5 turns)
    
- Effect: Allies in the area regain 2d8 HP per turn.

- Upcast: +2d8 HP per x2 Mana Cost.
