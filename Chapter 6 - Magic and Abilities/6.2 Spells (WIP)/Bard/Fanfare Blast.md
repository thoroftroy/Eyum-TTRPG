Mana Cost: 14  
Affinity: Generic  
Range: Self (30ft cone)  
Concentration: No  
Effect: Creatures in the cone must make a Dexterity Save (DC 16). On fail, take 3d6 Force damage and are Deafened until the end of their next turn; on success, half damage, no Deafened.
