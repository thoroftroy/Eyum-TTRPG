Mana Cost: 18  
Affinity: Water (Cold)  
Range: 60ft (wall up to 30ft long, 10ft high, 5ft thick)  
Concentration: Yes (5 turns)  
Effect: Create a wall of solid ice that blocks movement. A creature forced through the wall takes 3d6 Cold damage (Dexterity Save DC 16 halves). Attacks through the wall are at Disadvantage; line of effect for spells is blocked. The wall has 60 HP; Fire damage is doubled against it.  
Upcast: +20 HP and +10ft length per x2 Mana.
