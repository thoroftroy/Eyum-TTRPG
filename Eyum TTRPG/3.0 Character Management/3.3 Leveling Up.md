Level ups will be granted at the GM's discretion. Each level you gain will gain you bonuses:

**Every Level** _(1,2,3,4,5,6,7,etc)_
- +1 Skill Tree Level
- +2 Skill Points
- +1 Vitality Dice
- +1 Health Dice
- +1 Mana Dice

**Every 2 Levels** _(2,4,6,8,10,etc)_
- Gain +1 Affinity in Fire, Earth, Water, or Air affinity if you have any levels in Magical path.
- Gain +3 Vitality and +1 Hp if you have any levels in the Physical path.
- Gain +2 Skill Points if you have any levels in the Utility & Professional path.

**Every 3 Levels** _(3,6,9,12,15,etc)_
- +2 Stat Points
- +2 Affinity Points
- +1 Proficiency Upgrade or 2 Skill Points
- Gain 1 Feat
- Gain 1 Spell if you have taken any levels in the Magical path.
- If you have taken levels in the physical or magical paths you may do the following:
	- If you have more levels in the magical path gain +2 INT.
	- If you have more levels in the physical path gain +1 STR and +1 DEX.

> Feats are located in [[3.4 Feats]].

**Every 8 Levels** _(8,16,24,etc)_
- 1 Bonus Action Point (BAp)
- 1 Expertise Upgrade (must already be proficient in skill) or 5 Skill Points

**Every 10 Levels** _(10,20,30,etc)_
- 1 Action Point (Ap)
- +5 Stat Points

For more information on stats and their caps see [[1.6 Other Rules]] at the bottom section titled "Going Past the Limits With Conversions".
## Skill Tree
Every level, you earn 1 Skill Tree Level. This point is used to unlock new paths or progress deeper into your chosen specialties.
##### How to Progress
1. **Unlock the Path (The Root):** You must spend 1 Skill Tree Level to enter a Path (e.g., Physical, Magical). This grants an Initial Bonus.
2. **Unlock the Archetype (The Type):** Once a Path is open, spend your next point to unlock the Level 1 Perk of a specific Archetype (e.g., The Marksman).
3. **Linear Progression:** You must take Level 1 before Level 2, and Level 2 before Level 3 and so on.
4. **Sub-Levels (e.g., Level 1.5, Level 1.75):** These are optional "branches." You do not need them to reach Level 2, but you must have the preceding whole number to take them (e.g., you must have Level 1 to take Level 1.5). If there multiple optional levels on the same level you may take them in any order regardless of numbering (Level 3.5 and Level 3.75 are technically on the same level).

    - **Level 1:** Gain access to Tier 2 pacts with Fiends.
    - **Level 2:** Gain access to Tier 3 pacts with Fiends.
    - **Level 3:** Gain access to Tier 4 pacts with Fiends.
    - **Level 4:** Gain access to Tier 5 pacts with Fiends.
- **The Eldritch Horror**
- *Use otherworldly power to make yourself stronger. If you take a level in Eldritch Horror you may no longer take levels in The Beacon, The Occultist, or The Reaper and all levels previously taken in them will be lost.*
	- **Level 1:** Learn the spell Eldritch Blast. You lose 20 karma. 
		- **Level 1.5:** Gain +2 Eldritch Affinity, you may take this Level as many times as you wish. You lose 1 karma. 
	- **Level 2:** Your Eldritch Blast deals 3 damage instead of 1. Your veins glow a faint purple under your skin while using magic. You lose 1 karma. 
		- **Level 2.5:** Gain +20 ft of True Sight and +5 ft movement speed. You lose 1 karma. 
	- **Level 3:** Your Eldritch Blast's range increases by 60 ft. Your eyes glow a faint purple while using magic. You lose 1 karma. 
		- **Level 3.5:** Eldritch Affinity can now be upgraded with Affp. You lose 1 karma. 
	- **Level 4:** Your Eldritch Blast now costs 1 mana. You may learn 1 spell.  You lose 3 karma. 
	- **Level 5:** Learn the spell Eldritch Explosion. Your skin becomes cold to the touch at all times. You lose 10 karma. 
		- *The following Sub-levels must be taken in order.*
		- **Level 5.1:** Gain +20 ft fly speed (dark energy surrounds you and lifts you). You lose 5 karma. 
		- **Level 5.2:** Gain +20 ft fly speed. Creatures within 10 ft of you while you are flying have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You lose 5 karma. 
		- **Level 5.3:** Learn the spell Eldritch Tentacle. You lose 5 karma. 
		- **Level 5.4:** Gain +5 ft fly speed, you may take this Level as many times as you wish. You lose 5 karma. 
	- *You may not take levels beyond this point if you are of a good alignment. If you are of the good alignment you must do evil acts to lower your Karma first.*
	- **Level 6:** You may spend a Bonus Action (BAp) with any attack (not aoe) to infuse it with Eldritch Energy which changes the damage type and affinity (for spells) to Eldritch. On hit target gains the Eldritch Curse condition for 2 turns.  You lose 3 karma. 
		- **Level 6.5:** When targets gain the Eldritch Curse condition from one of your attacks they also take 5 eldritch damage at the start of each turn. You lose 3 karma. 
	- **Level 7:** Your Eldritch Blast deals 6 damage instead of 3. You become thinner and more frail looking, your eyes become sunken and hollow, lose 1 to your STR and CON scores. You lose 3 karma. 
	- **Level 8:** Your Eldritch Blast fires 3 beams as a base instead of 1. You may learn 1 spell. Your voice becomes frail and scratchy, lose 1 to your CHAR score. You lose 3 karma. 
	- **Level 9:** You may choose which creatures are or aren't affected by your Eldritch Explosion spell whom are in the radius. Your shadows lag behind your movements be a second. You lose 10 karma. 
	- *You may not take levels beyond this point if you are of a good/neutral alignment. If you are of the good/neutral alignment you must do evil acts to lower your Karma first.*
	- **Level 10:** The Unknown One has noticed your presence. You have advantage on Intimidation checks and gain +5 to Intimidation. All creatures who have pacts with deities will view you as a monster/danger who should be killed. You can no longer be resurrected by traditional means. You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 11:** All Eldritch Damage you deal deals 1.25x more damage (rounding up). Gain +5 Eldritch Affinity. You may learn 1 spell. You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 12:** When you deal more than 20 Eldritch damage at once with a single attack the target must make a STR save (DC 10 + Save DC Bonus) or be knocked prone. You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 13:** When you inflict Eldritch Curse the duration is doubled and it now does 5 eldritch damage at the start of each turn (if you have Level 6.5 damage is increased to 20). You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 14:** Learn the spell Eldritch Shadow. Damage multiplier from Eldritch Shadow stacks with Level 11 (add the multipliers to be 1.75x then multiply the damage). You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 15:** The Unknown One is pleased with your performance. Whenever you would deal Eldritch damage to Vitality you may choose to deal half of that damage to Health. You gain a greater weakness to Hallowed damage, this weakness cannot be removed by any means. You lose 10 karma. 
		- *The following Sub-levels must be taken in order.*
		- **Level 15.1:** Your Karma score lower cap is now -750 instead of -500. 
		- **Level 15.2:** You lose 25 karma.
		- **Level 15.3:** Your Karma score lower cap is now -1000 instead of -750. 
		- **Level 15.4:** You lose 50 karma.
		- **Level 15.5:** Your Karma score lower cap is now -1500 instead of -1000. 
		- **Level 15.6:** You lose 100 karma.
		- **Level 15.7:** Your Karma score lower cap is now -2500 instead of -1500. 
		- **Level 15.8:** You lose 175 karma.
		- **Level 15.9:** Your Karma score no longer has a lower cap. 
	- **Level 16:** When you die you will come back to life after 24 hours. Your body will regenerate and look the same as you did before but slightly more frail. You are now considered an undead creature. You no longer age. As an undead you gain +5 to your Necrotic and Eldritch Affinities and when you would normally make a roll with your Eldritch Affinity you may also add your Necrotic affinity. You may resurrect once every week, if you die again during the cool down period you are dead permanently. As an undead you suffer a -10 penalty on persuasion rolls against creatures of a good or Neutral alignment. You lose 25 karma. 
	- **Level 17:** You may now add your Generic Affinity to things which normally use Eldritch Affinity only. You may learn 1 spell. You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 18:** When you deal Eldritch Damage the damage is multiplied by an additional 1.25 (up to 2x from 1.75x with Eldritch Shadow or 1.5x without). You lose 10 karma. 
	- **Level 19:** Your race is now considered a Lich (applies like a racial modifier on top of your race). If you have made it to this point without dying you are now considered an undead and immediately gain +20 Eldritch Affinity (you do not gain this bonus if you have resurrected before). You may now resurrect once a day instead of once a week and resurrection takes 3 hours instead of 24. You lose 25 karma. 
	- **Level 20:** The Unknown One considers you one of his own. You are now capable of dealing damage to deities, deities take full damage from your attacks. All creatures will view you as a monster/danger which needs to be killed. You are considered the pinnacle of Absolute Evil. You lose 50 karma. When you deal Eldritch Damage the damage is multiplied by an additional 1.25x (Up to 2.25x with Eldritch Shadow or 1.75x without). You may change the Affinity and Damage type of any spell you know into Eldritch. You may add the absolute value of 1 fifth of your Karma (your karma but positive) rounded down (|karma| / 10) to any damage roll of the eldritch damage type at the cost of an action (Ap), damage doubling from previous tiers does not effect this damage (apply damage doubling to the damage from the original attack then add this karma damage). 
- **The Godslayer**
- *Requires at least 5 levels in The Eldritch Horror.*
	- **Level 1:** You can now deal damage to Deities. All Deities have greater resistance to your damage. 
	- **Level 2:** You have +10 to hit against Deities.
		- **Level 2.5:** You gain an additional +5 to hit against Deities, you may take this Level as many times as you wish. 
	- **Level 3:** All Deities have resistance to your damage. 
	- **Level 4:** You gain +10 to saving throws inflicted from Deities. 
	- **Level 5:** All Deities have lesser resistance to your damage. 
	- **Level 6:** You have lesser resistance to all damage dealt from Deities.
	- **Level 7:** All Deities now take full damage from you. 
	- **Level 8:** You deal +5 damage to Deities.
		- **Level 8.5:** You deal +3 damage to Deities, you may take this Level as many times as you wish. 
	- **Level 9:** All Deities have disadvantage on saving throws imposed by you and the DC of those rolls raises by +5.
		- **level 9.5:** The DC raises by an additional +3, you may take this Level as many times as you wish. 
	- **Level 10:** You deal double damage to Deities and have advantage on attack rolls against them.
