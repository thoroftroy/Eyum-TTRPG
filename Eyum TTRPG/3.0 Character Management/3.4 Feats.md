A feat is a special ability you gain as you level up. 

Types of Feats: 
- **Passive**: A feat that is always active.
- **Conditional**: A passive feat which is only active during some conditions.
- **Active**: An active use ability or skill, these can be actions, bonus actions, reactions, or free actions.
