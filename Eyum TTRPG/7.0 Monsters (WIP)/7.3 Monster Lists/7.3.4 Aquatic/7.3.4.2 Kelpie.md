A Kelpie is a horse adapted to live in water. Its body is built like a heavy riding horse with a deep chest, strong legs, and a short sleek coat that is smooth and glossy. The coat is deep blue-grey to near-black, slick like sealskin, and sheds water instantly. The hooves are broad and webbed, spreading wide on each step to prevent sinking into mud or silt. The mane and tail are long and thick, made of hair that does not mat or tangle underwater. The Kelpie has gill slits along the sides of its neck just behind the jaw that open when submerged, allowing it to breathe underwater indefinitely. The nostrils seal shut when diving.

On land the Kelpie walks with a heavy awkward gait, its webbed hooves slapping against hard ground and its movements noticeably slower than a normal horse. In water it moves with the same effortless speed a land horse has on open plains. A Kelpie can be ridden underwater at its full swim speed but the rider must provide their own means of breathing. A Kelpie can carry up to 300 lbs of rider and gear at full speed, or up to 500 lbs at half speed. Kelpies are wild animals that can be tamed and trained like any other horse. They have the intelligence of a normal horse and are not aggressive, fleeing rather than fighting unless cornered. They breed like normal horses: a mare carries a single foal for 11 months, the foal reaches level 1 after 1 year, and a Kelpie is considered adult and able to breed at level 5. Kelpies gain 1 level per year and live 30 to 40 years. When killed a Kelpie can be harvested for its hide and meat like any large animal. The hide is a waterproof leather used for aquatic gear. The meat is edible, lean, and slightly salty. See [[2.6 Materials]] for details.
###### Unique Abilities
- **Swift Current (Passive):** The Kelpie gains +5 ft to its Swim speed at level 3 and every 2 levels thereafter (level 3, 5, 7, 9, etc.). This bonus is cumulative.

**Standard Passives:** Affinity Body
**Standard Actives:** Slam, Charge
