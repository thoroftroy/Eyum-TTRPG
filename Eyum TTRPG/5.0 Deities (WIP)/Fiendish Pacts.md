Fiends typically want to spread chaos and death, typically by the means of their type of damage. They gain power from people fearing their Affinity. Fiends punishments are usually very severe.

> For condition references see [[2.4 Conditions]]. For damage type references see [[2.2 Damage Types]].

## Black Mercy
*Necrotic, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Necrotic damage. Once per turn when you damage a creature, you may convert that damage to Necrotic. If you do, you heal for 1d10 Vitality. You must, today, witness a death or kill a living creature yourself. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slow Death]] (3 stacks) that cannot be removed for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash a 30 ft cone of necrotic energy. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or take 4d10 Necrotic damage directly to their Health (bypassing Vitality). You heal for half the damage dealt. You must, before the end of the pact, personally attend a funeral or death rite and speak the deceased's name aloud. Failure results in you being unable to regain Health for 7 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per long rest, as a bonus action, you may Mark one creature within 60 ft for death. For 1 minute, that creature has disadvantage on all saves, cannot regain HP, and all damage you deal to it goes directly to Health (bypassing Vitality). You must kill at least 10 humanoid creatures during the pact period. Failure results in the [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] condition and [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Despair]] for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you may declare an Embrace of Mercy in a 40 ft radius. All non-deity creatures must make a CON save (DC 15) or be reduced to 0 Health instantly. Creatures below 25% Vitality automatically fail. Those who succeed take 5d10 Necrotic damage. You must kill at least 50 humanoid creatures during the pact period. Failure results in permanent [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slow Death]] (5 stacks) and you cannot be healed by any means for 30 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Black Mercy Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may drain the life from all creatures in a 60 ft radius. Each must make a CON save (DC 16) or take 10d10 Necrotic damage to Health. You heal for all damage dealt this way. You must kill at least 200 humanoid creatures before the pact ends. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Red Furnace
*Magma, Neutral Alignment*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Magma and Fire damage. You leave a trail of molten rock wherever you walk. Creatures that end their turn adjacent to you take 1d10 Magma damage. You must, today, destroy or melt something made of stone or metal. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|On Fire]] (5 stacks) that cannot be removed for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you spew a 40 ft cone of magma. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 13) or take 4d10 Magma damage and be [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Restrained]] as the magma cools. Those who succeed take half damage. You must, once per day, destroy a structure or object of value (a wall, a door, a piece of furniture worth 10+ Gold). Failure results in you being Restrained for 3 turns at the start of each combat for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Your body radiates intense heat. Melee attackers take 3d10 Magma damage. You are immune to Fire damage. You leave a trail of molten rock that persists for 1 minute, dealing 2d10 Magma damage to creatures that cross it. You must, by the end of the pact, raze a building or settlement structure. Failure results in permanent [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|On Fire]] (3 stacks) that cannot be removed and deals damage each hour for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you cause the ground to erupt in a 60 ft radius around you. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 15) or take 10d10 Magma damage and gain [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|On Fire]] (5 stacks). The area becomes difficult terrain for the rest of combat. You must, by the end of the pact, kill at least 30 creatures with Magma or Fire damage. Failure results in permanent [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slow Death]] (5 stacks) and half movement speed for 30 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Red Furnace Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may unleash a volcanic eruption in a 100 ft radius centered on yourself. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 16) or take 15d10 Magma damage and gain [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|On Fire]] (10 stacks). The area becomes molten wasteland for the rest of combat. You must, by the end of the pact, destroy a city block or equivalent large structure. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Knotted Word
*[[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]], Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] damage. Once per turn when you hit a creature, you may apply Cursed to it for 1 minute. You must, today, make someone promise you something in writing. Failure results in you being Cursed for 4 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Once per turn, as a Reaction when a creature within 60 ft rolls a d20, you may force it to reroll and use the lower result. If the reroll is lower, that creature also becomes [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] for 1 minute. You must, once per day, trick someone into agreeing to something they did not fully understand. Failure results in you being Cursed and unable to benefit from advantage for 7 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 30 ft radius Contract Zone for 1 minute. Enemies inside cannot benefit from advantage. The first time each round an enemy inside rolls a d20, it takes 3d10 [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] damage to its HP. You must, before the end of the pact, bind at least 5 creatures with a verbal or written contract. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Despair]] for 10 days and all your contracts or promises being automatically broken.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** All your attacks apply the [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] condition for 1 turn. When a Cursed creature within 60 ft takes an Action, you may spend a Reaction to cancel that Action (it does nothing). You must, before the end of the pact, make at least 20 creatures sign or agree to something that disadvantages them. Failure results in you losing your first Action each combat for 20 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Knotted Word Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It cannot take Actions, Bonus Actions, or Reactions for 3 turns. At the start of each of its turns it takes 6d10 [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Cursed]] damage to its HP. You must, before the pact ends, kill at least 100 creatures and apply Cursed at least 200 times. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Pale Cinder
*Ash, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Ash damage. When you deal damage, you may convert it to Ash damage. Creatures you damage with Ash become [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Blurred]] for 1 turn. You must, today, let something burn to ash (a piece of paper, wood, cloth). Failure results in you being Blurred for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 30 ft radius Ash Cloud within 60 ft for 1 minute. Creatures inside have disadvantage on attack rolls and search/spot checks. The first time each round an enemy inside takes damage, it takes an additional 2d10 Ash damage. You must, once per day, extinguish a light source or cause darkness in an area. Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Blinded]] for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** When a creature's [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|On Fire]] condition ends within 60 ft of you, you may use the remaining ash. As a bonus action: gain +2 AC until your next turn; or deal 2d10 Ash damage to a creature within 30 ft; or heal 1d10 Vitality. You may keep this ash for up to 3 turns. You must, before the end of the pact, kill at least 10 creatures with Ash or Fire damage. Failure results in you being unable to take Reactions for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you may cover the sky in ash in a 120 ft radius for 3 turns. During this time: enemies cannot regain HP and take 3d10 Ash damage to HP at the start of their turns; all light counts as dim or darkness; you are constantly in stealth while more than 15 ft from enemies. You must, before the end of the pact, have at least 40 creatures witness your use of this ability. Failure results in you taking 2d10 Ash damage at the start of each of your turns in combat for 20 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Pale Cinder Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you create an Ashfall in a 60 ft radius within 120 ft for 3 turns. Enemies inside take 6d10 Ash damage to HP at the start of their turns, gain the [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] condition, and have movement halved. You must deal at least 600 total Ash damage during the pact. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Wither Bloom
*Blight, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Blight damage. When you deal damage, you may convert it to Blight damage. Creatures you damage become [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Withered]] (1 stack). You must, today, let something organic rot or decay (food, a plant, a corpse). Failure results in you being Withered for 4 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, choose one creature within 60 ft. Until the end of your next turn, the first time it takes damage it also takes 3d10 Blight damage. You must kill at least 1 creature from the [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Withered]] condition during the pact. Failure results in you being Withered for 7 days and unable to regain Vitality.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 30 ft radius Rot Field within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside take 3d10 Blight damage at the start of their turns and have disadvantage on saving throws. You must deal at least 200 total Blight damage during the pact. Failure results in you being unable to regain Vitality by any means for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** When you deal damage, you may convert it to Blight. Once per combat, as a Reaction when a creature within 60 ft regains Vitality, you may negate that regain and deal 4d10 Blight damage to its HP instead. You must kill at least 5 creatures from the [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Withered]] condition during the pact. Failure results in permanent loss of half your maximum Vitality.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Wither Bloom Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, choose up to 3 creatures within 60 ft. They gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Withered]] immediately and take 6d10 Blight damage. You must apply Withered to at least 60 creatures and have at least 30 die from the condition during the pact. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Serpent's Kiss
*Poison, Neutral Alignment*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Poison damage. Your melee attacks apply [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] (1 stack) on hit. You must, today, poison something (food, drink, a weapon tip). Failure results in you being Poisoned for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may blow a 20 ft cone of venom. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] (2 stacks) and take 3d10 Poison damage. You must, once per day, get someone to trust you while you have ulterior motives. Failure results in you being Poisoned (4 stacks) for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 20 ft radius Venom Ring within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies that start their turn in the ring take 3d10 Poison damage and gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]]. If they are already Poisoned, they take an additional 2d10 Poison damage to HP. You must deal at least 250 total Poison damage during the pact. Failure results in permanent -2 to all saving throws and attack rolls.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per combat, for 3 turns, whenever a creature within 30 ft would remove [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]], it fails and instead takes 4d10 Poison damage and gains Poisoned for another turn. You must kill at least 1 humanoid creature per day by Poison damage during the pact. Failure results in you being permanently Poisoned and gaining Greater Weakness to Poison.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Serpent's Kiss Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It gains [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] (6 stacks) with no save. You must poison at least 300 unique humanoid creatures during the pact. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Lung Spore
*Toxin, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Toxin damage. When you miss an attack against a creature, it must make a CON save (DC 11) or take 2d10 Toxin damage. You must, today, infect someone with a disease or make someone sick (a lie about contamination counts). Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you exhale a 20 ft cone of spores. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] and [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Mute]] for 1 turn and take 3d10 Toxin damage. You must kill at least 3 humanoid creatures with Toxin damage during the pact. Failure results in you being Mute for 7 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 30 ft radius Spore Fog within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside have disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws. At the start of their turns, they take 3d10 Toxin damage to HP. You must kill at least 7 creatures with Toxin damage during the pact. Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] (3 stacks) at all times for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** As an action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It must make a CON save (DC 15) or gain [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] and [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] for 3 turns and take 4d10 Toxin damage. You must kill at least 12 humanoid creatures with Toxin damage during the pact. Failure results in you starting each combat Suffocating for 3 turns for 20 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Lung Spore Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you create a 60 ft radius Dead Air within 120 ft for 3 turns. Enemies inside gain [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] and take 6d10 Toxin damage to HP at the start of their turns. You must kill at least 40 humanoid creatures during the pact. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Shuddering Idol
*Tremor, Neutral Alignment*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Tremor and Quake damage. Once per turn when you hit a creature, you may knock it [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Prone]] if it is your size or smaller. You must, today, make someone flinch or show fear. Failure results in you being knocked Prone at the start of each combat for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you stomp the ground. Creatures in a 40 ft radius must make a DEX save (DC 13) or be knocked [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Prone]] and take 3d10 Tremor damage. You must, once per day, spread fear or panic in a public place. Failure results in you being unable to stand after being knocked Prone for 3 days (you must spend your full turn standing).

**Tier 3 (6 days):** You gain DR 20 against all physical damage. You cannot be knocked [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Prone]] or moved by any effect. Your footsteps cause the ground to shake — creatures within 20 ft must make a DEX save (DC 13) at the start of their turns or fall Prone. You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 20 creatures flee from you in fear. Failure results in you being Vulnerable to all physical damage for 10 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you slam the ground. Creatures in a 60 ft radius must make a DEX save (DC 15) or take 8d10 Tremor damage and be knocked [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Prone]]. Structures in the area take double damage. You must, by the end of the pact, cause a structure to collapse or be severely damaged. Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Stunned]] for 1 turn at the start of each combat for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (24 days):** You gain all Shuddering Idol Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you make a Seismic Strike against a creature within reach. The target takes 12d10 Tremor damage and all creatures within a 40 ft line behind it must make a DEX save (DC 16) or take the same damage. You must, by the end of the pact, cause a panic event that affects at least 100 people. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Drowned Prophet
*Deluge, Neutral Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Deluge and Water damage. Water pools around you in a 15 ft radius, becoming 1 ft deep. The area counts as difficult terrain for others; you move through it freely. You must, today, predict a disaster (weather, social, personal) and be correct. Failure results in you being unable to breathe water and [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] for 1 turn each hour for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash a 60 ft line of rushing water (20 ft wide). Creatures must make a STR save (DC 13) or be pushed 60 ft and take 4d10 Deluge damage. You must, once per day, warn someone of a coming catastrophe and be taken seriously. Failure results in you being unable to speak for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** You can breathe underwater and ignore the effects of deep water pressure. Once per turn, when a creature within 60 ft speaks, you may force it to make a WIS save (DC 13) or spend its action coughing up water. You must, by the end of the pact, survive a flood, storm, or similar disaster. Failure results in you being unable to hold your breath (immediately [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Suffocating]] in water) for 10 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you summon a 100 ft line of torrential water (30 ft wide) that surges forward. Creatures must make a STR save (DC 15) or take 8d10 Deluge damage and be pushed 100 ft. You must, by the end of the pact, convert at least 10 people to a doomsday or catastrophe cult. Failure results in you being unable to breathe air (treat all air as water) for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Drowned Prophet Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you summon a storm in a 1-mile radius for 1 hour. Torrential rain falls. Visibility is 30 ft, ranged attacks have disadvantage, and exposed enemies take 3d10 Deluge damage at the start of their turns. You must, by the end of the pact, drown or flood a settlement. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Screaming Hymn

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Shatter and Thunder damage. Your voice deals 1d10 Shatter damage to all creatures within 15 ft whenever you speak (you may whisper to avoid this). You must, today, make an unnecessary loud noise in a quiet place. Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Deafened]] for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Each time you deal damage, you apply 1 Resonance stack (max 3 per target). At Resonance 3, the target takes an additional 3d10 Shatter damage and has its resistances reduced by one tier for 1 turn. You must, once per day, break something valuable in public. Failure results in you being unable to deal damage for the first 2 rounds of each combat for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (6 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash a 60 ft cone of sonic force. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Shatter damage, be [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Deafened]] for 2 turns, and lose concentration. Those who succeed take half damage. You must, by the end of the pact, destroy at least 20 objects (furniture, walls, equipment). Failure results in you being Deafened at all times for 10 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you scream continuously for 1 turn. All creatures in a 100 ft radius must make a CON save (DC 15) at the start of each of their turns or take 6d10 Shatter damage. Structures and objects in the area are destroyed at the end of the turn. You must, by the end of the pact, incite a riot or mob that causes property damage. Failure results in you being unable to speak or cast spells with verbal components for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Screaming Hymn Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you release a 200 ft cone of pure sound. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 16) or take 12d10 Shatter damage, be [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Deafened]] for 5 turns, and have any concentration broken. Structures are destroyed. You must, by the end of the pact, bring down a major structure (castle, temple, fortress) with your voice. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Weeping Star
*Sorrow, Neutral Alignment*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Sorrow and Psychic damage. Creatures within 20 ft of you have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You must, today, make someone cry or feel deep sadness. Failure results in you being unable to take Actions for 1 turn each hour for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you release a 60 ft cone of sorrow. Creatures must make a WIS save (DC 13) or take 4d10 Sorrow damage and be [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Stunned]] with grief for 1 turn. You must, once per day, dwell on a personal loss or failure for at least 10 minutes. Failure results in you being Stunned for 1 turn at the start of each combat for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** Whenever a creature dies within 300 ft of you, you regain 20 Mana and 50 Vitality. You can feel each death distinctly and know the direction. You must, by the end of the pact, attend at least 3 funerals or deathbeds. Failure results in you being unable to regain Mana for 10 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you sing a song of loss. All non-deity creatures in a 100 ft radius must make a CON save (DC 15) or be reduced to 1 HP and Incapacitated for 1 turn. Those who succeed take 6d10 Sorrow damage. You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 10 people convert to a grief cult or mourning practice. Failure results in you being reduced to 1 HP at the start of each day for 15 days (this cannot kill you but leaves you vulnerable).

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Weeping Star Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you flood a 200 ft radius with magically conjured salt water (difficult terrain). Creatures in the water take 4d10 Sorrow damage at the start of their turns. You may, as a bonus action, summon the spirits of the dead within 1 mile to fight for you (each has 50 HP and deals 2d10 Sorrow damage). You must, by the end of the pact, cause at least 50 deaths through grief, despair, or your direct actions. Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.

## Iron Pit
*Infernal, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Infernal damage. Creatures within 30 ft that attempt to attack you must make a WIS save (DC 12) or lose their action (frozen in fear). This does not work on creatures with Godslayer levels. You must, once per day, give a command to someone weaker and expect obedience. Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Frightened]] of the next creature that challenges you for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (6 days):** You gain 50 temporary armor at the start of each combat. While you have any armor remaining, all damage you take is reduced by 25%. You must, once per day, punish someone who disobeyed you or defied a rule. Failure results in your armor being 0 and you taking double damage for 6 days.

**Tier 3 (10 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you may bind one creature within 60 ft in infernal chains. It must make a STR save (DC 14) or be [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Restrained]], taking 4d10 Infernal damage each turn until it escapes (DC 15 STR check as an action). You must, by the end of the pact, establish a hierarchy where at least 3 people answer to you. Failure results in you being Restrained at the start of each combat for 12 days.

**Tier 4 (16 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 60 ft radius Punishment Zone centered on yourself for 1 minute. You designate one action type per turn (as a free action). Creatures inside that take that action take 6d10 Infernal damage to their Health and lose the action. You must, by the end of the pact, have at least 20 creatures publicly submit to your authority. Failure results in you being unable to take Actions of your choice (GM picks) for 20 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Iron Pit Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you open a 30 ft radius portal to the Infernal Realm within 120 ft that lasts 1 minute. Creatures within 50 ft must make a STR save (DC 16) or be pulled toward it. Creatures pulled in are sent to Hell and must find their own way back. You must, by the end of the pact, send at least 10 creatures to Hell through your portal. Failure results in permanent death and your soul being claimed by Iron Pit.

## Black Mirror
*Obsidian*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Obsidian damage. Any spell or projectile that targets you has a 10% chance to reflect back at the caster. You must, today, keep a secret that would benefit you to reveal. Failure results in you being unable to hide or use Stealth for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you fire a razor-sharp obsidian shard at a target within 120 ft. The target takes 4d10 Obsidian damage and must make a CON save (DC 13) or bleed for 2d10 damage at the start of its turns for 2 turns. You must, once per day, destroy a record or document that contains information about someone. Failure results in you bleeding for 2d10 damage at the start of each combat for 5 days.

Failure results in you being unable to reform (permanent death on reaching 0 HP) for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 60 ft radius dome of reflective obsidian for 3 turns. Inside, all creatures see distorted reflections and must make a WIS save (DC 14) at the start of their turns or be Confused for that turn. You are immune. You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 5 people forget something important or believe a false memory. Failure results in you being Confused at the start of each combat for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Black Mirror Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may permanently erase one creature's name from all records, including the memories of everyone who knows it. The creature becomes a nameless entity that cannot form pacts, receive blessings, or be targeted by name-based effects (WIS save to resist). You must, by the end of the pact, delete at least 3 major historical records or important pieces of knowledge from the world. Failure results in your own name being erased from existence — no one remembers you and you cannot be revived.

## Eternal Stain
*[[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Corrupt]]ion, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Corrupt]]ion damage. Once per day, as a bonus action, you may apply Corrupt (1 stack) to a creature within 60 ft (no save). You must, once per day, convince someone to do something slightly wrong (tell a small lie, break a minor rule). Failure results in you being Corrupted (1 stack) for 5 days.

**Tier 2 (6 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It must make a WIS save (DC 13) or become Diplomatic toward you for 1 minute. While Diplomatic, it cannot attack you, considers you a trusted ally, and will follow reasonable suggestions. Damage breaks this effect. You must, before the end of the pact, corrupt at least one institution or organization (guild, temple, government office). Failure results in all social checks against you having disadvantage for 8 days.

**Tier 3 (10 days):** At the start of each of your turns, all creatures within 40 ft gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Corrupt]] (1 stack, no save). At Corrupt 5, the creature becomes fully corrupted and follows your commands for 1 minute (WIS save to resist each command). You must, by the end of the pact, convert at least 5 people to serve you or your cause through corruption. Failure results in you being Corrupted (3 stacks) permanently and unable to be cleansed for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (16 days):** All structures, magical barriers, and constructs within 120 ft of you are weakened. Magical barriers lose 25% HP. Non-magical structures take damage each hour. You may, as an action, cause a 60 ft radius area to rot: creatures take 4d10 [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Corrupt]]ion damage and magical items must make a CON save or lose one enchantment. You must, by the end of the pact, bring down a major institution (a government, a church, a trade guild). Failure results in all your equipment becoming nonmagical for 30 days and you being Corrupted (5 stacks).

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Eternal Stain Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, choose one creature or object within 200 ft. It ages 1,000 years. Creatures age to dust (instant death unless immortal, no save). Objects crumble. You may use this against a deity to age them 100 years (reducing their Divine Level by 1 permanently). You must, by the end of the pact, corrupt an entire settlement or region. Failure results in permanent death and your soul being consumed by Eternal Stain.

## Breath of Ruin
*Miasma, Evil Alignment*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Miasma damage. Creatures within 30 ft of you have disadvantage on all attack rolls. This is not a magical effect and cannot be dispelled. You must, today, convince someone to give up on a goal or hope. Failure results in you having disadvantage on all attack rolls for 4 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Creatures within 40 ft of you gain 1 stack of Exhaustion at the start of their turns (no save). At 3 Exhaustion, the creature is [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slowed]]. Exhaustion from this source cannot be removed while within 40 ft. You must, once per day, cause someone to fail at something important. Failure results in you gaining Exhaustion (2 stacks) that cannot be removed for 6 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash an 80 ft cone of soul-draining miasma. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Miasma damage and lose all temporary buffs, which you gain as healing. You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 10 people abandon their homes, jobs, or communities. Failure results in you being unable to benefit from any buffs or positive effects for 12 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 120 ft radius Field of Failure for 1 minute. Creatures in the area must roll all d20s twice and take the lower result. This bypasses advantage (roll three times, take lowest). You must, by the end of the pact, cause a community to collapse (economic, social, or structural). Failure results in you rolling all d20s with disadvantage for 20 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Breath of Ruin Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you emit a 200 ft radius wave of pure despair. All non-deity creatures must make a WIS save (DC 16) or be affected by [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Despair]] that cannot be removed for 24 hours. While Despairing, they cannot take any voluntary action. Those who succeed still take 8d10 Psychic damage. You must, by the end of the pact, reduce a region to hopelessness (no recovery efforts, no new construction, no births). Failure results in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
