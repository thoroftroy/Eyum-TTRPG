Gods gain power from their subjects. They typically want people to spread their name and gain good reputation. Gods punishments are usually not very severe.

## Ignivar
*Fire/Heat*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Fire damage. As a bonus action, you may wreathe your weapon or fists in flame until the end of your turn, dealing +1d10 Fire damage on hit. You must, today, start a fight or publicly challenge someone. Failure results in you being unable to take the Attack action for 1 day (you can still cast spells and use abilities).

**Tier 2 (2 days):** Once per turn when you take damage, you build 1 Heat Charge (max 5). Your next attack deals +2d10 Fire damage per Charge spent. You must, once per day, destroy something of value (at least 10 Gold worth). Failure results in On Fire (3 stacks) at the start of each day for 2 days.

**Tier 3 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash a 60 ft line of fire (10 ft wide). Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Fire damage and gain On Fire (3 stacks). Those who succeed take half damage. You must, once per day, make an attack that puts yourself in danger (provoke an opportunity attack, attack from unstable ground, etc.). Failure results in losing your bonus action each turn for 4 days.

**Tier 4 (8 days):** Once per long rest, you may enter Overdrive for 1 minute. While Overdrive, you take two turns on your first round of combat, your melee attacks deal double damage, and creatures within 15 ft of you take 2d10 Fire damage at the start of your turns. You must personally defeat (reduce to 0 HP) at least 3 hostile creatures during the pact period. Failure results in Exhaustion (3 stacks) that cannot be removed for 8 days.

**Tier 5 (20 days):** You gain all Ignivar Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may create a 40 ft radius Nova Pulse centered on yourself. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 16) or take 10d10 Fire damage and be Stunned for 1 turn. Those who succeed take half damage. You are immune to this effect. You must, before the pact ends, lead a charge into battle or be the first to engage in at least 3 separate combats. Failure results in permanent Greater Weakness to Fire and Cold damage.

## Aegirion
*Water*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Water damage. The weight of water aids you: all creatures within 30 ft treat each foot of movement as 2 ft (cumulative with difficult terrain). You are immune to this effect. You must, once per day, sign or write a binding agreement about something (a promise, a plan, a trade). Failure results in your speed being halved for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may call a wave that sweeps a 30 ft cone. Creatures must make a STR save (DC 13) or be pushed 20 ft away from you and take 3d10 Water damage. You must, once per day, wait at least 10 minutes before making a decision in a stressful situation. Failure results in you being unable to take the Dash or Disengage actions for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** At the start of each of your turns, you regain Vitality equal to your level if you are standing in or adjacent to water. If you are not near water, you regain half that amount once per hour out of combat. You must, by the end of the pact, construct or repair something that will last (a wall, a contract framework, a relationship). Failure results in you being unable to regain Vitality from any source for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 40 ft radius Pressure Zone centered on yourself for 1 minute. Creatures inside must make a CON save (DC 15) at the start of their turns or take 4d10 Bludgeoning damage and be Restrained for that turn. You must, once per day, convince someone to change their mind through calm discussion (not intimidation). Failure results in you gaining the Restrained condition at the start of each combat for 12 days until you take damage.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Aegirion Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may flood a 100 ft radius with waist-deep water for 1 hour. While in the water, you may teleport to any point within it as a bonus action, and non-allied creatures have their speed halved. You must, by the end of the pact, establish a lasting agreement between two hostile parties. Failure results in permanent half movement speed in all terrain.

## Eldathis
*Radiant*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Radiant damage. Once per turn when you deal damage to a creature that has lied to you or broken a promise, you deal +2d10 Radiant damage. You must, once per day, speak a hard truth that costs you something (a relationship, a reputation, an opportunity). Failure results in the Cursed condition for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may detect lies within 30 ft for 10 minutes. Any creature that speaks a deliberate lie while you are concentrating glows faintly and you know it is lying. You must, once per day, judge someone publicly (call out a wrongdoing, report a crime, or enforce a rule). Failure results in you being unable to speak anything but the truth for 4 days — any attempt to lie deals 2d10 Psychic damage to you.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you may fire a 120 ft line of Radiant light (10 ft wide). Creatures must make a WIS save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Radiant damage. Creatures that are Cursed, Withered, or Corrupted take double damage. You must, once per day, make a public atonement for a wrong you have done (apologize, return stolen goods, confess). Failure results in the Cursed condition and disadvantage on all CHA checks for 8 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, you may activate Aura of Judgment for 1 minute. Creatures within 30 ft that tell a lie, break an oath, or act against their stated alignment must make a WIS save (DC 14) or take 5d10 Radiant damage and gain the Cursed condition for 24 hours. You must, by the end of the pact, successfully resolve a moral conflict where you choose the harder right over the easier wrong. Failure results in you being Cursed permanently and unable to benefit from any divine blessing for 14 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Eldathis Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may declare Final Judgment on one creature you can see within 120 ft. It must make a WIS save (DC 16). On a failure, it takes 15d10 Radiant damage and is Stunned for 1 turn. On a success, it takes half damage. If the creature is below half Health, the damage is doubled. You must, by the end of the pact, convert at least 10 people to follow a moral code or law you uphold. Failure results in permanent Despair and you cannot be healed by Radiant or Holy effects.

## Cryovara
*Ice/Cold*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Cold damage. Creatures within 15 ft of you take 1d10 Cold damage at the start of their turns. You must, once per day, endure the cold for 10 minutes without shelter (or if cold is not available, endure some other discomfort like a cold bath). Failure results in the Frozen (2 stacks) condition at the start of each day for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** You gain 20 temporary armor at the start of each combat. This armor absorbs damage before your Vitality. You must, once per day, go without food for a full meal (skip one meal). Failure results in your armor being permanently reduced by 5 for 4 days (minimum 0).

**Tier 3 (7 days):** The ground within 20 ft of you becomes slippery ice. Non-allied creatures must make a DEX save (DC 13) at the start of their turns or fall Prone. You move across this ice at full speed. You must, once per day, prepare for a future threat (stock supplies, reinforce shelter, plan an escape route). Failure results in you being knocked Prone at the start of each combat for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you unleash a 60 ft cone of freezing wind. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 15) or take 6d10 Cold damage and gain Frozen (4 stacks). Those who succeed take half damage and gain Frozen (2 stacks). You must, once per day, complete a task that requires patience (wait in line for 30+ minutes, perform a repetitive chore for an hour, etc.). Failure results in Frozen (5 stacks) at the start of each day for 12 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Cryovara Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may call a Glacier Fall from the sky at a point within 120 ft (30 ft radius). Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 16) or take 10d10 Cold damage and be buried under ice (Restrained). Those who succeed take half damage. You must, by the end of the pact, survive a situation where you were severely disadvantaged (environmental hazard, numerical disadvantage, etc.). Failure results in permanent half Vitality maximum and Greater Weakness to Fire.

## Indravajra
*Lightning*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Lightning damage. Once per round, when a creature within 30 ft takes damage, you may deal 1d10 Lightning damage to it (no action required). You must, once per day, assert your dominance in a social situation (interrupt someone, issue a command, take control of a conversation). Failure results in you being unable to speak above a whisper for 1 day.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may call down a lightning strike on a point within 60 ft (10 ft radius). Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 13) or take 4d10 Lightning damage and be Stunned until the end of their next turn. You must, once per day, win a contest of strength, will, or skill against someone. Failure results in you being Stunned for 1 turn at the start of each combat for 3 days.

**Tier 3 (5 days):** Creatures that hit you with a melee attack take 3d10 Lightning damage and must make a CON save (DC 14) or be Stunned until the end of their next turn. You must, once per day, publicly challenge someone who disrespects you. Failure results in you being unable to take Reactions for 5 days.

**Tier 4 (10 days):** Once per long rest, you may take to the air for 1 minute. You gain a fly speed equal to your walking speed, your ranged attacks deal +2d10 Lightning damage, and you cannot be targeted by melee attacks unless the attacker can also fly. You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 3 creatures publicly submit to your authority or fear you. Failure results in you being Grounded (cannot fly or levitate) for 10 days.

**Tier 5 (22 days):** You gain all Indravajra Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you create a 100 ft radius storm overhead for 3 turns. At the start of each turn, lightning strikes all enemies of your choice in the area. Each must make a DEX save (DC 16) or take 6d10 Lightning damage and be Stunned for 1 turn. You must, by the end of the pact, defeat a powerful opponent in single combat or public contest. Failure results in permanent disadvantage on all CHA checks and Greater Weakness to Lightning.

## Seraphael
*Hallowed*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Hallowed damage. As a bonus action, you may cleanse yourself or an adjacent ally of one condition (Poisoned, Cursed, Withered, or Blinded). You may do this once per short rest. You must, once per day, help someone who cannot repay you. Failure results in you being Poisoned for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you may fire a 120 ft line of purifying light (10 ft wide). Creatures of the Evil Alignment take 4d10 Hallowed damage. Creatures with Withered, Corrupted, or Cursed take double damage. You must, once per day, refuse to let corruption go unchallenged (call out a lie, stop a theft, report a crime). Failure results in you gaining the Cursed condition whenever you enter combat for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** You and allies within 30 ft are immune to Poison, Blight, and Cursed damage. Creatures of Evil alignment within this aura take 2d10 Hallowed damage at the start of their turns. You must, by the end of the pact, purify something (cleanse a cursed object, heal a diseased person, remove a corrupt influence). Failure results in you being unable to enter sacred or consecrated ground for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, you may create a 60 ft radius Sanctuary for 1 minute. Creatures of your choice cannot enter. Evil creatures that attempt to enter take 6d10 Hallowed damage. Allies inside heal 1d10 Vitality at the start of their turns. You must, by the end of the pact, protect someone who could not protect themselves for at least 3 days. Failure results in all your healing being halved for 12 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Seraphael Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per turn as an Action you may purge evil. Choose a targeted creature of the Evil alignment, they take 100 Hallowed damage. There is no save. You must, by the end of the pact, convert at least one corrupted creature or location back to purity. Failure results in permanent inability to benefit from healing magic.

## Pelagion
*Torrent*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain Resistance to Torrent damage. You may make a melee attack as a bonus action once per turn. You must, today, attack first in a conflict (be the one to strike the first blow). Failure results in you being Slowed for 1 day.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** When you deal damage to a creature below half Vitality, you deal +2d10 Torrent damage. You must, once per day, pursue a fleeing enemy or refuse to let a wounded opponent escape. Failure results in you being unable to deal extra damage of any kind for 3 days.

**Tier 3 (5 days):** Your melee reach increases by 10 ft. When you hit a creature, you may attempt to grapple it as a free action (no action required). You must, once per day, hunt and kill something (a monster, a challenging animal, or a hostile NPC). Failure results in your reach being reduced by 5 ft (minimum 5 ft) for 5 days.

**Tier 4 (10 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you make 3 melee attacks against a single target. Each hit deals 4d10 Torrent damage. If all 3 hit the same target, it takes an additional 6d10 Torrent damage. You must, by the end of the pact, kill at least 5 hostile creatures without showing mercy (no accepting surrender). Failure results in you being unable to take the Attack action for the first round of each combat for 10 days.

**Tier 5 (20 days):** You gain all Pelagion Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, you may enter a Feeding Frenzy for 1 minute. You gain Haste (double speed, +2 AC, one additional attack per action) and all your attacks heal you for 25% of damage dealt. You must, by the end of the pact, lead or participate in a raid, invasion, or major assault. Failure results in permanent half damage against all creatures.

## Keraunos
*Thunder*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Thunder and Lightning damage. Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may speak with a voice that carries. All creatures within 60 ft that can hear you must make a WIS save (DC 12) or be Frightened of you until the end of your next turn. You must, once per day, give a command to someone and expect it to be followed. Failure results in you being unable to speak for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you call lightning on a point within 120 ft (20 ft radius). Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 14) or take 4d10 Lightning damage and be Deafened for 2 turns. Those who succeed take half damage. You must, once per day, demonstrate your power in a visible way (break something, use a loud spell, cause a scene). Failure results in Deafened at the start of each combat for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** You control the water within 60 ft. As a bonus action, you may part shallow water, create a 20 ft wall of water, or make a whirlpool that Restrains one creature (STR save to escape). You must, by the end of the pact, make at least 3 creatures submit to your will through fear. Failure results in you being unable to swim and treating all water as difficult terrain for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 100 ft radius storm for 3 turns. At the start of each turn, enemies in the area take 4d10 Thunder damage and 4d10 Lightning damage. You must, by the end of the pact, cause a visible change in the weather or environment through your actions. Failure results in you taking 2d10 Thunder damage at the start of each of your turns in combat for 12 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Keraunos Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may call a Tidal Wave that surges forward 200 ft (60 ft wide, 30 ft tall). Creatures must make a STR save (DC 16) or take 10d10 Bludgeoning damage and be swept to the wave's endpoint. You must, by the end of the pact, conquer or control a coastal area or body of water. Failure results in permanent Greater Weakness to Thunder and Lightning.

## Hephaestion
*Metal*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Metal damage and +2 AC. As a bonus action, you may repair a metal object you touch, restoring up to 20 HP of damage to it. You must, once per day, create or repair something with your hands (even a simple tool). Failure results in your AC being reduced by 2 for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Your melee attacks ignore 5 points of damage reduction or armor. Once per turn when you hit, you may reduce the target's AC by 1 until the end of combat (stacks up to -5). You must, once per day, inspect something closely for flaws and report them honestly. Failure results in your attacks dealing half damage for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You gain DR 10 against all physical damage. Your body is treated as one size category larger for the purpose of resisting grapple and forced movement. You must, by the end of the pact, forge or craft a high-quality item (GM decides quality). Failure results in your DR being removed and you taking double damage from physical attacks for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a divine metal cage around a target within 60 ft. The cage has 500 HP and AC 30. The target is trapped until the cage is destroyed or you release it. You must, by the end of the pact, teach someone a craft or skill that will outlast you. Failure results in you being trapped (Restrained) at the start of each combat for 14 days until you take 50 damage to break free.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Hephaestion Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may reshape the terrain within 120 ft. Raise walls, open ditches, or smooth ground. Creatures caught in the reshaping take 8d10 Metal damage. You must, by the end of the pact, create a permanent structure that will survive for at least 10 years. Failure results in permanent -5 AC and all your equipment becoming brittle (breaks on a natural 1 attack roll).

## Aeolus
*Storm*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Storm/Wind damage. Your speed increases by 10 ft. You cannot be grappled or restrained by physical means that rely on contact (webs, nets, etc. still work). You must, once per day, spend at least 10 minutes in a high place or open area feeling the wind. Failure results in your speed being reduced by 10 ft for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you unleash a 60 ft cone of wind. Creatures must make a STR save (DC 13) or be pushed 60 ft away from you and take 3d10 Wind damage. Creatures pushed into obstacles take an additional 2d10 Bludgeoning damage. You must, once per day, travel to a new place you have never been before. Failure results in you being unable to move more than 30 ft from your starting position each day for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** You can feel every air current within 300 ft. You know the exact position of all creatures in that range that are not magically hidden. You cannot be surprised. You must, by the end of the pact, free something or someone that was confined or imprisoned. Failure results in you being Blinded to hidden creatures and always surprised for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 40 ft radius Cyclone at a point within 120 ft for 3 turns. Creatures inside must make a STR save (DC 15) each turn or be lifted 40 ft into the air and take 4d10 Slashing damage. You must, once per day, refuse an offer of permanent residence, binding contract, or long-term commitment. Failure results in you being unable to fly or levitate and your jump height being halved for 12 days.

**Tier 5 (25 days):** You gain all Aeolus Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may release a 200 ft cone of wind that transports non-allied creatures to a random location within 1 mile (no save for creatures smaller than Large; Large+ get a STR save). You must, by the end of the pact, travel to at least 3 different settlements or regions and leave a mark on each. Failure results in permanent Grounded condition and half speed.

## Rimeflame
*Frostfire*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to both Cold and Fire damage. Once per turn when you deal Cold or Fire damage, you may treat the target's resistance as one tier lower (if immune, treat as resistant). You must, once per day, expose yourself to extreme temperature (very hot or very cold). Failure results in you gaining either On Fire (2 stacks) or Frozen (2 stacks) each day for 2 days (your choice each day).

**Tier 2 (4 days):** When you deal Cold damage to a creature that is On Fire, you deal an additional 3d10 Frostfire damage and the target is Slowed until the end of its next turn. You must, once per day, switch between two extreme activities (hot to cold, fast to slow, loud to quiet). Failure results in your damage being halved against creatures that have any condition for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you fire a 60 ft line of frostfire (15 ft wide). Creatures must make a CON save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Frostfire damage and gain both Frozen (2 stacks) and On Fire (2 stacks). Those who succeed take half damage. You must, once per day, endure something that tests your endurance for 10 minutes (run in place, hold a heavy object, hold your breath). Failure results in you starting each combat with both Frozen (2 stacks) and On Fire (2 stacks) for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (10 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create a 40 ft radius Thermal Shockwave. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 15) or take 8d10 Frostfire damage. Their armor and weapons are degraded (-1 to AC or damage until repaired). You must, by the end of the pact, survive in an extreme environment for at least 24 consecutive hours. Failure results in permanent -2 AC until you complete a quest for Rimeflame.

**Tier 5 (22 days):** You gain all Rimeflame Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per turn when you deal Cold damage to a creature that is On Fire, it takes an additional 6d10 Frostfire damage and is Stunned for 1 turn. You must, by the end of the pact, convert at least 3 creatures to a lifestyle of endurance and thrill-seeking. Failure results in permanent Greater Weakness to both Cold and Fire.

## Cryolith
*Glacial*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Glacial/Cold damage. You gain 20 temporary armor at the start of each day. You must, once per day, seal or lock something away (a container, a door, a memory). Failure results in your armor being 0 for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** Creatures within 20 ft of you have their speed reduced by 10 ft. You are immune to this effect and move at full speed on ice or frozen ground. You must, once per day, refuse to take a risk (choose the safe option when a risky one is available). Failure results in your speed being halved and you being unable to take the Dash action for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you fire a 60 ft cone of glacial shards. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 14) or take 6d10 Glacial damage and gain Frozen (4 stacks). You must, by the end of the pact, successfully contain a threat (lock up a monster, seal a leak, imprison a criminal). Failure results in Frozen (5 stacks) at the start of each combat that cannot be reduced for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** You gain 100 additional armor that regenerates at the start of each turn. While you have any armor, you cannot be moved by any force. You must, by the end of the pact, maintain a secret or keep a location hidden for the entire duration. Failure results in you being unable to gain armor from any source for 14 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Cryolith Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may encase one creature within 60 ft in a block of ice. The ice has 800 HP and AC 30. The creature is Incapacitated until the ice is destroyed. You must, by the end of the pact, permanently seal away a significant threat (GM decides). Failure results in permanent Frozen condition (cannot be removed) until you serve Cryolith for 1 year.

## Gelidra
*Gel*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Gel damage. You can squeeze through openings as small as 6 inches wide (takes 1 extra movement per 5 ft). You must, once per day, bind or tie something securely (a rope, a package, a prisoner). Failure results in you being Restrained for 1 hour each day for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you splash a 30 ft cone of sticky gel. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 13) or be Restrained for 2 turns. Restrained creatures take 3d10 Gel damage at the start of their turns. You must, once per day, successfully capture or restrain a creature (any size). Failure results in you being Restrained at the start of each combat until you take damage for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Creatures that hit you with a melee attack take 3d10 Gel damage and have their weapon or armor degraded (-1 to damage or AC until repaired). You take half damage from bludgeoning and slashing attacks. You must, by the end of the pact, capture a creature alive instead of killing it. Failure results in you taking double damage from bludgeoning and slashing for 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** As an action, you may collapse into a pool of gel and reform at any point within 60 ft. When you reform, creatures adjacent to you must make a DEX save (DC 14) or be Restrained. You may do this once per short rest. You must, once per day, use nonlethal means to subdue a threat. Failure results in you being unable to move more than 15 ft from your current position for 12 days.

**Tier 5 (24 days):** You gain all Gelidra Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may create a 40 ft radius pool of adhesive gel for 1 minute. Creatures inside are Slowed and must make a STR save (DC 15) each turn or be Restrained. Restrained creatures take 5d10 Gel damage at the start of their turns. You must, by the end of the pact, bind or capture at least 20 creatures. Failure results in permanent half movement speed and you cannot use teleportation effects.

## Mnemosyne
*Psychic*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Psychic damage. Once per turn, when you damage a creature you have seen use an ability before, you may deal +1d10 Psychic damage and learn one piece of information about it (its current HP, a resistance, a condition immunity). You must, once per day, write down something you learned that day (keep a journal or record). Failure results in you forgetting the last 24 hours (amnesia for that period) for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** As a bonus action, you may touch a creature or object and learn its recent history (last 24 hours). You may do this once per short rest. You must, once per day, recite from memory at least 3 facts about someone you spoke to. Failure results in you being unable to form new memories for 5 days (you function but remember nothing new).

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You have advantage on attacks against creatures you have fought before. You cannot be tricked by the same illusion or deception twice. You must, by the end of the pact, learn the names of at least 10 new people and remember them. Failure results in you forgetting the names of all allies and being unable to identify friend from foe for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you fire a 120 ft line of psychic memory (10 ft wide). Creatures must make a WIS save (DC 15) or take 6d10 Psychic damage and forget the last 10 minutes (lose memory of the current combat). You must, by the end of the pact, uncover a secret that someone has kept for at least 1 year. Failure results in you losing all memory of the past month and having to relearn it.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Mnemosyne Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may flood a 100 ft radius with ancestral memories. All non-allied creatures must make a WIS save (DC 16) or be overwhelmed, becoming Stunned for 1d4 turns. You must, by the end of the pact, preserve an important piece of knowledge or history that would otherwise be lost. Failure results in permanent memory loss — you lose all knowledge of one skill or language (your choice).

## Hemalux
*Bloodfire*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Bloodfire damage. When you deal damage to a creature, you heal for 1d10 Vitality. This healing cannot exceed the damage dealt. You must, once per day, swear an oath to protect someone or something. Failure results in you being unable to regain Vitality for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you may swear an Oath of Protection on one ally within 30 ft. For 1 minute, while you are within 30 ft of that ally, any damage that would hit them instead hits you. You gain +2 AC while protecting them. You must, once per day, fulfill your sworn oath in a tangible way. Failure results in you being unable to protect anyone (all damage protection abilities fail) for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** When you are below half Vitality, your melee attacks deal double damage. When below quarter Vitality, they deal triple damage and you may make one additional attack per turn. You must, once per day, put yourself in harm's way to protect someone else. Failure results in your damage being halved while below half Vitality for 8 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per round, when an allied creature within 30 ft would take damage, you may intercept that damage, taking it yourself instead. You gain +3 AC for 1 turn after doing so. You must, by the end of the pact, successfully protect someone from a genuine threat (not a staged one). Failure results in you being unable to take damage for allies (all interception abilities fail) for 14 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Hemalux Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, as an action, you may sacrifice half your current Vitality to grant all allied creatures within 120 ft immunity to all damage and conditions for 2 turns. You must, by the end of the pact, die for a cause or come within 1 HP of death while protecting someone. Failure results in permanent cowardice — you cannot willingly take damage for another creature ever again.
