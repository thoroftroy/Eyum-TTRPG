> Deities have two forms: their celestial body and their humanoid form. This section covers the humanoid form used for combat. Killing the humanoid form causes the deity to slowly regenerate it over approximately 10 years. Celestial bodies cannot be destroyed by any known means.

> For information on how players can damage deities, see the Godslayer skill tree in [[3.3 Leveling Up]].
>
> **Deity Attack Damage Bonus:** When a deity ability lists damage as `XdY + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for [Type]`, look up the deity's affinity on the Attack Damage Bonus table in [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus]]. For example, Darwin has Earth (+5,000); his Attack Damage Bonus for Earth is +71.
## Universal Deity Passives
Every deity's manifested combat form possesses the following abilities:

- **Divine Form (Passive)**: The deity is immune to all damage, conditions, forced movement, magical effects, and any negative effect from creatures that have not taken at least 1 level in the Godslayer skill tree. Against Godslayers, damage is calculated according to the Godslayer's level:
  - Godslayer Level 1: Deity has **Greater Resistance** to all damage
  - Godslayer Level 3: Deity has **Resistance** to all damage
  - Godslayer Level 5: Deity has **Lesser Resistance** to all damage
  - Godslayer Level 7: Deity takes **full damage**
  - Godslayer Level 10: Deity takes **double damage** from the Godslayer

> These resistance levels stack with the deity's own resistances and immunities. Divine Form immunity bypasses any "bypass immunity" effects; only the Godslayer tree can overcome it.

- **Celestial Anchor (Passive)**: The manifested form is a projection. If it is reduced to 0 Health, the humanoid form is destroyed. The deity slowly regenerates a new humanoid form over approximately 10 years. During this time the deity cannot manifest, grant pacts, or respond to prayers.
- **Worship Well (Passive)**: At the start of each of its turns, the deity regains Vitality equal to its Level x 10. If the deity has zero active worshippers this regeneration stops.
- **Divine Sense (Passive)**: The deity is aware of any creature within 1,000 miles per Level that speaks its name, prays to it, or makes a pact. It knows their exact location, current Vitality and Health, and general intent. When the creatures specifically pray to the deity it acts as Telepathy and the deity is able to hear the entire prayer. 
- **Divine Immunity (Passive)**: The deity is immune to all conditions from any source besides creatures with Godslayer levels. It cannot be stunned, paralyzed, petrified, knocked prone, restrained, grappled, banished, or have its stats reduced unless the effect is from a creature with Godslayer levels. It is immune to instant death effects.

**DR:** 1,000
**AC:** 55
**Speed:** 60
**Affinity:** Hallowed (+500)
**Gen Dmg Mod:** +500
**Resistance:** Blight, Necrotic, Corruption
**Weakness:** Necrotic, Corruption
**Vitality:** 8,000d6 (527,500)
**Health:** 8,000d8 (36,000)
**Mana:** 10,000d6 (534,500)
**Stats (STR/CON/DEX/WIS/INT/CHA):** 10,000/10,000/5,000/10,000/10,000/10,000

**Unique Passives:**
- **Cleansing Light (Passive)**: At the start of each of his turns, Seraphael removes all conditions and negative effects from himself and all allied creatures within 120 ft. This includes Withered, Corrupted, Cursed, and Poisoned.
- **Ward of Purity (Passive)**: Seraphael and allies within 60 ft are immune to Blight, Necrotic, Curse, Poison, and Miasma damage. Creatures of evil alignment within this area take 5d10 + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for Hallowed damage at the start of their turns.
- **Unbreakable Vow (Passive)**: Once per day, when Seraphael would be reduced to 0 Health, he instead is restored to full Health and Vitality. This represents his divine vow to protect the living.

**Unique Actives:**
- **Purifying Beam (Active)**: (8 Mana) 200 ft line, 10 ft wide. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Save DC Bonus) or take 15d10 + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for Hallowed damage. Creatures with Withered, Corrupted, or Cursed take double damage.
- **Sanctuary (Active)**: (12 Mana) 100 ft radius for 1 minute. Creatures of Seraphael's choice cannot enter this area. Evil creatures take 8d10 + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for Hallowed damage if they attempt to enter. Allies inside heal 2d10 Vitality at the start of their turns.
- **Divine Cleansing (Active)**: (20 Mana) 300 ft radius. All corruption, disease, poison, and negative magical effects in the area are cleansed. Evil creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Save DC Bonus) or take 20d10 + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for Hallowed damage and be Blinded for 3 turns.

### Hemalux / Mars
**Level:** 100
**DR:** 1,000
**AC:** 55
**Speed:** 80
**Affinity:** Bloodfire (+500)
**Gen Dmg Mod:** +500
**Resistance:** Torrent, Water, Force
**Weakness:** Water, Cold
**Vitality:** 10,000d6 (784,500)
**Health:** 8,000d8 (36,000)
**Mana:** 8,000d6 (277,500)
**Stats (STR/CON/DEX/WIS/INT/CHA):** 15,000/15,000/10,000/5,000/5,000/10,000

**Unique Passives:**
- **Bloodied Fury (Passive)**: When Hemalux is below 50% Vitality, his damage is doubled. When below 25%, his damage is tripled and he makes an additional attack each turn.
- **Oathbound (Passive)**: Hemalux cannot be forced to break an oath or abandon a sworn charge. He automatically passes all WIS saves related to loyalty, fear, or intimidation.
- **Sacrificial Guardian (Passive)**: Once per round, when an allied creature within 30 ft of Hemalux would take damage, he may intercept that damage, taking it himself instead. He gains +5 AC for 1 turn after doing so.

**Unique Actives:**
- **Blood Strike (Active)**: (8 Mana) Hemalux makes a melee attack that deals 15d10 + the deity's Attack Damage Bonus for Bloodfire damage. He heals for half the damage dealt. If the target is below 50% HP, the damage is doubled.
- **Oath of Protection (Active)**: (12 Mana) Hemalux chooses one allied creature within 60 ft. For 1 minute, that creature cannot take damage while Hemalux is alive and within 30 ft of it. All damage that would hit the protected creature instead hits Hemalux.
- **Martyr's End (Active)**: (30 Mana) Hemalux reduces himself to 1 Vitality. For the next 3 turns, all allied creatures within 300 ft have immunity to all damage, conditions, and negative effects. At the end of the 3 turns, Hemalux heals for 50% of the damage he prevented during this time.
