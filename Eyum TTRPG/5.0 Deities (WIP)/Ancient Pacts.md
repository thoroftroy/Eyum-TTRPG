## Newton
*Force*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Force damage. Once per round, when a creature within 60 ft damages you, that creature takes 1d10 Force damage at the end of its next turn (equal force and opposite reaction). You must, once per day, measure something precisely (distance, weight, time) and record it. Failure results in you taking 1d10 Force damage at the start of each of your turns in combat for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (6 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 40 ft radius Gravity Well centered on a point within 60 ft. Creatures must make a STR save (DC 13) or be pulled to the center, taking 3d10 Force damage and gaining the [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Restrained]] condition. [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Restrained]] creatures can escape with a STR save (DC 13) as an action. You must, once per day, balance something perfectly (stack coins, balance a broom, stand on one leg for 1 minute). Failure results in you being [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Restrained]] at the start of each combat for 6 days.

**Tier 3 (10 days):** Once per long rest, for 1 minute, when a creature within 120 ft deals damage to anyone, you may redirect that exact amount of damage back at the attacker or at any other creature within range. You must, once per day, repay a debt exactly (no more, no less). Failure results in you taking double damage from all sources for 10 days.

**Tier 4 (16 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you declare one creature or object within 120 ft to be "at rest." That target cannot move, be moved, or take any action for 2 turns. You must, by the end of the pact, keep a promise exactly as worded with no interpretation or loopholes. Failure results in you being unable to move or act for 1 turn at the start of each combat for 16 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Newton Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, all your pacts and binding effects cannot be dispelled or removed by any means. Once per day, when you would take damage, be forcibly moved, or gain a condition, you may spend a Reaction to negate that instance entirely and reflect it onto the source. You must, by the end of the pact, create a system of rules or laws that at least 20 people follow. Failure results in the [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slowed]] condition for 30 days that cannot be removed, and whenever you move you take 2d10 Force damage.

## Darwin
*Earth*

**Tier 1 (4 days):** You gain Resistance to Earth and Poison damage. When you take damage from a type you have not taken this combat, you gain Lesser Resistance to that damage type until your next long rest. You must, once per day, eat something raw and alive (a live insect, worm, or small fish). Failure results in the [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Sickened]] condition for 4 days and you lose all resistances.

**Tier 2 (8 days):** At the start of each of your turns, choose one: gain +2 AC, +10 ft speed, or +5 damage on your next attack. If you are below half Vitality, you may choose two. You must, once per day, bury something in the ground (a seed, a coin, a personal item). Failure results in Vulnerable to Earth damage and -10 ft speed for 8 days.

**Tier 3 (14 days):** As a bonus action, you may assume one of the following adaptations for 1 hour (may switch once per hour): Burrow speed 20 ft; Climb speed 30 ft; or Darkvision 60 ft. While adapted, you have advantage on attacks against creatures that cannot see you. You must, once per day, shed blood into soil (prick your finger and let it drip). Failure results in your body rejecting itself ,  you cannot regain Vitality from rest and healing is halved for 14 days.

**Tier 4 (20 days):** When you would drop to 0 Vitality, you instead drop to 1 and gain 1d20 Vitality. This can occur once per day. You gain Greater Resistance to Earth damage and ignore difficult terrain. You must, once per day, kill a creature with your bare hands or natural weapons and leave its body unburied. Failure results in -5 to STR, CON, and DEX scores for 3 days. If you reach 0 Vitality during this time, you die permanently.

**Tier 5 (35 days):** You gain all Darwin Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day when you take damage, you may evolve (gain Lesser Resistance to that damage type; using it again upgrades to Resistance, then Greater Resistance). You must, before the pact ends, create a new lifeform (breed an animal, cultivate a plant, or cause a birth under your supervision). Failure results in permanent death ,  you are erased from existence and cannot be revived.

## Da Vinci
*Air*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Air damage. You have advantage on one skill check of your choice per day (choose after rolling). You must, once per day, draw or sketch a design for something functional (a tool, a machine, a building). Failure results in you losing the ability to take Reactions for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** As a bonus action, you may gain a 20 ft flying speed until the end of your turn. If you end your turn in the air, you fall normally. You must, once per day, hang upside down for 5 minutes. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Grounded]] for 5 days (cannot fly, teleport, or be moved magically).

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you may create an air construct within 30 ft (wall, platform, cage, or blade) that lasts 10 minutes. It has AC 18 and 40 HP. If used as a weapon, it deals 3d10 Air damage. You must, before the pact ends, build something with moving parts that serves a function. Failure results in -5 to all attack rolls and Sleight of Hand checks for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** Once per long rest, as a Reaction, you may redirect any ranged attack, spell line, or projectile you can see within 60 ft to a new target within 30 ft of its original path. You may do this even if the effect would not normally be redirectable. You must, by the end of the pact, reach a high place Da Vinci directs you to and observe the world from there for 10 minutes. Failure results in the air turning against you ,  failing any check gives [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Suffocating]] for 2 turns for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Da Vinci Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, you gain +1 Action Point and +1 Bonus Action Point for the pact duration. Once per day, as an action, you may make a single attack that deals 12d10 Force damage and ignores all resistances, immunities, and damage reduction. You must, by the end of the pact, create a masterpiece that another creature willingly praises. Failure results in your genius being stolen ,  you cannot benefit from any feats, skill bonuses, or proficiencies for 30 days.

## Newcomen
*Steam*

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain Resistance to Steam, Fire, and Water damage. As a bonus action, you may vent steam: creatures within 5 ft must make a CON save (DC 12) or be pushed 10 ft and take 1d10 Steam damage. You must, today, boil water and inhale the steam for 10 seconds. Failure results in the [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Mute]] condition for 2 days.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** At the start of combat, gain Pressure equal to your level (max 50). Once per turn, you may spend any amount of Pressure to add +2 Steam damage per Pressure to an attack. Pressure resets at end of combat. You must, once per day, burn your hand on a hot surface (take at least 3 damage) and not heal it for 1 hour. Failure results in you taking 2d10 Steam damage at the start of each combat for 4 days.

**Tier 3 (8 days):** You have a 10 ft aura of scalding air. Enemies that start their turn in it take 2d10 Steam damage and have their speed reduced by 10 ft until the end of their turn. You must deal Steam damage at least once per day. Failure results in your speed being halved and taking 3d10 Steam damage whenever you Dash for 8 days.

**Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you rupture your pressure in a 30 ft radius. All creatures (including you) must make a CON save (DC 15). On a fail they take 8d10 Steam damage and are pushed 30 ft away. You take half damage regardless. You must, once per day, sit in steam for at least 30 minutes. Failure results in you taking 1d10 Steam damage to HP at the start of each day (cannot heal below 1 HP) for 14 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Newcomen Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, you may maintain Pressure between combats. You may spend 30 Pressure to gain an additional Action Point for one turn. You must deal at least 500 total Steam damage before the pact ends. Failure results in permanent Greater Weakness to Fire, Water, and Steam and permanent loss of half your maximum Vitality.

## Hubble
*Dust*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Dust damage. You gain Darkvision 60 ft (or extend existing darkvision by 30 ft). You can see through fog, smoke, and dust as if it were not there. You must, each night, spend 10 minutes staring at the sky. Failure results in the [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Blinded]] condition for 3 days.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** As a bonus action, mark one creature you can see within 300 ft. You always know its direction and distance, and your attacks against it ignore cover. The mark lasts 1 hour. You may use this twice per day. You must remain covered in dust at all times ,  if you intentionally clean yourself, you fail the pact. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Grounded]] and -4 to Perception checks for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you create a 20 ft radius Dust Field within 60 ft that lasts 10 minutes. Enemies inside are outlined (cannot benefit from invisibility or stealth). Allies inside are treated as stealthed. You may have one Dust Field active at once. You must kill at least 1 creature using Suffocation damage during the pact. Failure results in you taking 1d10 Dust damage at the start of each turn in combat for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** You may change the damage type of any of your attacks to Dust damage. You double any Dust damage you deal. You must keep one eye covered at all times. While one eye is covered, you have disadvantage on ranged attacks and Perception checks. Failure results in half movement speed and [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Suffocating]] every other turn in combat for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Hubble Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, if you die during the pact period, you may reform your body from dust after 1 hour. Once per day, as an action, you may compress cosmic dust into 1d4 volatile star fragments within 120 ft. Each explodes after 1 turn, dealing 6d10 Dust damage in a 20 ft radius. You must apply the [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Suffocating]] condition to at least 10 people and all must die before the pact ends. Failure results in permanent death ,  to dust you return and cannot be revived.

## Pasteur
*Mud*

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain Resistance to Mud damage and immunity to disease and the [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] condition. When you deal damage, you may choose to infect the target ,  it takes 1d10 Mud damage at the start of its next turn. You must, today, wash your hands exactly once and only once. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Poisoned]] for 3 days and your healing is halved.

**Tier 2 (5 days):** While standing on dirt or mud, you deal an additional 1d8 Mud damage. When you deal Mud damage, you gain half of it back as Vitality. You must, once per day, consume something fermented or spoiled on purpose. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slowed]] and [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Grounded]] for 5 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** As an action, you may sink into mud and move through it. While inside mud, you cannot take other actions and cannot be targeted unless by fire damage. Leaving ends your turn. You must, once per day, take this mud-sinking action or you cannot take short rests for 9 days.

**Tier 4 (15 days):** Once per day, choose a creature within 60 ft. It must make a CON save (DC 14). On a failure, it becomes your Carrier for 1 hour ,  it spreads a 15 ft aura that gives enemies disadvantage on all rolls, and they take 2d10 Mud damage at the start of their turns. You are immune to your own plague. You must, once per day, sleep in wet mud for at least 1 hour. Failure results in you being unable to heal from any source for 15 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Pasteur Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, any Carrier you create deals double Mud damage. When a Carrier deals Mud damage, roll d100 ,  on a 10 or higher, the damaged creature becomes a new Carrier. You must infect at least 400 living creatures with Mud Wrath before the pact ends. Failure results in death.

Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Blurred]] for 2 days.

Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Mental-Conditions|Demoralized]] for 5 days.

You must, by the end of the pact, produce an accurate map of a route you traveled. Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Blinded]] for 9 days.

Your attacks deal +6d10 Nova damage and apply [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Blinded]] (1 stack) and [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Burned]] (1 stack). Failure results in [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Slowed]] permanently during combat and you gain [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Stunned]] (1 stack) after every Action you take for 30 days.

## Lorenz
*Chaos*

  - 4: You regain 1d10 Vitality at the start of each of your turns.

On a 16-20, they are [[2.4 Conditions#Magical-Conditions|Hasted]] for that turn (you decide the effect ranges each time you use this). **Tier 4 (14 days):** Once per long rest, as an action, you create 1d4+1 illusory copies of yourself. Each copy has 100 HP, AC 25, and deals 3d10 Chaos damage with attacks. You must, before the end of the pact, cause a significant unpredictable change in your life or surroundings (quit a job, move homes, end a relationship, start a feud). Failure results in you being unable to take more than one action per turn for 14 days.

**Tier 5 (28 days):** You gain all Lorenz Tier 1-4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day, you may force the entire combat to reroll initiative. All ongoing effects, conditions, and buffs are cancelled. All creatures reset to their starting positions with their current HP. You must, by the end of the pact, never hold the same opinion or follow the same routine for more than 7 consecutive days ,  change something fundamental about your approach each week. Failure results in permanent predictability: you lose your Reactions, have disadvantage on all saves, and can be predicted by any divination effect.
