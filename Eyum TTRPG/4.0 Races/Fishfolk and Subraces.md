You can breathe air and water. 
- **Tier 1:** **Amphibious Frame.** While submerged, gain +1 AC.
- **Tier 2:** **Webbed Grip.** Gain +2 to Athletics and Acrobatics checks made while swimming as well as gain advantage on attack rolls while underwater. If you succeed in a melee attack against a creature it gains the [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Soaked]] condition for 1 turn. 
- **Tier 3:** **Tail Lash.** You gain a natural melee attack: 1d6 + STR mod + Racial Tier Bludgeoning. 
- **Tier 4:** **Water Resilience.** Gain Resistance to Water damage and you can reroll 1s on attack rolls and damage dice from Water Affinity spells.
- **Tier 5:** **Riptide Burst.** Once per turn as a Bonus Action, you may move up to 15 ft in water without provoking reactions and make one Tail Lash.
- **Tier 6:** **Gill Surge.** Gain +15 ft swim speed.
- **Tier 7:** **Predator’s Bite.** Your Tail Lash damage increases to 1d10 + STR mod + Racial Tier Bludgeoning.
- **Tier 8:** **Deep Diver.** You gain 60 ft of darkvision while underwater and advantage on attack rolls made in light and dim light while underwater. 
- **Tier 9:** **Current Crush.** As an action you may choose a target that is currently touching water or has the soaked condition and crush them with the pressure of the water. The target must make a CON save (DC 10 + Racial Tier + [[2.1 Affinities#Save DC Bonus|Save DC Bonus]]) on a fail they take 3d12 water damage and gain the [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Suffocating]] condition for 3 turns (end DC raised to 20). 
- **Tier 10:** **Apex Nerid.** Choose one:
    - **Leviathan Tail:** Your Tail Lash becomes 2d20 + STR mod + Racial Tier Bludgeoning.
    - **Ocean Crush:** Your Current Crush damage raises to 4d20 and [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental / Positional Conditions|Suffocating]] is applied for 5 turns (end DC raised to 25). 
    - **Abyss Hunter:** For one turn any attack you do which would deal water damage is now a crit (once per combat).

You can breathe air and water. 
- **Tier 1:** **Ocean-Bound.** While in a large body of water (at least 1,000 gallons) you gain +1d6 to all skill checks, saving throws, and attack rolls. 
- **Tier 2:** **Slick Escape.** Once per turn you may choose to succeed a athletics check to escape a grapple. 
- **Tier 3:** **Mermaid Call.** Once per combat as an Action, one creature within 30 ft that can hear you must pass a WIS save (DC 10 + CHA mod + Tier) or suffer -10 Accuracy against all targets except you until your next turn.
- **Tier 4:** **Water Affinity Bloom.** Gain +3 Water Affinity and become resistant to water damage.
- **Tier 5:** **Wave Kiss.** Once per turn when you deal damage with a spell or weapon, add 1d6 Water damage.
- **Tier 6:** **Mermaid Voice.** Your Mermaid Call skill can be used against up to 2 creatures and can now be used once per turn. 
- **Tier 7:** **Afraid to get wet?.** As a bonus action choose one creature without the soaked condition, it now has the soaked condition for 10 turns. 
- **Tier 8:** **Sea-Sense.** While in the ocean gain +10 to Search and Spot checks. 
- **Tier 9:** **Too Wet.** As an action you may choose one creature with the soaked condition, that creature takes 2d20 water damage (bypassing resistances). 
- **Tier 10:** **Queen of the Shoals.** Choose one:
    - **Perfect Voice:** Your Mermaid Call targets all enemies within 40 ft and the penalty is raised to -20 accuracy as well as preventing the use of damage dealing aoe spells from affected creatures.
    - **Abyssal Charm:** Gain +2 CHA and immunity to charmed, your Too Wet skill now does 4d20 + Water Affinity water damage.
    - **Vengeful Voice:** Any creature that succeeds against your Mermaid Call will take 5d6 water damage.

    - **Perfect Ink:** Your Ink Jet lasts 2 rounds and imposes -10 Accuracy.
    - **Tentacle Master:** Your Tentacle Strike damage increases to 2d20 + Racial Tier + Strength mob bludgeoning

- **Tier 1:** **Shellguard.** Gain +5 to your CON score, +1 AC and 10 Damage Reduction vs non-magical bludgeoning/piercing/slashing.
- **Tier 2:** **Ocean Weight.** You are immune to forced movement effects and the prone condition. 
- **Tier 3:** **Withdraw.** Once per turn consume all Ap, BAp, Rp, and movement, you retract into your shell until the start of your next turn: gain +10 AC. This action must be taken at the start of your turn. You may choose to stay withdrawn on your turn which will simply skip it and maintain the effects. 
- **Tier 4:** **Water Resilience.** Gain Resistance to all damage other than True and Psychic and +3 to your CON score.
- **Tier 5:** **Shell Bash.** You gain a natural melee attack: 1d12 + STR mod Bludgeoning.
- **Tier 6:** **Reinforced Plates.** Gain +1 AC (stacks), while using Withdraw skill your AC bonus raises from +10 to +20.
- **Tier 7:** **Hard to Kill.** Once per combat, when you would drop to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead.
- **Tier 8:** **Ancient Bulk.** Your damage reduction becomes 15 and now works on all damage types. Gain +5 to your CON score. 
- **Tier 9:** **Shell Fortress.** While withdrawn you automatically succeed all saving throws and heal 1d4 Vitality per turn. 
- **Tier 10:** **Elder Chelonar.** Choose one:
    - **Titan Shell:** Gain +4 AC and your damage reduction becomes 25.
    - **Crushing Dome:** Your Shell Bash becomes 2d20 + STR mod + Racial Tier Bludgeoning.
    - **Unbreakable:** Critical hits against you become normal hits and aoe attacks deal all of their damage to your vitality instead of split into HP and Vitality.
    - **Shell Hermit:** Withdrawn no longer consumes movement and while withdrawn your damage reduction raises to 100 but you still cannot take Actions, Bonus Actions or Reactions.
