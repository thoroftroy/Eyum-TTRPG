When learning a spell, you may choose from the available options. All spells can be upcast by increasing the mana cost according to the specified multiplier, such as x2, x3, or higher. Upcasting is applied multiplicatively for each tier you add; for example, if a spell has a base cost of 2 mana and a x2 upcast multiplier, the first upcast costs 4 mana, the second costs 8 mana, the third costs 16 mana, and so on. This allows you to stack multiple levels of upcasting in a single cast to exponentially increase the spell's potency, provided you have the mana to support the total cost.

**Notes:**
- **Spell Attack Modifier:** Generic Affinity + Relevant Affinity + Base Magic Accuracy
- **Spell Save DC:** 10 + Generic Affinity + Relevant Affinity
- Concentration means that it stays active as long as you are focusing on the spell. Attempting to cast the spell again or another spell will cancel the previous one. If you take damage to Vitality or Health you must make a Concentration (just a 1d20) check (DC: 12) if you fail you lose concentration on the spell. 
- As is always with conditions, when a spell inflicts a condition but does not specify an end time use the conditions table to determine the end condition. End Condition DC's are raised (or lowered) to your spell save DC. 

All spells have levels of upcast, you can only upcast spells based on your level in the Spell Socialist. Most elements have one spell, each upcast costs more mana but creates a much greater effect. 

Magic Blast
- Magic Blast, Choose an affinity type you have at least 3 in and make a magical attack against a creature in range which you can see. On hit deal 1d4 of  your chosen affinity damage.
- Mana Bolt, Choose an affinity type you have at least 5 in and make a magical attack against a creature in range which you can see. On hit deal 1+ Affinity of  your chosen affinity damage.
- Mana Bomb, Choose an affinity type you have at least 5 in choose a 10 ft area within range that you can see, that area explodes with magic dealing 3 + Affinity damage to all creatures in the area who fail a Dex Save.
- Mana Explosion, Choose an affinity type you have at least 10 in choose a 25 ft area within range that you can see, that area explodes with magic dealing 5 + Affinity damage to all creatures in the area who fail a Dex Save and half to all who succeed.
- Mana Decimation, Choose an affinity type you have at least 10 in choose a 25 ft area within range that you can see, that area explodes with magic dealing 30 + (Affinity damage * 2) to all creatures in the area who fail a Dex Save and half to all who succeed.
