Fiends typically want to spread chaos and death, typically by the means of their type of damage. They gain power from people fearing their Affinity. Fiends punishments are usually very severe. 
## Black Mercy
_Necrotic, Evil Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Necrotic damage. You may convert one attack per turn's damage to Necrotic. Once per turn when you deal Necrotic damage you may deal an additional 2d10 Necrotic damage. You must kill at least 1 creature with necrotic damage today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Slow Death (1 stacks) for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** You temporarily learn the spell Necrotic Blast (Range 80 ft, Affinity Necrotic, 5 Mana) Make a spell attack against a creature you can see within range, on hit the creature takes 4d10 necrotic damage and gains one stack of Slow Death (upcast x2 for +1 blast (separate attack roll)). You must kill at least 5 humanoid creatures before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Cursed for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per long rest, as an Action, you may create a 20 ft radius Mercy Sink within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside cannot regain HP, and the first time each round an enemy inside takes an Action it takes 3d10 Necrotic damage to its HP. You must kill at least 12 humanoid creatures before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you losing the ability to regain HP for the next 30 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, you may declare No Mercy for 3 turns. During No Mercy, all damage you deal bypasses Vitality and goes to HP, and the first time each round you deal damage to a creature’s HP it gains Demoralized for 1 turn. You must kill at least 30 humanoid creatures before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Despair for the next 12 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Black Mercy Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, choose one creature within 60 ft. For 3 turns, at the start of its turn it takes 8d10 Necrotic damage to its HP and cannot regain HP for 3 turns. You must kill at least 200 humanoid creatures before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Red Furnace
_Magma, Neutral Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Fire damage. Once per turn when you hit with a melee attack, you may deal an additional 2d10 Magma damage. You must deal Magma damage at least 5 times today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Hellfire for 1 minute every hour for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** At the start of each combat you gain Heat equal to your level. Once per turn, you may spend any amount of Heat to add +2 damage per Heat spent to one damage roll. Heat resets at the end of combat. You must spend at least 20 total Heat before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you taking 1d8 Infernal damage to your HP at the start of each of your turns in combat for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per long rest, as an Action, create a 30 ft line (5 ft wide) of molten ground within 60 ft for 1 minute. Creatures that enter or start their turn in the line take 4d10 Magma damage and their speed is halved until the end of their turn. You must deal at least 120 total Magma damage during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you permanently losing 25 maximum HP.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per combat, as a Reaction when you take damage, you may halve that damage, then deal the prevented amount as Magma damage split among enemies within 15 ft of you (choose targets; split as evenly as possible). You must trigger this reaction at least once per day. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Hellfire for 1 minute every hour for the next 30 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Red Furnace Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, create a 40 ft radius Furnace Field centered on you for 3 turns. Enemies that start their turn in the field take 6d10 Magma damage. You must deal at least 500 total Magma damage during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Knotted Word
_Cursed, Evil Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** Once per turn when you hit a creature, you may apply Cursed to it for 1 minute. You must apply Cursed to at least 3 different creatures today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Cursed for the next 7 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per turn, as a Reaction when a creature within 60 ft rolls a d20, you may force it to reroll and use the new result. If the reroll is lower than the original, that creature also becomes Cursed for 1 minute. You must force at least 3 rerolls each day. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you having Cursed at all times for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per turn you may convert your damage from an attack to Cursed Damage. As an Action, create a 30 ft radius Contract Zone within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside cannot benefit from advantage from any source. The first time each round an enemy inside rolls a d20, it takes 2d10 Cursed damage to its HP. You must kill at least 20 humanoid creatures from Cursed Damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Despair for the next 7 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** All of your on hit attacks apply the cursed condition for one turn. When a creature with the cursed condition within 60 ft takes an Action, you may spend a Reaction to cancel that Action (it is spent and does nothing). You must apply cursed to at least 40 unique creatures before the end of your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you losing your first Action on your first turn of every combat for the next 30 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Knotted Word Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It cannot take Actions, Bonus Actions, or Reactions for 3 turns. At the start of each of its turns it takes 8d10 Cursed damage to its HP. Before the end of the pact period you must kill at least 100 creatures and apply cursed at least 200 separate times. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Pale Cinder
_Ash, Chaotic Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You may change any damage you deal into Ash damage. Once per turn when you deal Ash damage to a creature’s HP, it becomes Blurred for 1 turn. You must deal Ash damage to HP at least 2 times today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Blurred for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per combat, as an Action, create a 20 ft radius Ash Cloud within 60 ft for 1 minute. Creatures inside have double disadvantage on Search and Spot checks, and the first time each round an enemy inside takes damage it takes an additional 2d10 Ash damage. You must keep at least one Ash Cloud active in at least 2 combats during your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Blinded for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** As an Action you may apply On Fire to any flammable object or any creature you touch. After the creature's On Fire condition ends you may do any of the following with the resulting ash. You may only use the ash from a creatures On Fire ending once, it must be used within 3 turns of the condition ending. You may use ash from any On Fire, you don't have to cause it. 
- As a bonus action you may control the ash to protect you, this increases your AC by +2 until the start of your next turn. 
- As a bonus action command the ash to attack a creature you can see within 40 ft, they must make a CON save (save halves, DC 15) or take 2d6+10 Ash damage. 
- As a bonus action consume the ash and heal 1d6+5 Vitality.
You must kill at least 10 creatures with Ash or Fire damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being unable to take Reactions for the next 30 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per long rest, declare Grey Sun (cover the sky in ash and blot out the sun) for 3 turns (radius 120 ft with you at the center). During Grey Sun,
- Enemies within the radius cannot regain HP and take 4d10 Ash damage to HP at the start of their turns. 
- All light within the radius counts as dim or darkness.
- All creatures (other than you) have double disadvantage on search and spot checks and attack rolls.
- You are constantly in stealth as long as you are behind a creature or more than 10 ft away from them. 
At least 40 creatures must witness and be affected by your Gray Sun during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you taking 2d10 Ash damage at the start of each of your turns in combat or 1d10 Ash damage every 1 hour outside of combat for the next 30 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Pale Cinder Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, create an Ashfall in a 60 ft radius within 120 ft for 3 turns. Enemies inside take 8d10 Ash damage to HP at the start of their turns, gain the suffocating condition, and their movement is halved. You must deal at least 600 total Ash damage during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Wither Bloom
_Blight, Lawful Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** When you deal damage you may change it to Blight damage. You must apply Withered to at least 1 creature today (from any source). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you becoming Withered for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per combat, as a Bonus Action, choose one creature you can see within 60 ft. Until the end of your next turn, the first time it takes damage it also takes 3d10 Blight damage. You must kill a creature from the Withered condition within the pact period. Failure will result in you becoming Withered for the next 7 days. 

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per combat, as an Action, create a 30 ft radius Rot Field within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside take 3d10 Blight damage the start of their turns and have disadvantage on saving throws while inside. You must deal at least 200 total Blight damage during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being unable to regain Vitality by any means for the next 30 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** When you deal damage you may change it to Blight damage. Once per combat, as a Reaction when a creature within 60 ft regains Vitality, you may negate that regain and instead deal 4d10 Blight damage to its HP. You must kill at least 5 creatures from the Withered condition during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you permanently losing half of your Vitality maximum.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Wither Bloom Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, choose up to 5 creatures within 60 ft. They immediately gain Withered and take 6d10+10 Blight damage. You must apply Withered to at least 60 total creatures, at least 30 of which must die from the condition during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Serpent’s Kiss
_Poison, Neutral Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Poison damage. Any on hit attacks you hit apply the poisoned condition. You must kill at least one humanoid creature with poison damage today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Poisoned for the next 7 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** When you would deal damage you may change its type to poison. Once per combat, as an Action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It must make a DC 15 CON save. On a fail it gains Poisoned (2 stacks). You must successfully apply Poisoned at least 1 time per day. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Poisoned (4 stacks) for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per long rest, create a 20 ft radius Venom Ring within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies that start their turn in the ring take 3d10 Poison damage and gain the poisoned condition. If they are Poisoned, they take an additional 2d10 Poison damage to HP. You must deal at least 250 total Poison damage during the pact duration. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you permanently losing 2 to all d20 saving throws and attack rolls.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** Once per combat, declare Antivenom Theft for 3 turns. During this time, whenever a creature within 30 ft would remove Poisoned or otherwise end the poisoned condition, it fails and instead takes 4d10 Poison damage and gains poisoned for another turn. You must kill at least 1 humanoid creature per day by poison during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being permanently poisoned and gaining lesser weakness to poison damage. 

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Serpent’s Kiss Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It gains Poisoned (6 stacks) with no save. You must end the pact with at least 300 total unique humanoid creatures poisoned by you during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
## Lung Spore
_Toxin, Evil Alignment_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Toxin damage. When you miss an attack against a creature it must make CON save (DC 10) or take 2d12 Toxin damage. You must kill at least one humanoid creature with Toxin damage today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Suffocating for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (3 days):** Once per combat, as a Bonus Action, exhale a 15 ft cone. Creatures in the cone make a DC 15 CON save. On a fail, they gain Poisoned and Mute for 1 turn and take 3d12 Toxin damage. You must kill at least 3 humanoid creatures with Toxin damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Mute for the next 12 days.

**Tier 3 (7 days):** Once per combat, as an Action, create a 30 ft radius Spore Fog within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside have disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws, and at the start of their turns they take 2d10 Toxin damage to HP (all goes to HP not half from normal aoe rules). You must kill at least 7 creatures with Toxin damage during your pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being Poisoned (3 stacks) at all times for the next 30 days.

**Tier 4 (12 days):** As an Action, choose one creature within 60 ft. It must make a DC 15 CON save. On a fail, it gains Suffocating and Poisoned for 3 turns and takes 3d10 toxin damage. You must kill at least 12 humanoid creatures from Toxin damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you starting every combat Suffocating for 3 turns for the next 30 days.

**Tier 5 (30 days):** You gain all Lung Spore Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat, as an Action, create a 60 ft radius Dead Air within 120 ft for 3 turns. Enemies inside gain Suffocating immediately and take 6d10 Toxin damage to HP at the start of their turns. You must kill at least 40 humanoid creatures during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in permanent death. You cannot be revived by any means.
