None can predict what it is an ancient might want, their goals are seemingly random and their punishments random. 
## Newton
*Generic/Force*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You are immune to Force damage, forced movement and the prone condition. You must throw an apple at someone today. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining the Slowed Condition (cannot be removed by any means and applies even if you are immune) for the next day. 

**Tier 2 (4 days):** The faster you are moving the more damage you deal. For every 5 ft of movement you move each round you gain +2 damage on your next melee or ranged attack. You must stick a needle into your eye once each day and leave it there. While the needle is there you lose -5 to search and spot checks. After 1 hour you may remove the needle and the damage will be healed. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in taking 1d8+1 Force damage at the start of each turn (bypassing resistances and immunities) while in combat and your speed will be reduced by half for the next 3 days. 

**Tier 3 (9 days):** Whenever a creature hits you, you may spend a reaction to deal the same amount of damage back to them (you still take the damage as well). Any conditions or movement applied will also be reflected. You must view a rainbow once each day while the pact is active, you may make the rainbow yourself with use of a prism or waterfall but it must be viewed. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you losing all resistances and immunities to damage as well as all of your immunities to conditions and effects for the next 7 days, for the first half of each day (from midnight to noon) you will have the Slowed condition (cannot be removed by any means and applies even when you are immune). 

**Tier 4 (16 days):** Once per round, when you would take damage, be forcibly moved, or gain a condition, you may spend a Reaction to negate that instance entirely and reflect it onto the source (damage, movement distance/direction, and condition all apply to the source instead). You must experience at least 60 consecutive feet of true free fall once each day while the pact is active (no climbing, no swimming, no being held up; magic is allowed only if it results in genuine free fall (you are allowed to do things to negate the fall damage as long as you did at least 60 feet of free fall)). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Greater Weakness to all damage for 12 days. 

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all benefits of Newton Tier 1–4 while this pact is active. Also, you cannot be restrained, grappled, or slowed and your movement never provokes reactions. You must, once each day, strike a creature with a thrown object you launched yourself from at least 60 ft away (the object must leave your hand; a sling/bow/crossbow does not count). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining the Slowed condition for the next 30 days (cannot be removed by any means and applies even if you are immune), and whenever you move on your turn you take 2d10 Force damage at the end of that movement (bypassing resistances and immunities).
## Darwin
*Earth*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Poison damage and immunity to the Poisoned condition. Whenever you take toxin, blight, poison, cold, fire, lightning, or necrotic damage, you gain resistance to that damage type until your next long rest (one type at a time; the most recent replaces the old). You must eat something living today (a live insect, worm, small fish, or similar; it must be alive when it enters your mouth). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining the Sickened condition for the next day, and you lose all resistances and immunities you currently have for that day.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** At the start of each of your turns, choose one: Gain +3 AC until the start of your next turn, or gain +10 ft speed until the start of your next turn, or gain +10 damage on your next attack this turn. If you are below half Vitality, you may choose two. You must, once each day, leave a personal item in the dirt and bury it fully (you may retrieve it when the pact ends). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you becoming Vulnerable to Earth damage for the next 4 days and your speed is reduced by 10 ft for the next 4 days.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** As a bonus action, you may assume one of the following adaptations for 1 hour (you may switch once per hour): Burrow speed 30 ft; climb speed 40 ft; or True Sight 60 ft. While adapted, you have advantage on attacks against creatures that cannot see you. You must, once each day, shed blood into soil (take any damage from the bleeding condition, you may heal afterwards). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in your body rejecting itself: for the next 9 days, you cannot regain Vitality from rest, and whenever you regain HP by any means, you instead regain half and take the other half as Earth damage (bypassing resistances and immunities) to your vitality (if you have no Vitality you just regain half of the HP you should be gaining).

**Tier 4 (16 days):** When you would drop to 0 Vitality, you instead drop to 1 Vitality and immediately gain 2d20 Vitality. This can occur once per day while the pact is active. Also, you gain Greater Resistance to Earth damage, and you ignore difficult terrain. You must, once each day, kill a creature with your bare hands or natural weapons and leave its body unburied until the next sunrise. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you becoming incredibly frail: for the next 3 days you have -5 to your STR, CON, and DEX scores. If you reach 0 Vitality you immediately die, and you cannot be revived by any means.

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all Darwin Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, whenever you take damage, you may spend a Reaction to evolve (gain lesser resistance to that damage type, if you do it again to the damage type it upgrades to regular resistance, then greater resistance). You must, by the end of the pact, create a new lifeform in some way: breed a beast, cultivate a strain of plant or fungus, or cause a new creature to be born under your supervision (the Ancient (GM) decides whether it counts). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you being erased from existence. You die and cannot be revived.
## Da Vinci
*Air*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain +10 to any type of skill check for the day. You must draw a working blueprint today (it must be a real design that could be built, not a doodle). Failure will result in you losing the ability to take Reactions for the next day.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** As a bonus action, you may gain a 30 ft flying speed until the end of your turn. If you end your turn if in the air, you fall normally. You must, once each day, hang upside down for 10 minutes without touching the ground. Failure will result in you becoming Grounded: for the next 4 days you cannot fly, teleport, or be moved by any magical effect, and your jump height becomes 0.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You may create one air construct per short rest: a visible, solid formation of wind within 30 ft (wall, platform, cage, or blade) that lasts 10 minutes. It has AC 20 and 50 HP and is immune to poison and psychic damage. If used as a weapon, it deals 3d10 Air damage on hit. You must build something with moving parts which serves some function before the end of the pact period. Failure will result in your fingers betraying you: for the next 9 days you take -10 to all attack rolls and sleight of hand skill checks.

**Tier 4 (16 days):** As a reaction you may change the direction of any ranged attack, spell line, or otherwise projectile you can see within 60 ft, redirecting it to a new target within 30 ft of its original path. You may do this even if the effect would not normally be redirectable. Da Vinci will give you a location (GM specified) of a high point you must reach within the pact period. You must reach that location and sit in silence there for 5 minutes. Failure will result in the air turning against you: for the next 16 days you gain the Suffocating condition for 3 turns if you fail any skill check, attack roll, or saving throw, this will also immediately end your turn. 

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all Da Vinci Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition you gain +1 Ap and +2 BAp for the pact duration. You must, by the end of the pact, create a masterpiece that another creature willingly praises (the Ancient (GM) decides if the praise is sincere enough). Failure will result in the theft of your genius: for the next 33 days you cannot benefit from feats, skill bonuses, or proficiency of any kind for skill checks and attack rolls; all checks are made with only raw stats.
## Newcomen
*Steam*

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain resistance to Fire, Water and Steam damage and immunity to Exhaustion. You can, as a bonus action, vent steam: creatures within 5 ft must succeed a CON save or be pushed 10 ft and take 1d10 Steam damage. You must boil water today and inhale the steam for 10 seconds. Failure will result in your lungs filling with steam (gain the Mute condition for 1 day).

**Tier 2 (4 days):** At the start of combat, you gain Pressure equal to your level (Additive, no cap). Once per turn, you may spend any amount of Pressure to add that much Steam damage to an attack (3 Steam damage per 1 Pressure). Pressure resets at the end of combat. You must, once each day, burn your hand on a hot surface (at least 5 damage) and not heal it for 1 hour. Failure will result in Pressure backlash: for the next 4 days, at the start of each combat you take 2d10 Steam damage (bypassing resistances and immunities).

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You gain a 15 ft aura of scalding air; enemies that start their turn in it take 2d10 Steam damage and have their speed reduced by 10 ft until the end of their turn. You must deal Steam damage at least once each day. If you fail than for the next 9 days your speed is halved, and any time you take the Dash action you take 3d10 Steam damage.

**Tier 4 (16 days):** You gain resistance to Steam damage. Once per day, as an action, you may rupture your pressure in a 30 ft radius: all creatures (including you) must make a CON save. On a fail they take 10d10 Steam damage and are pushed 30 ft away from you (if you fail you are not pushed); on a success, half damage and pushed 10 ft. You take half damage regardless. You must sit in steam for at least 1 hour each day. On failure, for the next 16 days, you will take 1d10 Steam damage (bypassing resistances and immunities) at the start of each day, this damage goes directly to HP and cannot be healed until the punishment period is over. This damage cannot reduce your health below 1 hp. 

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all Newcomen Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, you may maintain Pressure between combats. You may spend 20 Pressure to gain an additional Ap. You must deal at least 500 points of Steam damage before the pact period ends. Failure will result in permanent greater weakness to Fire, Water, and Steam damage. You also permanently lose half of your Vitality Maximum. 
## Hubble
_Dust_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain True Sight 60 ft and can see through fog, smoke, and dust as if it were not there. You must spend 10 minutes tonight staring at the sky with both eyes open. Failure will result in you gaining the Blinded condition for a day (cannot be removed early and applies even if you are immune).

**Tier 2 (4 days):** As a bonus action, mark one creature you can see within 300 ft. You always know its direction and distance, and your attacks against it ignore cover. The mark lasts 1 hour and can be used twice per day. You must be covered with dust as often as possible for the duration of the pact, if you clean yourself off intentionally or do not get dirty again within a reasonable time frame (Hubble (GM) decides) then you fail the pact. Failure will result in you being constantly covered in dust. You gain the Grounded condition for 4 days and during the duration you have -4 to Search and Spot checks as well as -3 to CHAR score.

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You may create a Dust Field within 60 ft as an action; it fills a 20 ft radius for 10 minutes. Enemies inside are outlined and cannot benefit from invisibility or stealth. Allies inside are treated as if they are always in stealth. You may only have one Dust Field active at once. You must kill a creature using Suffocation damage within the Pact duration. Failure will result in you taking 1d4 dust damage at the start of each turn in combat and gaining the suffocating condition for 2 turns each time you take dust or earth damage for 9 days. 

**Tier 4 (16 days):** You may change the damage type of any of your attacks to Dust damage. You may double any dust damage you deal. You must at all times keep one of your eyes covered. While one eye is covered you have disadvantage on ranged attacks and search and spot checks. Failure will result in you having half movement speed and taking suffocating damage every other turn (starting on your second turn in combat) for the next 16 days. 

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all Hubble Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. If you die during the pact period you may reform your body from dust and resurrect after 1 hour. You must apply the Suffocating condition to at least 10 people before the end of the pact period, this condition must result in the deaths of at least 10 of those people. Failure will result in death, you're body becomes one with the earth and to dust you return. You cannot be revived by any means.  
## Pasteur
_Mud_

**Tier 1 (1 day):** You gain immunity to disease and poison conditions and Mud damage. When you deal damage, you may choose to infect the target; it takes 1d12 Mud damage at the start of its next turn. You must wash your hands exactly once today, and only once. Failure will result in you being Poisoned for the next day and any healing you receive is halved.

**Tier 2 (4 days):** Whenever you deal damage while standing on dirt you may deal an additional 1d8 Mud damage. Whenever you deal mud damage you gain half of it back as Vitality. You must, once each day, consume something fermented or spoiled on purpose. Failure will result you being Slowed and Grounded for the next 2 days. 

**Tier 3 (9 days):** You can sink into mud and move through it at will, while inside of the mud you cannot take any other actions or bonus actions and you cannot be targeted unless by fire damage. Leaving this mud state will end your turn. You must take this action at some point every day or you will be unable to take short rests for the next 9 days. 

**Tier 4 (16 days):** Once per day, choose a creature you can see within 60 ft. It must make a CON save. On a failure it becomes your Carrier for 1 hour: it spreads Mud Wrath in a 15 ft aura; enemies in the aura have disadvantage on all rolls and take 3d10 Mud damage at the start of their turns. You are immune to your own plague. You must, once each day, sleep in wet mud for at least 1 hour. If you fail you will be unable to heal from any source or gain any buffs from potions or runes for 16 days. 

**Tier 5 (33 days):** You gain all Pasteur Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. Any creatures who are carriers for your Mud Wrath take double damage from mud and when they deal mud damage from their Aura roll a percentage dice. If you get a 5 or above the affected creature is now also a carrier of your Mud Wrath. You must infect at least 400 living creatures with Mud Wrath before the pact period is over. Failure will result in death.  
## Kepler
_Nova_

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You cannot be surprised. You have advantage on Initiative. Once per combat, you may reroll one d20 you rolled (attack, save, or check). You must, once during the pact, spend 10 uninterrupted minutes observing the night sky and record at least 6 distinct points (stars, lights, or celestial objects) in a note or sketch. Failure results in gaining the Blurred condition for 2 days, this condition cannot be removed early and applies even if you are immune.

**Tier 2 (6 days):** Once per turn when you miss an attack, you may immediately make that same attack again against the same target for free. If it hits, it deals +3d10 Nova damage and applies Blinded x1. You must, once per day, pick a fixed direction (north, sunrise, a landmark, etc.) and travel at least 1,000 feet total that day while keeping it on the same side of your body the entire time (GM adjudicates reasonable exceptions). Failure results in gaining the Demoralized condition for 6 days, this condition cannot be removed early and applies even if you are immune.

**Tier 3 (12 days):** Once per short rest, as an action, you may orbit around a point you can see within 120 ft for 1 minute. While in the orbit:
- You constantly move clockwise around the target point (30 ft away) in a circle, you must expend all movement each turn moving in the orbit. If you do not expend all movement moving in the direction of the orbit or are knocked out of place the orbit ends. 
- You ignore difficult terrain.
- Your movement does not provoke reactions.
- The first time each round you hit a creature, add +4d10 Nova damage and apply Burned x1.  
You must, by the end of the pact, produce an accurate map of a route you personally traveled during the pact (it can be crude but must let someone follow it). Failure results in gaining the Blinded condition for 12 days, this condition cannot be removed early and applies even if you are immune.

**Tier 4 (18 days):** Once per day, you may mark one creature you can see as “Inevitable” for 1 minute. On each hit, deal +6d10 Nova damage; if the hit would apply a condition from your attack, increase its stacks by +1 (if it stacks).  You must, once per day, correctly predict one concrete thing that will happen before your next long rest (a creature enters a room, an NPC says a specific phrase, a door is opened, combat starts, etc.). The prediction must be stated aloud or written before it happens. Failure results in gaining the Despair for 18 days, this condition cannot be removed early and applies even if you are immune.

**Tier 5 (29 days):** You gain all Kepler Tier 1–4 benefits while active. In addition, once per day you may declare a “Transit” for 10 minutes: you and allies within 30 ft gain +20 ft speed, ignore difficult terrain, and each of your hits during Transit deals +8d10 Nova damage and applies Blinded x1 and Burned x1. You gain the ability to banish creatures into an unknown plane of existence if Kepler wills it. At the start of your pact Kepler will tell you the target, you must find and banish the target before the pact period ends. Each time you banish a target Kepler may give you a new target but you must only complete one in order for the pact to be fulfilled. Each time you Banish a target you gain advantage on all rolls (attack, save, checks) for the next 2 days. Failure results in: for 29 days, your first turn of every combat is skipped and you begin each combat Blurred and Demoralized for 3 turns, these conditions cannot be removed early and apply even if you are immune.

Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Prone x1 and Deafened x4 at the start of every combat for the next 18 days.

**Tier 5 (29 days):** You gain all Richter Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per day you may cause a catastrophic tremor: all creatures of your choice within 120 ft take 10d10 Quake damage and gain Prone x3. You must, by the end of the pact, apply Deafened to a total of 40 unique creatures. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Prone x2, Deafened x8, and Stunned x1 at the start of every combat for the next 29 days.
## Hawking
_Void_

**Tier 1 (3 days):** You gain resistance to Void damage and immunity to Suffocating. Once per day, as a bonus action, you may make your body weightless until the end of your turn; during that turn your movement does not provoke reactions. You must, once per day, spend 10 uninterrupted minutes in complete silence (no speaking, no deliberate noise, etc). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Frozen (2 stacks) x2 and Suffocating x2 at the start of every combat for the next 3 days.

**Tier 2 (7 days):** When you deal damage you may change the damage type to Void. The first time each combat you deal Void damage to a creature, it gains Frozen (2 stacks) x2 and Suffocating x2. You must kill at least 5 humanoid creatures with Void damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Frozen (3 stacks) x3 and Suffocating x3 at the start of every combat for the next 7 days.

**Tier 3 (14 days):** Once per day, as an action, create a 30 ft radius Void Pocket within 60 ft for 1 minute. Enemies inside have their movement halved, and the first time each round they take an action, they take 3d10 Void damage. The area inside counts as magical darkness. You are unaffected and can see through the darkness perfectly. You must, at least 10 times during the pact period, start a combat without speaking a single word or casting a spell during the first 3 rounds. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Frozen (4 stacks) x4 and Suffocating x4 at the start of every combat for the next 14 days.

**Tier 4 (21 days):** As an action, select one creature you can see within 60 ft. It must make a CON save (DC 12 + WIS mod). On a failure, it becomes airless for 1 minute: it immediately gains Suffocating, and at the start of each of its turns it takes 4d10 Void damage. This attack has a cool down of 3 turns. You must successfully apply this airless condition onto at least 1 creature a day. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Frozen (5 stacks) x5 and Suffocating x5 at the start of every combat for the next 21 days.

**Tier 5 (35 days):** You gain all Hawking Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, as an Action once per day you may create a black hole (radius 40 ft) in a space you can see within 120 ft. The space in which you create the black hole must be empty as well. 
- This black hole shrinks by 5 ft each turn until it disappears. 
- All creatures within 100 ft of the black hole must make a STR save at the start of their turn. Failing the save pulls them 10 ft closer to the Black Hole and prevents movement on their turn. Movement is halved if in the range of the Black Hole. The DC for the save changes based on the distance from the black hole as well as some of its other effects. 
	- 5-20 ft away, DC is 35. Movement is reduced to 5 ft or halved, whichever is lower. Cannot take Actions, Bonus Actions or Reactions..
	- 25-45 ft away, DC is 25. Movement is reduced to 15 ft or halved, whichever is lower. Cannot take Bonus Actions or Reactions.
	- 50-100 ft away, DC is 15. Movement is halved. Cannot take Reactions.
- Creatures within the Black Hole take 500 True Damage at the start of each turn and they are Petrified for their turn.
You must kill at least 350 creatures using your Black Hole during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in death, the void consumes you, you cannot be resurrected by any means. 
## Oppenheimer
_Atomic_

**Tier 1 (2 days):** You gain resistance to Atomic, Fire and Radiant damage. Once per day, as an action, deal 4d10 Atomic damage to a creature you can touch. You must, once per day, willingly take at least 10 damage from a single damage instance while not at 0 Vitality. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Radiation x1 for the next 2 days.

**Tier 2 (6 days):** Once per combat, you may Prime a creature you can see within 120 ft. The next time it takes damage (within 1 minute), it takes an additional 10d10 Atomic damage and gains Radiation x1. You must, once per day, deal Atomic damage at least once, and you may not heal yourself for 1 hour after doing so. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Radiation x2 for the next 6 days.

**Tier 3 (12 days):** When you kill a creature, it detonates: all enemies within 20 ft take 6d10 Atomic damage and gain Radiation x1. You are immune to your own detonation. You must, once per day, end your turn adjacent to a creature that currently has Radiation (any creature, any source) for 3 total turns that day (not necessarily consecutive). Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you radiating: every creature within 10 ft takes 1d10 Atomic damage at the start of its turn for the next 12 days and you permanently have radiation condition.

**Tier 4 (18 days):** Once per combat, as an action, create a 60 ft radius blast within 300 ft: enemies take 12d10 Atomic damage and gain Radiation x2. You take 6d10 Atomic damage. You must kill at least 50 creatures from atomic damage during the pact period. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining Radiation x3 permanently.

**Tier 5 (29 days):** You gain all Oppenheimer Tier 1–4 benefits while this pact is active. In addition, once per combat you may unleash a true detonation at a point within 300 ft: 120 ft radius, 30d10+100 Atomic damage and all creatures hit gain Radiation x4. You must, by the end of the pact, kill at least 1,000 creatures. Failure to complete your end of the pact will result in you gaining permanent Radiation x5.
