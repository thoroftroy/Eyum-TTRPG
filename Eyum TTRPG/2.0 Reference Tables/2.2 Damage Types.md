Damage types are grouped into broad categories. Creatures may have multipliers against specific damage types or entire groupings. 

- **Immunity:** Take no damage
- **Greater Resistance:** Take 1/3 damage (round down)
- **Resistance:** Take 1/2 damage (round down)
- **Lesser Resistance:** Take 3/4 damage (round down)
- *(no multiplier — take full damage)*
- **Lesser Weakness:** Take 1.5× damage (round down)
- **Weakness:** Take 2× damage
- **Greater Weakness:** Take 3× damage

Groupings
- **Physical**: Non-magical sources (slashing, bludgeoning, piercing)
- **Elemental**: Elemental sources (fire, earth, water, air, and combinations)
- **Magical**: Magical sources (elemental damage type when from a spell specifically)
- **Mixed/Magical Physical**: Physical damage (slashing, bludgeoning, piercing) from magical sources or enchanted weapons)
- **Special**: Unusual sources (psychic, force, true)

For example, a creature with Greater Resistance to Elemental takes 1/3 damage from fire, water, lightning, magma — everything elemental. A creature with Greater Weakness to slashing takes 3× damage from any slashing weapon.

## Combination Damage Types
Combination Damage Types are advanced damage categories formed by merging two or more elemental or magical sources into a single hybrid effect. In order to be resistant or weak to a combination damage type you must either be weak or resistant to all components or the combination type specifically. 

**Note:** If a condition causes a damage type which causes a condition or vice versa it is not recursive.

> **Damage types at a glance: what you actually need to know**
>
> There are six groupings: Physical (non-magical weapons), Magical (spells and enchanted weapons), Elemental (fire, water, etc.), Mixed (physical enhanced with magic), and Special (psychic, force, true). Creatures can have immunity, greater resistance (1/3), resistance (1/2), weakness (2x), or greater weakness (3x) to any of these. Resistances to a grouping apply to every damage type in that group. If a creature has greater resistance to Elemental, it takes 1/3 damage from Fire, Water, Lightning, Magma; everything elemental. Same for weakness. 
>
> **Combination damage types** like Lightning (Fire + Air) use a different rule: you're only resistant or weak to them if you're resistant or weak to ALL their components OR the combination type specifically. So being resistant to Fire alone doesn't make you resistant to Lightning; you'd also need Air resistance.
>
> **Special cases to remember:**
> - Psychic only affects Vitality and never deals Health damage, but imposes powerful conditions.
> - True damage ignores everything Vitality, resistances, immunities. True is true.
