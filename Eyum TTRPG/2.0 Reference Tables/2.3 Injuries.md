Use the damage type of the source that dealt HP damage. If a source explicitly inflicts a listed condition (such as [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Necrosis]] or [[2.4 Conditions#Physical-Conditions|Hellfire]]), apply that condition as written as well as the table’s default.  Conditions from injuries are applied on top of the standard conditions and checks from taking damage from that damage source. Injury conditions are applied separately from normal conditions, they do not stack with normal conditions like normal conditions would stack, they do however stack with other injury conditions. 

Each condition includes an x# value indicating how many turns it lasts. Unless otherwise specified these conditions cannot be ended early without using a spell or skill that directly ends them (Normal end/removal conditions do not apply to injuries). Otherwise, the condition automatically ends at the end of its listed duration, regardless of any other timing or effects. Some effects (like obsidian's slow death) do not have a specified x#, this means the condition continues like normal until it is removed as normal instead of waiting it out like a normal injury. Also effects like [[2.4 Conditions#Environmental-Positional-Conditions|Prone]] can be removed as normal, having multiple stacks will mean you fall prone at the start of each turn until your stacks run out.
