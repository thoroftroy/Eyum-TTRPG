----------------------------------------

       - Increase base weapon damage or weapon damage_bonus values.
       - Add more damage-boosting feats for physical builds.
       - Check if extra_damage_die on weapons is being applied correctly.
  2. Affinity: Corruption Mage wins 61% of all BEST categories, indicating severe over-tuning.
       - Reduce Affinity: Corruption Mage's stat scaling or damage output.
       - Verify Affinity: Corruption Mage is not receiving unintended bonuses from paths or gear.
       - Consider splitting its advantages across multiple builds.
  3. Affinity: Corruption Mage has a monopoly on Dmg/Turn (98%).
       - Rebalance damage formulas so other builds can compete in this category.
       - Check if Affinity: Corruption Mage has unintended damage synergies.

       - This may be acceptable if Jack's niche is flexibility, not specialization.
       - If Jack should be viable, consider giving it unique hybrid bonuses or more skill/stat points.
