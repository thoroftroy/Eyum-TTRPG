Some affinities cannot be increased until reaching high enough affinity with other affinities. 

> To find what a creature or player would be weak to you can use the opposing affinity. Determine the opposite of each component affinity, then combine those opposites into a resulting affinity; this combined affinity is the weakness. If multiple valid combinations exist, all resulting affinities are considered weaknesses. If no valid combination can be formed, each individual base opposite is treated as a weakness.
