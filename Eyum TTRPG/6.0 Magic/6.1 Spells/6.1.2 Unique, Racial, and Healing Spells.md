#### Quick Reference
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Roll Bonus|Attack Roll Bonus]] (Generic) + [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Roll Bonus|Attack Roll Bonus]] (Relevant) + Base Magic Accuracy
- **Spell Attack Damage** = Specified Damage + Base Magic Damage
	- **Spell Attack Damage Bonus:** When a spell references your [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus|Attack Damage Bonus]], look up the relevant affinity on the [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus|Attack Damage Bonus]] table.
- **Spell Save DC** = 10 + [[2.1 Affinities#Save DC Bonus|Save DC Bonus]] (Generic) + [[2.1 Affinities#Save DC Bonus|Save DC Bonus]] (Relevant)
	- **Condition Saves:** Conditions imposed from spells use your Spell Save DC instead of their written DC in [[2.4 Conditions]] unless otherwise specified. 
- **Concentration Check** = 1d20 vs DC 12 on taking damage
- **Upcasting** = Defined per spell, typically x2 mana per tier

> For damage type references see [[2.2 Damage Types]]. For condition references see [[2.4 Conditions]].

## Healing Spells
All healing spells are known at all times by any creature with any levels in The Healer. Other prerequisites must still be met. These spells cannot be learned without having at least 1 level in The Healer. Casting a healing spell on an Undead creature will instead deal damage equal to the healing amount. If you use Hallowed instead of Generic affinity the damage is tripled. When a healing spell uses your Generic [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus|Attack Damage Bonus]] or Hallowed [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus|Attack Damage Bonus]], look up the relevant affinity on the [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus|Attack Damage Bonus]] table in [[2.1 Affinities#Attack Damage Bonus]].
