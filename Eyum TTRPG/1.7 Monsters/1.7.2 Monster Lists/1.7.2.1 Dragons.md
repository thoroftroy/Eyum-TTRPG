Dragons are the apex form of elemental creatures, representing the ultimate manifestation of their respective elements. Unlike slimes and golems, dragons are living beings with flesh, blood, and scales, but their bodies are suffused with pure elemental energy. They possess tremendous size, intelligence, and power, making them the most fearsome of all elemental creatures.

At the heart of every dragon lies its *heartcore*, a blazing orb of pure elemental energy that serves as both the source of its power and its true vital organ. Unlike the cores of slimes or golems, the heartcore is warm and alive, pulsing with the dragon's life force. It grants the dragon immense power but also creates a connection to its element that defines its very existence.

Dragons are apex predators in every sense. They possess not only immense physical power but also high intelligence, capable of strategy, language, and even magic. Their bodies are perfectly adapted to their element, with scales, wings, claws, and breath all reflecting their elemental nature. A dragon's presence alone can alter the environment around it—fire dragons create heat waves, ice dragons cause frost to form on nearby surfaces, and storm dragons bring clouds and lightning.

Dragons grow by absorbing elemental energy directly from their environment. Unlike slimes and golems which grow through mass accumulation, dragons grow through a combination of age, elemental absorption, and combat experience. Each level represents a significant increase in power, with elder dragons reaching sizes that can rival small mountains.

- A Level 1 dragon is approximately:
    - **Length:** 20 ft
    - **Wingspan:** 30 ft
    - **Weight:** 2,000 lbs
- Each level:
    - +5 ft length
    - +5 ft wingspan
    - +400 lbs weight

Dragons reproduce through a unique process called *Elemental Conception*. Dragons are genderless and can reproduce with any other dragon of the same element type regardless of gender. Two dragons of the same element can merge their heartcores together to create a new dragon. This process requires both dragons to be willing and to have at least 50 mana each. The resulting egg contains the combined essence of both parents, and the new dragon emerges at level 1 after a gestation period of 1 month. The resulting dragon can be any of the possible subtypes of that element.

Dragons can also reproduce with different element types, but this creates a hybrid dragon with mixed properties from both elements. Hybrid dragons follow the same rules as regular dragons but have access to abilities from both parent elements. The resulting dragon will have a type that combines both elements (e.g., Fire + Ice = Frostfire).

When a dragon is killed, its body does not simply disappear. Instead, the elemental energy contained within its body is released in a dramatic final burst, and its physical form gradually dissolves over the course of an hour, leaving behind valuable elemental materials. The heartcore always dissolves instantly upon death. The scales, bones, and other remains can be harvested and used for powerful equipment and magical items. Typically, approximately 80% of the dragon's weight can be successfully harvested, with the remaining portion consisting of energy that dissipates upon death.

**All dragons possess the following passive abilities:**
- **Dragon Heart**: A dragon's vitality is divided into two distinct components: its Health and its Heartcore HP. The Health represents the physical condition of the dragon's body. The Heartcore HP represents the condition of the heartcore itself. Damage is applied to Health first. As long as any Health remains, the heartcore cannot be directly damaged even with AOE or Critical attacks or even attacks which would normally deal damage directly to health. When Health reaches 0, the dragon becomes severely weakened—it loses access to all actions, bonus actions, and reactions, its movement speed is reduced to 10 ft, and it gains the Prone condition. At this point, all remaining damage is applied directly to Heartcore HP. If Heartcore HP reaches 0, the heartcore is destroyed and the dragon dies permanently.
- **Elemental Aura (30 ft)**: Dragons emit an aura of their element in a 30 ft radius. Creatures within this aura at the start of their turn take damage equal to the dragon's Affinity + half their level. This damage does not trigger resistances or weaknesses. The dragon can choose to suppress this aura at any time.
- **Elemental Resistance**: Dragons have Resistance to their own element.
- **Flight**: Dragons have a flying speed equal to their listed speed. They can hover.
- **Elemental Form**: Dragons are immune to damage from their own element. Additionally, Dragons can move through their own element without taking damage or being hindered.

**Unique Skills for Specific Dragons:**
- **Elemental Breath (Active)**: (4 Mana) 30 ft cone. Targets take 3d8 + Affinity damage of the dragon's element type. Targets who fail a DEX save (DC 15 + Affinity) take half damage.
- **Elemental Wing Buffet (Active)**: (3 Mana) All creatures in a 15 ft radius must make a DEX save (DC 15 + Affinity) or take 2d8 + Affinity damage of the dragon's element type and be pushed 10 ft away. Flying creatures have disadvantage on this save.
- **Solar Annihilation (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft line. Targets take 10d10 + Affinity radiant damage. Undead and creatures vulnerable to radiant take an additional 5d10 damage.
- **Supernova (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft radius. Targets must make a CON save (DC 20 + Affinity) or take 15d10 + Affinity radiant damage and be blinded for 3 turns. Those who succeed take half damage and aren't blinded. The dragon takes 10d10 damage from the exertion.
- **Molten Core (Active)**: (6 Mana) The dragon explodes in a 30 ft radius, dealing 8d10 + Affinity fire damage to all creatures. The dragon then reforms at full health after 3 rounds but is stunned during this time.
- **Absolute Zero (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius. All creatures in range must make a CON save (DC 20 + Affinity) or take 6d10 + Affinity cold damage and gain 10 stacks of the Frozen condition. Those who succeed take half damage and 3 stacks.
- **Desert Domain (Active)**: (4 Mana) Transform the battlefield into a desert for 1 hour. While active, the dragon is hidden within the sand and can take the Hide action as a bonus action. Additionally, any creature that takes damage from the dragon loses 50% of its movement speed for 1 turn.
- **Swallowed by the Earth (Active)**: (6 Mana) 50 ft radius. The ground beneath all creatures becomes quicksand. Creatures must make a DEX save (DC 15 + Affinity) or be restrained and take 3d6 + Affinity bludgeoning damage at the start of each turn.
- **Solar Fusion (Active)**: (6 Mana) The dragon merges with the sun for 1 round. During this time, it is invulnerable and can take no actions, but all creatures in a 100 ft radius take 10d10 + Affinity radiant damage at the start of each of the dragon's turns. After 1 round, the dragon reappears.
- **Cosmic Form (Active)**: (4 Mana) The dragon transforms into pure starlight for 1 round. While in this form, it can move through any obstacle and cannot be targeted by attacks. At the end of its turn, it reforms and all creatures within 20 ft take 6d10 + Affinity radiant damage.
- **Curse of Ruin (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius. All creatures must make a WIS save (DC 18 + Affinity) or have disadvantage on all rolls and all attacks against the dragon have advantage for 1 hour. Additionally, whenever an affected creature rolls a 1, they take 5d10 + Affinity curse damage.
- **Ash Zombie (Active)**: (3 Mana) When the dragon reduces a creature to 0 HP, that creature rises as an ash zombie under the dragon's control at the start of the dragon's next turn. Ash zombies have HP equal to the creature's Level x 5 and deal 1d6 + Level fire damage on each hit.
- **Plague of Wither (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Affinity) or gain 20 stacks of the Withered condition. Those who succeed gain 10 stacks.
- **Venom Empire (Active)**: (3 Mana) Spawn 1d4 giant poisonous snakes from its body each round as a bonus action. These snakes have HP equal to the dragon's Level x 5 and can make a bite attack dealing 2d8 + Level poison damage plus the poisoned condition.
- **Biochemical Warfare (Active)**: (6 Mana) 80 ft radius cloud of toxic gas. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Affinity) or take 8d10 + Affinity toxin damage and gain the Suffocating condition for 3 turns. Those who succeed take half damage.
- **Blood Ritual (Active)**: (4 Mana) Sacrifice 50% of current health to deal that same amount as damage to all creatures in a 40 ft radius. Creatures can make a DEX save for half damage. The dragon then regains health equal to the damage dealt.
- **Seismic Smash (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft radius. All creatures must make a DEX save (DC 18 + Affinity) or take 10d10 + Affinity bludgeoning damage and be stunned for 2 turns. Those who succeed take half damage and aren't stunned.
- **Tsunami (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft line, 40 ft wide. Creatures in the path must make a STR save (DC 18 + Affinity) or take 8d10 + Affinity bludgeoning damage and be carried 60 ft away. The wave also deals 4d10 + Affinity water damage on the following turn.
- **Crystal Shards (Active)**: (4 Mana) 60 ft radius. Each creature takes 4d10 + Affinity slashing damage and must make a DEX save (DC 16 + Affinity) or take an additional 2d10 + Affinity damage from embedded shards at the start of their next turn.
- **Despair Incarnate (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius. All creatures must make a WIS save (DC 18 + Affinity) or gain the Despair condition and take 6d10 + Affinity psychic damage. Creatures that succeed are immune to this effect for 24 hours.
- **Reality Fracture (Active)**: (6 Mana) 50 ft radius. Each creature in the area must roll on the Wild Magic table. The dragon is immune to all effects and can choose the result of its own roll.
- **Hellfire Storm (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius storm of infernal fire that lasts for 3 turns. Creatures that start their turn in the storm take 5d10 + Affinity infernal damage. The dragon can move this storm up to 30 ft each turn as a bonus action.
- **Iron Dominion (Active)**: (4 Mana) Summon 1d4 iron constructs that are under its control. These constructs have HP equal to the dragon's Level x 10 and AC 20. They act on the dragon's initiative and can make slam attacks dealing 3d8 + Level damage.
- **Flood (Active)**: (4 Mana) Transform into a massive wave for up to 3 turns. While in wave form, it has no attacks but can move through creatures and objects. Any creature the wave moves through takes 6d10 + Affinity water damage and must make a STR save (DC 16 + Affinity) or be carried 30 ft.
- **Sonic Devastation (Active)**: (6 Mana) 80 ft radius. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Affinity) or take 10d10 + Affinity thunder damage and be deafened for 1 minute. Those who succeed take half damage.
- **Illusory Domination (Active)**: (4 Mana) Create 3 perfect illusory copies of itself. These copies have HP equal to the dragon's Level x 5 and can make attacks that deal half the dragon's normal damage. The dragon can see through any of its copies' senses.
- **Event Horizon (Active)**: (6 Mana) 40 ft radius region of extreme gravity. Creatures in the area must make a STR save (DC 18 + Affinity) or be pulled to the center and restrained. Any creature that starts its turn in the area takes 4d10 + Affinity void damage.
- **Spatial Tear (Active)**: (6 Mana) 50 ft radius. All creatures in the area are pulled toward the center and take 8d10 + Affinity force damage. Additionally, the dragon can teleport to any location within 100 ft as a bonus action for 3 turns.
- **Tempest King (Active)**: (6 Mana) Control all weather within 1 mile. Can cause hurricanes, tornadoes, or thunderstorms at will. Additionally, summon 1d6 lightning elementals that obey its commands.
- **Thermal Cycle (Active)**: (4 Mana) Rapidly alternate between fire and ice attacks. Each attack that deals fire damage also deals 1d6 cold damage, and vice versa. Additionally, unleash a blast dealing 10d10 + Affinity fire and 10d10 + Affinity cold damage to all creatures in a 40 ft radius.
- **Ice Age (Active)**: (6 Mana) Plunge the entire battlefield into a glacial age for 10 turns. All water on the battlefield freezes, and all creatures take 2d10 + Affinity cold damage at the start of each turn. The dragon can move through ice at full speed.
- **Abyssal Hunger (Active)**: (6 Mana) 60 ft radius. All light is consumed. Creatures must make a CON save (DC 18 + Affinity) or take 6d10 + Affinity void damage and gain the Soul Shattered condition. Additionally, consume one corpse per turn to heal 3d10 + Affinity HP.
- **Volcanic Eruption (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft radius. All creatures must make a DEX save (DC 20 + Affinity) or take 12d10 + Affinity fire damage and be buried under rubble (restrained). Those who succeed take half damage.
- **Earthbreaker (Active)**: (6 Mana) A fissure 10 ft wide and 100 ft long opens, dealing 10d10 + Affinity bludgeoning damage to all creatures in the area. Creatures that fall in take an additional 10d10 + Affinity damage and are trapped at the bottom.
- **Corruption Incarnate (Active)**: (4 Mana) Transform into a being of pure corruption for 3 turns. While transformed, all attacks deal an additional 3d10 + Affinity corruption damage, and any creature that hits the dragon with a melee attack gains the Corrupt condition.
- **Pandemic (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft radius. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 20 + Affinity) or gain 15 stacks of the Plagued condition and 15 stacks of the Poisoned condition. Those who succeed gain 5 stacks of each.
- **Dissolution (Active)**: (6 Mana) Dissolve into a pool of gel and reform elsewhere. While dissolved, can move through any opening and cannot be targeted. At the end of the turn, reform and all creatures within 15 ft take 6d10 + Affinity acid damage.
- **Nuclear Fusion (Active)**: (6 Mana) 100 ft radius. All creatures must make a CON save (DC 22 + Affinity) or take 20d10 + Affinity radiant damage and gain the Radiation condition with 20 stacks. The dragon takes 10d10 damage from the exertion.
- **Reality Collapse (Active)**: (8 Mana) 200 ft radius. All creatures must make an INT save (DC 30 + Affinity) or take 30d10 + Affinity eldritch damage and be transported to a random plane. Those who succeed take 15d10 + Affinity damage. The dragon takes 20d10 damage from the exertion.

## Dragon Descriptions

**Fire Dragon**
A massive reptilian creature wreathed in flames, with scales that glow orange and red. Smoke perpetually rises from its nostrils, and its eyes burn like coals. Flames dance along its body, and its breath is a torrent of fire that can melt stone.

**Earth Dragon**
A hulking dragon with thick, rock-like scales the color of brown and gray. Its movement is slow but powerful, and its claws can crush boulders. Its breath creates waves of earth and stone that bury anything in their path.

**Water Dragon**
A sleek, streamlined dragon with scales that shimmer like deep ocean water. It moves with surprising grace both in water and on land. Its breath is a powerful torrent of water that can knock down buildings.

**Air Dragon**
A graceful dragon with thin, almost transparent scales that catch the light. It moves with incredible speed and can hover in place. Its breath is a devastating wind that can shred anything in its path.

**Radiant Dragon**
A dazzling dragon with scales that shine like polished gold. Light emanates from its body, and its presence brings warmth and hope. Its breath is a blinding light that burns the impure.

**Necrotic Dragon**
A terrifying dragon with scales as black as night, marked with sickly green veins. Its presence causes decay and death in its vicinity. Its breath is a wave of necrotic energy that rots living things.

**Psychic Dragon**
A strange dragon with smooth, almost translucent scales that shift in color. Its eyes show infinite depth, and its presence causes headaches and unease. Its breath is a wave of psychic energy that shatters minds.

**Lightning Dragon**
A jagged dragon with scales that crackle with electricity. Static builds on its body, and its movements are punctuated by bolts of lightning. Its breath is a bolt of lightning that can strike multiple targets.

**Steam Dragon**
A dragon wreathed in thick clouds of steam, its scales nearly hidden beneath the fog. It moves silently through its own cloud. Its breath is superheated steam that burns and blinds.

**Magma Dragon**
A massive dragon with scales of black obsidian split by rivers of glowing magma. The ground melts beneath its feet. Its breath is a torrent of molten rock that destroys everything in its path.

**Ice/Cold Dragon**
A beautiful dragon with scales of crystalline ice that shimmer in the light. Frost clings to its body, and the air around it is bitterly cold. Its breath is a freezing wind that can freeze a creature solid.

**Dust Dragon**
A wispy dragon made largely of swirling dust and sand, with darker patches where its eyes should be. It moves like a desert wind. Its breath is a sandstorm that erodes everything.

**Mud Dragon**
A heavy dragon coated in thick, dripping mud that obscures most of its body. It moves slowly but inexorably. Its breath is a wave of mud that can trap and suffocate.

**Nova Dragon**
A blinding dragon whose scales radiate intense light, almost too bright to look at. It seems ready to explode at any moment. Its breath is a nova that can blind and incinerate.

**Solar Dragon**
A brilliant dragon whose scales glow with the light of the sun. It brings warmth and energy wherever it goes. Its breath is a wave of solar energy that burns and empowers.

**Starlight Dragon**
A beautiful dragon with scales that shimmer like distant stars. It seems almost to twinkle in the darkness. Its breath is a beam of starlight that pierces the darkness.

**Cursed Dragon**
A disturbing dragon covered in dark markings that seem to shift and writhe. Its presence brings misfortune. Its breath is a wave of pure curse energy.

**Ash Dragon**
A skeletal dragon made largely of ash and cinders, with glowing embers in its chest. It leaves ash trails wherever it goes. Its breath is a wave of ash that chokes and blinds.

**Blight Dragon**
A twisted dragon covered in rotting growths and diseased flesh. Its presence causes plants to wither. Its breath is a wave of blighted energy that rots living things.

**Poison Dragon**
A dragon with scales that drip with venom, creating pools of poison beneath it. Its breath is a cloud of poisonous gas.

**Toxin Dragon**
A dragon covered in a thin mist of toxic particles that float around it. Its breath is a wave of toxic energy that poisons everything it touches.

**Bloodfire Dragon**
A terrifying dragon with scales that appear to be made of dried blood, with flames flickering beneath. Its breath is a wave of bloodfire that burns and drains.

**Tremor Dragon**
A massive dragon that shakes the ground with every step. Its scales are the color of earth and stone. Its breath causes earthquakes.

**Deluge Dragon**
A dragon made largely of water, constantly flowing and rippling. Its breath is a flood that can drown everything.

**Shatter Dragon**
A dragon with crystalline scales that seem ready to break. Its movements create sharp sounds. Its breath causes everything to shatter.

**Sorrow Dragon**
A melancholy dragon that seems to radiate despair. Its scales are a dull gray. Its breath is a wave of sorrow that drains hope.

**Chaos Dragon**
A constantly shifting dragon whose scales change color and pattern. Its breath is a wave of pure chaos.

**Infernal Dragon**
A demon-like dragon wreathed in hellfire with scales of black and red. Its breath is a wave of infernal fire.

**Metal Dragon**
A dragon made entirely of metal, with scales that clang against each other. Its breath is a wave of metal shards.

**Torrent Dragon**
A dragon that seems to be made of rushing water, moving with incredible speed. Its breath is a focused jet of water.

**Thunder Dragon**
A dragon that crackles with thunder, with scales that vibrate with sound. Its breath is a thunderclap.

**Mirage Dragon**
A dragon that seems to shift and change, never quite in one place. Its breath creates illusions.

**Vacuum Dragon**
A strange dragon that seems to fold in on itself, with scales that look like holes in space. Its breath is a wave of void energy that tears things apart.

**Warp Dragon**
A dragon that seems to bend space around it. Its breath is a wave of warp energy that scrambles things.

**Storm Dragon**
A massive dragon wreathed in a storm, with lightning flashing in its clouds. Its breath is a combination of wind, lightning, and rain.

**Frostfire Dragon**
A dragon with scales of both fire and ice, flickering between the two. Its breath is a combination of both elements.

**Glacial Dragon**
A massive dragon made of ancient ice, frozen solid. Its breath is a wave of glacial cold.

**Void Dragon**
A dragon that seems to be a hole in reality, with edges that blur into nothing. Its breath is pure void.

**Obsidian Dragon**
A dragon made of black volcanic glass, with razor-sharp edges. Its breath is a wave of obsidian shards.

**Quake Dragon**
A massive dragon that causes earthquakes with every step. Its breath is a massive tremor.

**Corruption Dragon**
A twisted dragon that radiates corruption, causing things to rot and decay. Its breath is a wave of corruption.

**Miasma Dragon**
A dragon surrounded by a cloud of toxic mist. Its breath is a wave of poisonous gas.

**Gel Dragon**
A dragon made of strange gel-like substance. Its breath causes things to dissolve.

**Atomic Dragon**
A glowing dragon that radiates intense radiation. Its breath is a wave of atomic energy.

**Eldritch Dragon**
A horrifying dragon from beyond reality, with impossible geometry. Its breath is a wave of eldritch energy.
