- Bugfolk (13 subraces)
	- All Bugfolk speak Common + Bugish. 
	- **Miregnat**: The fly. Small, agile, with full hover flight. Gets immunity to most physical conditions like poison and desiease, vision-based skills (compound eyes, wide angle, see all directions), and toxin abilities. **COME BACK TO THIS AFTER SIGHT RULES ARE UPDATED, this has happened so its time to fix it**

	- **Formid**: The titan beetle. Super strong tank that charges forward. Slow and powerful with crushing blows and size-manipulation (counts as larger for grapple/shove). Main gimick is just being an unstopable immovable object, goes through all terrain easily, all creatures easily.

- Demon (7 subraces)
	- All Demons speak Common + Infernal. 
	- **Demon**: The pactbound fiend. Special abilities with pacts and Infernal magic. Must keep their deals but grows stronger from each pact fulfilled. 
	- **Ferramite**: The iron hellknight. A heavily-armored honorable knight fused with Infernal magic. Spellblade archetype that mixes weapon attacks with Infernal casting. 
	- **Pyrrakin**: The pyromaniac skirmisher. Fast, agile demon focused entirely on fire, magma, infernal, and heat. Gets wall-running, searing strikes, speed boosts that leave fire trails, and an evasive skitter. 
	- **Drakari**: The dragonborn. Tanky with fire breath (which can change damage type if you have high enough affinity in another element), wings (fly at higher tiers), dragon bite, and scale armor. Should get access to some of the passives real dragons have (frightful presence, elemental auras). 
	- **Cinderkin**: Ash, fire, and Infernal magic with social/deception skills. Gets flight at higher levels (but not built in, earned through tiers). Should have some survivability skills similar to base Human (grit, second wind). 
	- **Sylphamare**: The ancient judge. Slow, huge, powerful, a terrifying monster that nullifies magic, condemns enemies, and weighs down entire battlefields with despair. Current design is good: long reach, auto-succeed saves, Condemn spell, Slow Death stacks, Silenced zones.
	- **Varkuun**: The hellforged warrior. A blend of Pyrrakin and Kravari, lots of fire skills combined with genuine weapon and armor mastery. Gets demonic weapon edges, psychic needle on hit, predator instincts, and the ability to command allies. The martial-focused demon. **Similar to Ferramite, ferramite should be more dragon and defense where Varkuun is more Kravari with Fire magic.**

	- **Dryad**: The living forest. Unique nature spells.
	- **Nymph**: The water spirit. Always has the Soaked condition (intrinsic). Unique water spells and easier paths to different water based affinities. 
	- **Siren**: A race entirly based on hypnotism and charisma, highest raw stat bonus of any race (all just charisma). 
	- **Dwarf**: Skills are not combat focused, get better alchemy, forging, bartering, etc. Lots of skill points and affinity and stats. 
	- **Snerin**: A dwarf but can turn invisable, is smaller, and gets less bonuses
	- **Ironwroth**: A Dwarf with actual combat skills
	- **Fairy**: Unique sprite magic similar to elves, elves keep sprites as pets where fairys use sprites as companions which grow with them. 
	- **Pixy**: Basically a fairy but with dark magic flavoring.
