A list of terms in the handbook which you may not know.

| Word                             | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | Example Sentence                                                                          
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Action Point (Ap)**            | A resource used to perform primary actions on your turn, such as attacking, casting a spell, or dashing. Characters start with 1 Ap at level 1 and gain more at levels 10, 20, 30, etc.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "I have 2 Ap this turn, so I can Attack twice."                                           
| **Active Ability**               | A feat or ability that requires an action (Ap, BAp, or Rp) to use.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | "Second Wind is an active ability that costs a BAp."                                      
| **Advantage**                    | Roll 2d20 and take the higher result. Only affects d20 rolls (attack rolls, skill checks, saving throws). Multiple sources stack up to triple advantage (3 dice).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "I have advantage from high ground, so I roll two dice and take the higher."              
| **Affinity Point (Affp)**        | Points used to increase specific elemental affinities (Fire, Earth, etc.). You start with 5 at level 1. Cannot be used on Generic Affinity.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | "I put 2 Affinity Points into Fire to increase my fire spell damage."                     
| **Affinity Prerequisites**       | Advanced affinities require minimum total points in parent affinities before they can be unlocked. Examples: Lightning requires 10 Fire + 10 Air.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "I need 10 Fire and 10 Air before I can put points into Lightning."                       
| **Alignment**                    | A label derived from your Karma score: Good (250-500), Neutral (-249 to 249), Evil (-500 to -250). Affects which pacts and abilities are available.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "My alignment shifted to Good after all those heroic deeds."                              
| **Alignment Requirement**        | Some deities require a specific Karma-based alignment to form pacts: Good (250-500 Karma), Neutral (-249 to 249), Evil (-500 to -250).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "Mercy requires an Evil Alignment for pacts."                                             
| **Ancient**                      | The eldest celestial beings, neutral or alien in nature. Their power does not depend entirely on mortal belief; their age and mass grant immense strength.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | "Darwin and Newton are Ancients."                                                         
| **Ancient Pact Stone**           | A pact stone recovered from ancient altars, ruins, or burial grounds. Almost always unidentified when found. Used to form pacts with Ancients.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "We found an Ancient Pact Stone in the ruins."                                            
| **Area of Effect (AoE)**         | Spells or effects that hit an area rather than a single target. Half of AoE damage bypasses Vitality and hits HP directly (unless otherwise stated).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "The fireball is an AoE, so I take half damage to HP even on a save."                     
| **Armor Class (AC)**             | A number representing how hard you are to hit. Base calculation: 10 + Armor + Dex Bonus + Shield + Other Bonuses. An attack roll must meet or exceed AC to hit.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | "My AC is 18, so the enemy needs to roll an 18 or higher to hit me."                      
| **Armor Expertise**              | Increases AC bonus: Light +1, Medium +2, Heavy +4. Requires proficiency in that armor type first.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "With Heavy Armor Expertise, my plate gives +4 more AC."                                  
| **Armor Proficiency**            | Divided into Light, Medium, and Heavy. Must be gained in order (Light → Medium → Heavy). If proficient, ignore all penalties. If not, all penalties apply as written.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "I'm proficient in Medium armor, so I ignore the -1 AC penalty."                          
| **Attack Roll**                  | A d20 roll to determine if an attack hits. Melee uses Strength (or Dex for finesse). Ranged uses Dexterity. Magical uses affinity modifiers. Total must meet or exceed target's AC.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I roll a 12, add +5 for a total of 17. That hits the fire slime's AC of 14."             
| **Background**                   | Your character's life before the campaign. Grants advantage on tasks your character would logically excel at due to past experience. No direct stat bonuses unless GM agrees.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "My Soldier background gives me advantage on battle tactics checks."                      
| **Base 7 Affinities**            | The seven starting affinities that can be assigned at character creation: Fire, Earth, Water, Air, Radiant, Necrotic, Psychic.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "At level 1, I can only put points into the base 7 affinities."                           
| **Base Accuracy**                | A class-based accuracy bonus for melee, ranged, or magic attacks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "My Base Melee Accuracy is +1 from the Duelist path."                                     
| **Base Damage**                  | A class-based damage bonus divided into three categories: Melee, Ranged, and Magic. Increases primarily through class levels.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "At level 3 my Base Melee Damage is +2."                                                  
| **Beacon**                       | A Pact Path sub-path for progressing in Godly pacts. Higher-tier godly pacts require progress in Beacon.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | "I need Beacon levels to access Tier 3 godly pacts."                                      
| **Bloodline Evolution**          | A skill tree path for unlocking higher-tier racial abilities. Each level grants access to the next racial ability tier (up to Tier 10). Requires Racial Path first.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I take a level in Bloodline Evolution to unlock my Tier 3 racial ability."               
| **Bonus Action Point (BAp)**     | A resource used to perform secondary actions on your turn, such as activating a class ability or casting a bonus-action spell. Characters start with 1 BAp at level 1 and gain more every 8 levels.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I use my BAp to Rage."                                                                   
| **Campaign**                     | A series of connected gaming sessions that tell a long, evolving story.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "Our campaign has been running for six months."                                           
| **Celestial Body**               | The planetary or stellar form of a deity. The deity *is* the celestial body, not merely a symbol of it. Celestial bodies cannot be destroyed by any known means.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | "Ignivar's celestial body is Mercury."                                                    
| **Charisma**                     | Core stat governing social presence. Modifier used for Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion, Social Insight, Barter, and Charisma saving throws (charm effects).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | "Charisma 18 gives me +4 on persuasion checks."                                           
| **Chimera**                      | A hybrid animal hatched from an egg, its body pressed from two or more different creatures into a stable form. Includes griffins, hydras, sphinxes, and many others. Levels determined by age (1 level per year).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "The griffin is a type of chimera."                                                       
| **Combat Role**                  | A monster's function in an encounter. **Berserker**: constant attacks, never retreats. **Tanker**: absorbs hits. **Guerrilla**: hit-and-run. **Ranger**: attacks from range. **Stealth**: sneak attack then fade. **Mixed**: melee and ranged. **Support**: buffs allies or summons. **Swarmer**: overwhelms with numbers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | "This golem is a Tanker. It will try to absorb our attacks."                              
| **Combination Damage Type**      | An advanced damage type formed by merging two or more elemental/magical sources (e.g., Lightning = Fire + Air). Resistant only if resistant to ALL components or the combination specifically.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "Lightning is a combination of Fire and Air affinities."                                  
| **Condition**                    | A temporary state applied to a creature, organized into four categories. **Environmental**: Prone, Grappled, Stunned, Unconscious, Surprised, Threatened, Invisible, Intangible, Grounded, On Fire, Frozen, Soaked, Storm Shocked, Suffocating, Hurting, Entangled, Lethargic. **Physical**: Bleeding, Pierced, Burned, Frostbitten, Shocked, Poisoned, Diseased, Hellfire, Corrupt, Necrosis, Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Petrified, Gelled, Radiation, Sickened, Withered, Plagued. **Mental**: Charmed, Frightened, Enraged, Demoralized, Despair, Hypnotized, Mute, Blurred, Addicted. **Magical**: Cursed, Hexed, Silenced, Slow Death, Slowed, Hasted, Blessed, Hexproof, Eldritch Curse. Each condition lists its effects and removal method. | "I am Poisoned: I take damage and have disadvantage on skill checks."                     
| **Companion**                    | A non-player ally (familiar, summon, or tamed creature) that acts on your turn. Default companion stats: 1 Ap, 1 BAp, 0 Rp.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | "My companion has 1 Ap and 1 BAp on its turn."                                            
| **Concentration**                | Maintaining focus on a sustained spell. You can only concentrate on one spell at a time. Taking damage requires a Concentration Check (1d20 vs DC 12) or the spell ends.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | "I cast Flame Wall and must maintain Concentration or it fizzles."                        
| **Conditional Ability**          | A feat that is only active under specific conditions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "Heavy Hitter only works while using a two-handed weapon."                                
| **Constitution**                 | Core stat governing toughness and endurance. Modifier used for Vitality rolls, concentration checks, and Constitution saving throws (poison, disease, etc.).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | "My Constitution 12 gives me +1 Vitality per level."                                      
| **Contested Roll**               | When two creatures roll against each other (e.g., grappling). The higher total wins. If tied, the creature with the higher relevant attribute modifier wins. If still tied, reroll.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I roll Athletics to grapple, and the earth golem rolls Athletics against me."            
| **Critical Fail**                | Rolling a Natural 1. You fail in the worst way possible; major negative consequences.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "A critical fail means I accidentally hit my ally."                                       
| **Critical Hit**                 | A Natural 20 on an attack roll or exceeding AC by 10+. Deals maximum possible damage (no damage roll) and bypasses Vitality, going straight to HP. Increases Injury Severity by 1 level.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | "I crit with my longsword. I deal maximum damage straight to HP!"                         
| **Critical Success**             | Rolling a Natural 20 or exceeding the DC by 10+. Flawless execution. In combat, you always hit and deal maximum damage, bypassing Vitality.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | "With a critical success, I disarm the trap perfectly."                                   
| **d20**                          | A 20-sided die, the primary die used for determining success or failure in most actions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | "Roll a d20 to see if you hit the fire slime."                                            
| **Damage Bypass**                | Damage that goes directly to HP, ignoring Vitality. Caused by: critical hits, AoE (half damage bypasses), attacks on sleeping/unconscious creatures, undodgeable attacks, and Psychic damage.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "The crit bypasses my Vitality, dealing 12 damage straight to HP."                        
| **Damage Type Groupings**        | Categories of damage types: Physical (non-magical sources), Magical (spells/enchanted), Elemental (fire, water, etc.), Mixed (physical + magic), Special (psychic, force, true). Creatures may have resistances to entire groupings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "The golem has resistance to Physical damage, so my sword does less."                     
| **Darkvision**                   | See in total darkness as if it were dim light, but only in shades of gray. Default range 60 ft. Does not pierce magical darkness.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "My elf has Darkvision 60 ft, so I can see in the dark cave."                             
| **Dash**                         | An action that lets you move an additional distance equal to your Base Movement Speed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "I Dash to close the 60 ft gap."                                                          
| **Deity**                        | Vast celestial beings that appear as planets, stars, or moons. Three types: Gods, Fiends, and Ancients. They grow stronger through mortal belief, worship, or death.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "Ignivar is a God of fire and heat."                                                      
| **Desaturation**                 | The process of clearing unstable mana from a failed inscription attempt. Costs (Mana Spent ÷ 3) × (Runes Inscribed + 5) mana.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "I failed the inscription and must pay the desaturation cost to try again."               
| **Dexterity**                    | Core stat governing agility and precision. Modifier used for ranged attacks, finesse weapons, initiative, AC bonus, and Dexterity saving throws.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | "Dexterity 14 gives me +2 to AC and initiative."                                          
| **Difficult Terrain**            | Ground that costs double movement to traverse: mud, rubble, deep snow, etc. Does not stack with other difficult terrain.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | "Moving through the rubble costs double movement."                                        
| **Difficulty Class (DC)**        | A target number set by the GM representing how hard a task is. Your roll plus bonuses must meet or exceed the DC to succeed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | "The GM says the DC to climb the wall is 15."                                             
| **Difficulty Rating (DR)**       | An estimate of how many level 5 players are required to defeat a single monster instance. DR 1 = one level 5 player can win. DR 1/5 = closer to a level 1 player. DR 0 = trivial.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "The slime has DR 1/5. It is easy for a level 1 party."                                   
| **Dirty 20**                     | Refers to when you reach 20 from your roll + modifiers instead of it being a crit.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | "Aw man dirty 20, its good but I wish it was a crit."                                     
| **Disadvantage**                 | Roll 2d20 and take the lower result. Only affects d20 rolls. Multiple sources stack up to triple disadvantage (3 dice).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "Firing into darkness gives me disadvantage on the attack roll."                          
| **Disengage**                    | An action that prevents you from provoking opportunity attacks for the rest of your turn. Costs 1 Ap.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "I Disengage to safely retreat from the earth golem."                                     
| **Dodge**                        | An action granting disadvantage on attacks against you and advantage on Dexterity saving throws until your next turn.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "I Dodge, making it harder for the archers to hit me."                                    
| **Dragon**                       | A large, winged reptilian creature made of biological tissue suffused with elemental energy. Fully organic with skeletal, muscular, circulatory, and nervous systems infused with their element.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | "The fire dragon descends from the mountains."                                            
| **Elemental Damage**             | Damage from elemental sources: Fire, Earth, Water, Air, and their combinations (Lightning, Steam, Magma, Ice/Cold, Dust, Mud, etc.).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "Firebolt deals elemental fire damage."                                                   
| **Enchantability**               | A material property that subtracts from the DC mod when inscribing runes. Higher is better. Gold has 12, making rune inscription much easier.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "Using a gold item makes rune inscription DC much lower due to its Enchantability of 12." 
| **Exhaustion**                   | A cumulative penalty from overexertion, lack of rest, or magical effects. 6 levels ranging from disadvantage on ability checks (level 1) to death (level 6). Removed 1 level per long rest.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | "I have level 2 exhaustion. My speed is halved."                                          
| **Expertise**                    | True mastery in a skill, weapon, or armor. For skills: grants Advantage on checks. For specific weapons: grants Advantage on attack rolls. For weapon types: grants +2 to attack rolls. For armor: increases AC cap.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "I have Expertise with the Longsword, so I roll with Advantage on attack rolls."          
| **Fall Damage**                  | 1d4 bludgeoning per 5 ft fallen. Prone if falling more than 10 ft. Past 25 ft, gain Hurting stacks. Past 50 ft, damage increases to 1d8 per 5 ft.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "I fall 30 ft and take 6d4 bludgeoning damage."                                           
| **Familiar**                     | A tamed and bonded monster companion that fights alongside you and grows in power. Uses its own statblock. Shares your initiative. Requires Monster Specialist path to unlock.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "My slime familiar follows my commands in combat."                                        
| **Feat**                         | A special ability gained when leveling up (every 3 levels). Types: Passive (always active), Conditional (active under conditions), Active (requires action to use).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I take the Toughness feat for +1 HP and Vit per level."                                  
| **Fiend**                        | A type of deity generally viewed as evil. Fiends feed on death, despair, corruption, and the collapse of life.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "Black Mercy is a Fiend of necrotic death."                                               
| **Fiendish Pact Stone**          | A pact stone acquired through cults or black-market channels, often requiring joining those groups. Used to form pacts with Fiends.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | "I bought a Fiendish Pact Stone from the dealer."                                         
| **Flanking**                     | Attacking from behind while an ally engages from the front gives advantage on your attack roll. The target also has disadvantage on saving throws.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | "I flank the magma golem while my ally distracts it from the front."                      
| **Force Damage**                 | A Special damage type with no special properties.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | "Magic Missile deals force damage."                                                       
| **Free Action**                  | Minor actions requiring no action points, such as speaking or dropping an item.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | "As a free action, I shout a warning to my allies."                                       
| **Game Master (GM)**             | The person who describes the world, portrays NPCs, and acts as rules referee. Also called the Narrator.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "The GM describes the ancient temple we enter."                                           
| **Generic Affinity**             | Your baseline magical talent. Applies to all spells equally. Adds to all spell attack rolls and spell save DCs. Cannot be increased with Affinity Points; gained from class features, race bonuses, and feats.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "My Generic Affinity is +2, so all my spell attacks get that bonus."                      
| **Generic Damage Mod (Monster)** | Added to all damage a monster deals. Typically the creature's Affinity damage type. Scales with monster level.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "The fire monster's Generic Damage Mod adds +7 fire damage to all attacks."               
| **Ghost (Monster)**              | An incorporeal remnant of a creature that died with unfinished business. Gains Creature of Darkness, Ghost Form, Ghostly Touch, Incorporeal, Terrifying Presence, Spiritual Echo, and various actives. Considered Undead.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              | "The ghost modifier makes the monster incorporeal and terrifying."                        
| **God**                          | A type of deity generally viewed as good. Gods pursue devotion and grow stronger through worship and sacred covenant.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | "Ignivar and Aegirion are Gods."                                                          
| **Godly Pact Stone**             | A pact stone found in temples devoted to a specific god. Used to form pacts with Gods.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "The temple offers Godly Pact Stones to the faithful."                                    
| **Godslayer**                    | A skill tree path that allows characters to damage deities. Each level reduces the deity's damage resistance against the Godslayer, from Greater Resistance (L1) to taking double damage (L10).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | "I need Godslayer level 7 to deal full damage to the deity."                              
| **Going Past Limits**            | Rules for converting excess gains: excess spell learning → +1 Affinity Point. Duplicate proficiency → 3 Skill Points. Duplicate expertise → 5 Skill Points. Full skills → convert to Stat Points. Full feats → 2 Stat Points.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "I already know all the spells I want, so I convert this into an Affinity Point."         
| **Golem**                        | An elemental construct of dense, solidified material bound around a single animating core. Rigid, durable, humanoid form. Designed to protect the core at all costs. Role: Tanker.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | "The earth golem stands guard before the tomb."                                           
| **Grapple**                      | An action to grab a creature. Roll Athletics vs target's Athletics or Acrobatics. On success, the target is Grappled (Speed 0, attacks against them have advantage).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "I grapple the fire slime to hold it in place."                                           
| **Great Success**                | When your roll meets or exceeds DC +5. You succeed exceptionally well and may gain extra benefits.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | "I beat the DC by 6, so the GM says I find extra clues."                                  
| **Greater Resistance**           | Take 1/3 damage from a source.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | "The elemental has Greater Resistance to fire, taking only 1/3 damage."                   
| **Greater Weakness**             | Take triple (3x) damage from a source.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "The plant has Greater Weakness to blight, taking 3x damage."                             
| **Half Cover**                   | +2 to AC and advantage on Dexterity saves.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | "I hide behind the low wall for half cover."                                              
| **Health (HP)**                  | Your actual life force. When HP hits 0, you die. HP damage represents real injury. Healed by potions, magic, or long rest.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | "I'm down to 3 HP and bleeding - I need healing now."                                     
| **Help**                         | An action granting an ally within 5 ft advantage on their next ability check or attack.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "I use Help to distract the patrol so my ally can sneak past."                            
| **Hit Dice**                     | Dice rolled to determine starting and per-level Vitality, Health, and Mana. Your class determines your Hit Dice size (e.g., d6, d8, d10). Rolled at each level-up to increase your maximums. Monsters also use Hit Dice to scale stats.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "As a Duelist, I roll a d10 Hit Die for Vitality."                                        
| **Hide**                         | An action using Stealth to become hidden. Must be in light cover or darkness.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "I Hide behind the crates."                                                               
| **High Ground**                  | Being at least 1 ft above a target gives advantage on attack rolls against them. They have disadvantage attacking you.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "The archer on the balcony has high ground against me."                                   
| **Hold Breath**                  | Characters without water-breathing can hold breath for 1 + Constitution modifier rounds (minimum 1). After that, suffocation begins.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | "I can hold my breath for 3 rounds before I start suffocating."                           
| **Immunity**                     | Take no damage from a source.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | "Ghosts are immune to Physical damage."                                                   
| **Initiative**                   | The order in which combatants act each round. Calculated as 1d20 + Dexterity modifier + other bonuses.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "I roll a 14 for initiative, so I go third."                                              
| **Injury**                       | A negative condition applied when you fail a Constitution saving throw after taking HP damage. Severity depends on percentage of max HP lost from a single source: Minor (10-24%), Moderate (25-49%), Severe (50-74%), Extreme (75%+).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | "The magma golem's hit took 60% of my HP, so it's a Severe injury."                       
| **Injury Severity**              | Determined by the percentage of max HP lost from a single source: Minor (10-24%, DC 10), Moderate (25-49%, DC 15), Severe (50%+, DC 20), Extreme (75%+, DC 25). Critical hits increase severity by 1 level.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | "A crit that deals 15% of my HP counts as Moderate instead of Minor."                     
| **Inscription Check**            | A d20 roll + Relevant Affinity Modifier vs DC 12 + the rune's DC Mod. Required for each rune in a circle. Failure ruins the entire circle. Natural 20 makes the rune free and gives Advantage on the next roll.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | "I roll 18 on my inscription check. The Fire Rune is etched successfully."                
| **Inspiration**                  | A reward the GM grants for exceptional roleplay or creativity. Can be spent for advantage on any d20 roll. Multiple points can be held. Must be declared before the result is revealed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | "The GM gives me Inspiration for that emotional speech."
