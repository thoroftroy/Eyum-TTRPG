Rarity Reference

RarityDescription
CommonLow power; simple or utility effects
UncommonEnhanced utility or small combat edges
RareStrong power; often requires attunement
Very RareMajor power; risks/limits common
LegendaryUnique artifacts tied to myth/gods
MythicNear-divine, reality-bending, often sentient

Attuning. Attune (1 min; 5 Vitality to un-attune). Max 3 attuned items per character unless an item (or your class) says otherwise.
